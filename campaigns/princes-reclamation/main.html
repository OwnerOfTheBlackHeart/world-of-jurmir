This is the Story of a Prince's Reclamation

XXX is the son of Pedaeus of Heminus, the hedonistic, Sithraki king of the Kingdom of Indos. Due to Pedaeus's irresponsibility and refusal to establish order, the Kingdom of Indos is in a state of decay, with the majority of the power being held not by King Pedaeus, but by the 8 dukes of Indos. XXX seeks to ensure that when he ascends to the throne, he will have a stable and obedient kingdom.
