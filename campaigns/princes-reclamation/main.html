This is the Story of a Prince's Reclamation

Kafka is the son of Pedaeus of Heminus, the hedonistic, Sithraki king of the Kingdom of Indos. Due to Pedaeus's irresponsibility and refusal to establish order, the Kingdom of Indos is in a state of decay, with the majority of the power being held not by King Pedaeus, but by the 8 dukes of Indos. Kafka seeks to ensure that when he ascends to the throne, he will have a stable and obedient kingdom.

If he chooses 2 companions, he starts at citizen (rep rank 1) in with both associated factions. Cody automatically starts with at citizen (rep rank 1) for Du Indos. Taking just Zardril will make him start at friend (rep rank 3) while taking Zardril and one other companion will make him start at supporter (rep rank 2) for Du Indos.
