Gefro Kuste, The Duchy of Two Dragons

Ruled by a mating pair of different colored dragons, the silver XXX and the bronze YYY, Gefro Kuste is widely considered to be the safest and most comfortable duchy in Indos. It is also notable for the meritocratic society XXX and YYY work hard maintain.

The people of Gefro Kuste are normally well fed and cared for, with the lords and ladies viewing their titles as jobs rather than being of higher status. Wonder lust is a common trait among the residents of Gefro Kuste.

Relations

Gefro Kuste likes Akrida because they bring trade, strength, security and a strong moral compass to the Kingdom of Indos.

Gefro Kuste likes Idanthus because they are friendly and kindhearted, with a certain spirit that makes the people of Gefro Kuste smile.

Gefro Kuste hates Ruvanna because they slander Gefro Kuste any chance they can get, which has devolved into violence more than once. The elves also have unrealistic and unreasonable expectations of Gefro Kuste, which they constantly try to force on the people of Gefro Kuste.

Gefro Kuste hates Eisiger Fel because they allow horrible things, such as slave trading, to happen within their borders and they harbor evil dragons.
