Ruvanna, The Dream Forest

Made up of elven grove that banded together for protection against the green dragon Umur, The People Collector, who rules over Grunbaum. Ruvanna is well known for the many hallucinogenic plants that hide among the underbrush.

The elves of Ruvanna are a fearful and protective lot. They particularly fear the other races and are quick to flee if they sense danger.

The Ruvannian elves are the last hold out of the native elves of the island, the unique dangers of their forest offering the protection that has allowed them to survive as they have. While the wild and wood elves generally don't like the fact that they have been absorbed into "civilized" society, the high elves actively work to use the system to their protection.

The elves of Ruvanna are evenly split between high elf, wild elf and wood elf.

Ruvanna is officially ruled over by Duchess Therlas Ilaro.

Relations

Ruvanna doesn't like any other factions because they view them all as the people who pushed them out of their traditional homes.

Ruvanna hates Grunbaum because they consistently send raiding parties to steal, kill and capture from Ruvanna.

Ruvanna hates Gefro Kuste because they blame them for not getting rid of the local evil dragons and consider their inaction as equal to the actions of the evil dragons.
