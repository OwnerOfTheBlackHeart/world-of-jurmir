The Duchy of Sirine

Compared to the other duchies, Sirine's people are much more isolationistic. While they aren't necessarily friendly to outsiders, they aren't friendly either. They have a notably higher population of tieflings and aasimars compared to the other duchies and have a reputation as being a safe haven for half-orcs and half-elves.

The people of Sirine secretly worship the Red King. They believe that the current state of the world indicates that the end isn't yet and are content to live their lives and wait for the call of the next age.

Relations

Sirine likes Eisiger Fel because they form a barrier against the rest of the kingdom, enjoy relaxed trading with Sirine, and are similarly suspicious of outsiders. The people of Sirine are also more accepting of Eisiger Fel because they are mainly human, goliath and giant.

Sirine hates Akrida because the people of Akrida are too nosey and are constantly trying to interfere in their affairs or convert them to Lyrnanism.

Sirine hates Idanthus because they frequently send traders and missionaries, both of which have like to stay longer than they are welcome. The people of Sirine also don't like Idanthus because Idanthus is made up of dwarves that refused to accept, what the people of Sirine see as, their rightful end.
