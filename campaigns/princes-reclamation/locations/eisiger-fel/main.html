Eisiger Fel, the Frozen Peaks

Ruled by the white dragon Duchess Ammetu, The Mountain Singer, Eisiger Fel is primarily populated by goliaths and giants. Eisiger Fel is considered the most well defended of Indos's duchies.

The people of Eisiger Fel are always welcoming to merchants, but are suspicious of outsiders that travel without goods.

Relations

Eisiger Fel likes Sirine because they understand boundaries and endeavour to follow appropriate social practices. They also have a long history of being good neighbors, at least by the standards of Eisiger Fel.

Eisiger Fel likes Idanthus because they trade high quality goods at reasonable exchanges and are always willing to share with an ailing tribe.

Eisiger Fel likes Grunbaum because they obsessively learn and follow social practices, and they trade in goods of both quality and rarity.

Eisiger Fel hates Akrida because they send so many non-merchants that the people of Eisiger Fel worry that Akrida is planning to attack them.

Eisiger Fel hates Gefro Kuste because the dragons there represent a threat to the Eisiger Fel way of life, weather through trying to spread their way of life or by trying to kill XXX.
