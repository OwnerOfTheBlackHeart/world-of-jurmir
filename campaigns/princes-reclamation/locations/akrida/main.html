Akrida, The Lyrnan Duchy

While all of Indos is technically a kingdom under the Holy Lyrnan Empire, Akrida is the most distinctly Lyrnan duchy, housing many lyrnan's from other colonies and/or the mainland.

Relations

Akrida likes Gefro Kuste because they are a good, strong example of what a successful merger between cultures looks like, and because Gefro Kuste treats its citizens well. Akrida also appreciates how good Gefro Kuste is at stomping out corruption.

Akrida likes Idanthus because they are friendly, good hearted dwarves with a desire to see the world become a better place.

Akrida hates Sirine because they are unfriendly to missionaries and Akrida is certain that Sirine.

Akrida hates Eisiger Fel because they are unfriendly to missionaries and regard honest attempts to improve their lives with excessive suspicion.
