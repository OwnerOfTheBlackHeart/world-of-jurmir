Idanthus, The Artificial Mountain

The Duchy of Idanthus is named after the artificial mountain Idanthus, which sits at the center of the Duchy. Idanthus is know as the place to go for new beginnings.

The dwarves of Idanthus are a generally cheerful lot with a hopeful outlook for the future. They openly welcome outsiders and are happy to offer support in times of need or sadness.

Clan Chiseltide, the largest clan within Idanthus, made the mountain after their prior mountain was destroyed by a volcanic eruption.

While Idanthus presents itself as a single, coordinated entity, it is really a collection of city states that have sworn fealty to the High Clanmaster, XXX.
