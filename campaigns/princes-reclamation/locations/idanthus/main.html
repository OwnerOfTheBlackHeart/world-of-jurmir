Idanthus, The Artificial Mountain

The Duchy of Idanthus is named after the artificial mountain Idanthus, which sits at the center of the Duchy. Idanthus is know as the place to go for new beginnings.

The dwarves of Idanthus are a generally cheerful lot with a hopeful outlook for the future. They openly welcome outsiders and are happy to offer support in times of need or sadness.

Clan Chiseltide, the largest clan within Idanthus, made the mountain after their prior mountain was destroyed by a volcanic eruption.

While Idanthus presents itself as a single, coordinated entity, it is really a collection of city states that have sworn fealty to the High Clanmaster, and thus duke, Cretheus of Aburii.

Relations

Idanthus likes Eisiger Fel because they are always welcoming of merchants, even when they aren't looking to trade.

Idanthus likes Akrida because they are good people, and without the Holy Lyrnan Empire, Idanthus would have been impossible.

Idanthus likes Gefro Kuste because they are good neighbors that truly embody what it means to be a good and just society.

Idanthus hates Sirine because they are unwelcoming, depressing, and feel like they are hiding something.

Idanthus hates Grunbaum because they are openly evil and routinely attack the natives, who are trying to survive like the rest of us.
