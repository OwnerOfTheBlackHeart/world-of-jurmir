Grunbaum, The Great Green Forest

Ruled over by the green dragon Duke Umur, The People Collector, Grunbaum is a massive forest with villages built into, and among, the trees.

The people of Grunbaum are friendly and loyal, but they also tend to be hardened by their relatively wild lifestyle. It is no secret that the government of Grunbaum is corrupt, and the vast majority of its workers will take "donations," but not a one would go against orders from Duke Umur, direct or indirect.

Grunbaum is known for its slave trade.

Relations

Grunbaum likes Eisiger Fel because they have a defense agreement with Eisiger Fel and can easily trade slaves within their borders.

Grunbaum hates Idanthus because the artificial mountain destroyed the forests where it originally was and has, to an extent, disrupted the weather on the island. Grunbaum also doesn't like that they can't wipe those smug smiles off of the faces in Idanthus without provoking multiple attacks in return.

Grunbaum hates Ruvanna because they are food and slaves, and the consideration of elves as people is insulting.
