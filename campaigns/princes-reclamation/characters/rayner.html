Rayner of Clan Ozeanvulkan, Noble Sorcerer

Picture of Rayner

Rayner is a human sorcerer from Gefro Kuste. He is proud of his noble heritage and of the fact that his family has managed to maintain their status in the meritocracy of Gefro Kuste. He claims to draw power from dragonblood, and his love of collecting rare coins and fine paintings only seem to support his claim.
