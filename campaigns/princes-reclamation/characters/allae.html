Allae, Elven Duskblade

Picture of Allae

Allae is a fearful elf from the Ruvanna Duchy. She travels now because she was caught in a raid from Grunbaum and got lost, leading her to have more contact with the rest of Indos than other elves.

While Allae is perfectly capable of navigating social situations, she is constantly afraid of one thing or another. She learned magic due to the general fear of all the big strong people outside her home forests. She is also acutely aware of the fact that she has a terrible sense of what is, and isn't, dangerous.

Allae refers to herself as a woman, however she is actually a feminine male.

Ability score roll: 17, 16, 15, 13, 12, 7 (wis)
