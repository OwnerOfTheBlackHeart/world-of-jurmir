Yrsa Halladottir, Dwarven Druid

Picture of Yrsa

Yrsa is a dwarven druid from Idanthus, being the first of the native born to undertake a journey outside of the mountain.

Yrsa is a cheerful and curious type who is looking forward to experiencing more of the world. She has an interest in these creatures called birds and how fun flying sounds.

Yrsa wears a bra of holding that was given to her by the patron mothers of her home town, partially because it was a gift and partially because it's convenient not having a pair of dwarven tits in the way of casting.

Ability score roll: 17, 16, 15, 14, 13, 12
