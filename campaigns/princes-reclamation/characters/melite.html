Melite of Cytherna, Orc Fighter

Picture of Malite

Melite came to Indos after a short career in pit fighting, seeking a life with more opportunity than Cytherna had to offer her. She settled in the Duchy of Akrida, though she quickly found herself outshined by the clerics and paladins, with the National Church of the Gods handling most of the work she would normally be able to take.

Melite has two real goals: To actually own a nice house of her own and to prove herself... mainly to herself.

Ability score roll: 18, 16, 15, 14, 10, 12
