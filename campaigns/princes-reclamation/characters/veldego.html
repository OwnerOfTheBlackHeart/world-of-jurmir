Veldego, Half-Dragon Psion

Picture of Veldego

Veldego is a female half-dragon human who may be found among the court of Duke Umur. While Veldego has a limited amount of martial skill, most of it comes from her half-dragon heritage. Instead, she prefers to think of herself as a scholar and master of the mind.

The dragon-hide scale mail that Veldego wears is made from the scales of Duchess Myli, which Veldego paid well to have stolen for her over the course of nearly a decade, which she is highly proud of. Her armor is a good guide as to what Veldego views as worthy of being in her horde: Gems and trophies, especially those with stories or taken from enemies she particularly hates, are her favored pieces, while Veldego places little value in coins, beyond their ability to get her items she actually wants.

Veldego has D cup tities.

Veldego is a feminine herm and has a 7 inch penis.

Veldego is the daughter of Duke Umur and Rochelle, a late native woman that Duke Umur liked because she had green hair. Because of the fact that Duke Umur can't add Veldego to his collection, because she is HIS daughter, he only cares for her as much as she can be useful to him.

Veldego is neutral evil.
