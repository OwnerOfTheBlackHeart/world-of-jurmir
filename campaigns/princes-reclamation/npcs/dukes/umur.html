Duke Umur, The People Collector of Grunbaum

Picture of Umur

Umur is the male, mature adult, green dragon DC (16) who rules over Grunbaum. He is known for his authoritarian leadership style and his support of the slave trade.

Duke Umur has many other titles, such as Ratslayer, Deer Hunter, Elf Eater, or Treekeeper, though the title he earned through his most recent hobby, collecting and breeding humanoids with rare traits, is his favorite by far.

Duke Umur has an understanding of exactly where he stands politically and physically, and is more than willing to work with those of a higher status than him, though those of equal status must ensure that Umur will benefit enough from their exchange in order to having him work with them. Umur enjoys the feeling of being in control and will give strange, or nonsensical, orders to test the loyalty of those under his command. Umur is a dragon of his word and will go through Hell just to ensure that his agreements and promises are kept, however, he is also exceedingly careful of what he is willing to agree to. Just because Umur is true to any agreement he makes doesn't mean that he isn't willing to lie.

Umur supports legislation that weakens the good aligned leaders and improves the slave trade. Locally, Umur focuses on his war with Ruvanna, expanding his slave trade (namely so that he has more opportunity to find exotic/strange humanoids for his collection), and on punishing those that disobey his orders. As long as they follow his commands and pay their taxes, Umur doesn't care if an official is corrupt.

Umur likes to fuck Duchess Ammetu any time they meet and he is more than happy to be take humanoids up on offers of sex, though he does so for his pleasure, not theirs. If someone pleases him, he will allow them to mate with his collection.

Umur likes to occasionally fuck members of his collection. Umur is gentle when he fucks the members of his collection that are prized and/or willing additions.

Umur wishes to acquire Duchess Therlas Ilaro of Ruvanna, who he plans on using as a sex toy, but not breaking her mind, just so that he can see her writhe in pain.
