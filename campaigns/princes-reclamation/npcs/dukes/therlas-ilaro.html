Duchess Therlas Ilaro of Ruvanna

Picture of Therlas

Therlas is a female elf. Therlas wears simple clothes for a duchess, preferring to garb herself in a set of purple robes with teal to green embroidery and a matching lining. Therlas usually carries a satchel with her spellbook and 2 enchanted daggers, one which is a +1 bane (dragon), the other being +1 flaming.

Therlas moves with the expected grace of an elf, and a little more, and is skilled in traditional elven song and dance, though she often lacks the "noble air" about her. However, Therlas tries to maintain a strong retainer presence, with most of her retainers being kept nearby. While Therlas generally acts respectful around other nobility, it is no secret that she shares the sentiment of her dukedom when it comes to non-elves.

Therlas supports legislation that restricts the ability of dukedoms to operate outside their borders, that restrict or abolish slavery, and that might cause division among the other dukes. Locally, Therlas spends a great deal of effort hunting down the spies of other dukes, securing her borders against raids from Grunbaum, and slowly pushing her people into well protected fortresses.

Therlas prefers to avoid noble functions and actively avoids Duke Umur.

Therlas's name means "wild mist in the summer sky".

Therlas rightfully fears that Duke Umur wishes to add her to his collection. She believes that he will bite off her limbs then have his servants cause her to regenerate them as a kind of infinite food farm.
