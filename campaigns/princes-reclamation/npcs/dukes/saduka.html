Duke Saduka, The Winged Knight of Gefro Kuste

Picture of Saduka Picture of Saduka's "Noble" Form

Duke Saduka is a young adult (CR 13) male silver dragon. When in dragon form, Saduka moves with a confident strut and exudes an aura of nobility. In "noble" form, Saduka appears as a silver haired elf wearing a fine military uniform with a cane, which is topped with a gold sphere, in which he keeps a scale of his mate, Duchess Myli.

Saduka is always polite and respectful, holding himself to a high standard even when those around him stoop to vile lows, and he takes great pride in his honor. When not managing Gefro Kuste or spending time with Myli, he practices noble etiquette and skills.

Saduka supports legislation that helps improve the security and unity of the duchies, protects the merchant class, makes the noble class serve the people more, and otherwise encourages meritocracy, though he also supports having an increased military presence, to better protect the people from attack. In terms of local politics, Saduka focuses on managing the merchant and noble classes, as well as pushing for a well organized, trained and behaved professional military force.
