Duke Otto of Sirine

Picture of Otto

Duke Otto is a tiefling male. While his clothes are all of noble quality, his favored colors are black, vibrant red, and muted greens and browns.

Otto generally avoids noble functions where he is not needed, as both his nature as a tiefling and as his title of Duke of Sirine make him endure constant scrutiny and suspicion.

Otto is a sorcerer 5, rogue 5.

Otto has a 7 inch penis.
