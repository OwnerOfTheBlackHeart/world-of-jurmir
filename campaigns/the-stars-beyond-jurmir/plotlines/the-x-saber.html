The X-Saber

The X-Saber is an extremely heavily upgraded Ringworks Wanderer intended to act as a bomber and as a display of the technology that Starlight Starship Systems has available for licensing.

Outline

Figure Out what to do with the X-Saber

Several entities are interested in acquiring the X-Saber, each of which is offering a different reward in exchange for the ship.

Get Reward

After determining what to do with the X-Saber, the party gains the appropriate reward.

Ninans

The ninans are willing to offer access to ninan shipyards in exchange for the "gifting" of the X-Saber (+2 tier to future ships) as well as 3 suits of graphite carbon skin armor (core 197).

Starlight Starship Systems

Starlight will customize a ship for the party and will pay the party 6,000 credits. They will pay an extra 1,000 credits if the party is willing to deliver the X-Saber to Marata.

Merchant's Guild

The merchant's guild is willing to give the party 2,000 credits, a private space station (2x build points for normal ship), and membership within the guild (including opportunities to take jobs for the merchant's guild).

Drow

The drow are willing to offer a subzero ice carbine (armory 16) and 2 explorer handcoils (armory 15). Drow will express interest in finding more information about the Gap.

Aeon Empire

The Aeon Empire is willing to part with an AG assault rifle (MM 7, 50 rounds), amber hyperboloid aeon stone (armory 110) and a dark blue rhomboid aeon stone (core 223). The Aeon Empire is willing to pay for the acquisitions of specific materials and preforming of specific materials.
