Crash of the Red Raven

A transport ship carrying passengers and cargo was passing by the jungle planet of Voqua when it is shot down. As the ship falls from the sky, those who manage to get to escape pods are killed as they eject from the Red Raven. Among the survivors or the crash are the party, who are rapidly being approached by hostile forces and seperated from their equipment by halls of the ship and those enemies that have managed to enter the remains of the Red Raven.
