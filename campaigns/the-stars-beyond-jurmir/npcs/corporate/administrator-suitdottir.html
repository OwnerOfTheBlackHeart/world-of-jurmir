Description

Administrator Runa Suitdottir is a well dressed halfling with a black bun on the back of her head without a single misplaced, or dangling, hair. Administrator Suitdottir is almost never seen without at least 2 assistants and is nearly always on one of 8 phones.

Administrator Runa Suitdottir tries to act like a caring and kind woman to new people and those outside of her employ. To those within her employ, she is a tough boss with extremely high expectations.
