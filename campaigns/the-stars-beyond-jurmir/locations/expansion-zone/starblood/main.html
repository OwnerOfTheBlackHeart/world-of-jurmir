Starblood

[ ["Owning Species", "Jurmirian"], ["Location", "The Expansion Zone"] ]

Starblood is a desolate world that is only barely livable and surrounded by several planets the wouldn't be considered for colonization, or even terraforming, in the most dire of situations. The only reason that the planet is inhabited is because the dangerous environments of the planet and its surrounding space make the perfect place for military weapons testing and special forces training. As such, almost exclusively military personal inhabit the planet.
