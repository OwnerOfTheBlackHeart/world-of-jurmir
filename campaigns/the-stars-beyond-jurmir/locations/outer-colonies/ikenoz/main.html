Ikenoz Station

[ ["Owning Species", "Criminal (primarily Jurmirian)"], ["Location", "The Outer Colonies"] ]

Located within an asteroid belt of a star system with a red dwarf and no livable planets. It is often considered to be the biggest hub of crime in the Outer Colonies, but the local pirate and mercenary fleets make attacking the station to expensive of an endeavor for the Jurmirian to make a significant move against.
