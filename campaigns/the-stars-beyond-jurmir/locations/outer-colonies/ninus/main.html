The Ninus System

[ ["Owning Species", "Human (speciesist)"], ["Location", "The Outer Colonies"] ]

Within the Ninus solar system are two major worlds: Ninus 5, a tropical world known for a booming agricultural industry as well as the raddest waves in the Outer Colonies, and Ninus 8, a relatively cold world with rich ore deposits and easy access to Ninus's asteroid belt.
