Estifoa

[ ["Owning Species", "Jurmirian"], ["Location", "The Jurmirian Zone"], ["Exports", "Starships, Vehicles, Weapons, Armor"], ["Imports", "Raw Metals, Foods, Organic Compounds"] ]

Estifoa is the largest producer of starships in the Jurmirian Coalition, with a surface that cannot be seen beneath the layers of factories and urban living. The planet has a large focus on efficiency and quality, owing to the fact that Estifoa was originally a dwarven colony.
