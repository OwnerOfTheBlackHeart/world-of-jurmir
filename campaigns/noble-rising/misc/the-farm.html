    The Main House

    The main house is a large, two story wattle and daub building shaped like a rectangle with a thatched roof. The first floor has a kitchen, dining room, pantry, living/talking room, and a latrine. The second story contains 4 bedrooms, each with a wooden wardrobe. Each bedroom can comfortably fit a couple, but can fit up to 4 people without privacy.

    The house currently has 3 goats, 50 chickens (2 roosters), a guard dog, and Chad Thundercock (a 6 pound rooster that screams the sound of thunder).

    The Animal Barn

    The animal barn is a 3 story tall, barn style timber building with a slate roof and floor level windows with shutters. Inside, the building doesn't have any upper floors, but does have 10 stalls along the length of the barn and a bench and tools at the back of the barn.

    The barn currently has 6 cattle (2 bulls and 4 cows), 1 oxe, and 2 pigs.
