The Nera-Methnaes Agreement

The Nera-Methnaes Agreement is a contract signed on Zolter 2nd, 1488 by Duchess Eclavna Ichiir and (then) Baroness Nera Black.

Points

  • Upon ascension to the rank of Duchess, Baroness Nera will maintain the rank of Duke of Taaira Ruzara and Taaira Ruzara will become a duchy within the Queendom of Chalfryn'breena (Greater Methnaes).
  • Baroness Nera will transport 3 bands of Methnaean soldiers, and their supplies, into separate positions of strategic importance within Taaira Ruzara. Baroness Nera will also transport a forth band of exclusively drow Methnaean soldiers, and their supplies, to Blackhide, along with no less than one representative of the Queendom of Chalfryn'breena/Duchy of Methnaes and 1 slave capable of casting the spell Sending, to be handed over to Baroness Nera upon arrival at Blackhide. The primary representative of the Queendom of Chalfryn'breena/Duchy of Methnaes will be given 1 scroll of Two Way Magic Circle (tuned to the city of Barakuir), Ring of the Drow Shape and 1 writ of invitation to Barakuir, to be delivered to Baroness Nera upon arrival to Blackhide.
  • Baroness Nera will transport 400 Methnaean soldiers, and their supplies, to Nelloun Enial County, where they will construct a fortified settlement.
  • Methnaes will provide Baroness Nera with 10 griffons, to be delivered to Blackhide. The griffons may be delivered with the prior mentioned group of 400 Methnaean soldiers, or delivered separately, up to 1 month later.
  • So long as all other agreements are held, Methnaes will not further invade Taaira Ruzara and will withhold the official declaration of war.
