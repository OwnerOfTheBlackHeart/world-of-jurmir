Known Rituals

Ulath's Rite of Rebirth

Prerequisites

A living, non-lizard humanoid.

Costs

1 cup of lizardkind blood and a gem worth at least 100gp.

Focuses

100 pounds of filth, a ritual circle carved into the ground, and a ritual dagger made of wrought iron.

Effects

The subject is placed within the circle while the filth is lathered over the subject before being brought to a knee. The subject stays kneeling, willingly or by force, for 2 hours while they are continually lathered with the filth. During this time, a ritual lead chants arcane words while pouring the cup of blood, drop by drop, into the grooves of the ritual circle. At the end of the two hours, the ritual lead impales the wrought iron dagger into the heart, or head, of the subject, killing them. Any remaining filth is placed over the subject while the dagger remains in the subject. 24 hours later, the subject rises as a troglodyte.

If the subject has 2, or less, class levels, it looses all memory of its past and rises as a standard troglodyte. If the subject has 3, or more, class levels (npc or pc) it loses all racial levels and all ability score modifiers, special attacks, natural armor, special abilities, and all else related to race. The subject then gains all the abilities, hit dice, natural weapons, natural armor, and special abilities of a troglodyte. The subject does not gain any extra experience if the racial levels would leave the subject at a higher ECL than before the ritual. Instead, the amount of experience required to reach the next level increases appropriately.

Unknown Rituals

The Ritual of Draconic Purification and Dilution

Costs

The blood to be purified/diluted, 10 gp per cup of purified blood produced, and 5 cp per cup of diluted blood produced.

Focuses

An alchemical lab.

Effects

The Ritual of Draconic Purification and Dilution can be used to purify or dilute blood with at least traces of draconic blood. When purifying, blood from dragonblooded creatures (sorcerers, those with the draconic template, kobolds) creates dragon typed blood (wyverns, half-dragons) at a 10:1 ratio, dragon typed to true dragon (roll for metallic or chromatic) at a 10:1 ration, and metallic and chromatic true dragon blood into pure dragon blood (rainbow dragon, near Kyranious) at a 10:10:1 ratio. Purifying takes 1 hour per cup of purified blood produced.

When used to dilute dragon blood, pure dragon blood because metallic and chromatic dragon blood at a 1:8:8 ratio, true dragon blood to dragon typed blood at a 1:8 ratio, and dragon typed blood to dragonblooded blood at a 1:8 ratio. Diluting takes 1 hour per cup of blood being diluted.

The Ritual of Moral Devotion

Prerequisites

A living, non-draconic humanoid.

Costs

A symbolic egg crafted for/by the prospective dragonborn which costs 100 gp to create, and requires a handful of scales from dragons. All the scales must be from either a chromatic or metallic dragon.

Effects

Applies the dragonborn template to the recipient of the ritual.
