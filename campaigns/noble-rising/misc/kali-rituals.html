Kali Rituals

Learning Rituals

The easiest way to learn a ritual is to find information on how to perform the ritual wishing to be learned. However, most rituals are not going to be found that way. This leaves two other ways of learning new rituals.

The first is to simply research topics until an unknown ritual can be researched. Once enough points in research topics has been gained, the GM will offer an "idea," which can be researched. Researching the idea takes 2 weeks and costs the research points required by the ritual. After this time, a spellcraft check is rolled versus the learn DC of the ritual. If failed, ritual is not learned, but can be researched again without costing research points. If the spellcraft check is succeeded, then the ritual is learned.

The second way is to declare that a ritual is to be created. Describe the effects of the ritual, possibly working any kinks with the GM, then the GM will determine all of the information concerning the ritual. The research points required for the ritual must be spent, but they can be researched normally in pursuit of the ritual. After this time, a spellcraft check is rolled versus the learn DC of the ritual. If failed, ritual is not learned, but can be researched again without costing research points. If the spellcraft check is succeeded, then the ritual is learned.

Gaining Research Points

Research points can be gained in one of two ways: Declare the topic being researched or declare the research being done. When declaring the topic being researched, the GM will declare the resources necessary for the research and may decide that the topic either isn't able to be researched at the moment or that the topic may count as an existing topic. If declaring the research being done, the GM will determine what topic(s) the research will grant a point in and if the research will reveal any new topics.

Regardless of the method chosen, research takes 1 week. At the end of the week, roll a spellcraft check of DC 15 + 2 per topic of the research. If a success is rolled, each topic the research covers gets one research point and any topics revealed by the research are revealed. If the research is failed, no research points are gained and no topics are revealed, unless the GM decides that a hidden topic will be discovered regardless of success. Success or fail, any materials involved in the research are used up.

Known Rituals

Ulath's Rite of Rebirth

Prerequisites

A living, non-lizard humanoid.

Costs

1 cup of lizardkind blood and a gem worth at least 100gp.

Focuses

100 pounds of filth, a ritual circle carved into the ground, and a ritual dagger made of wrought iron.

Effects

The subject is placed within the circle while the filth is lathered over the subject before being brought to a knee. The subject stays kneeling, willingly or by force, for 2 hours while they are continually lathered with the filth. During this time, a ritual lead chants arcane words while pouring the cup of blood, drop by drop, into the grooves of the ritual circle. At the end of the two hours, the ritual lead impales the wrought iron dagger into the heart, or head, of the subject, killing them. Any remaining filth is placed over the subject while the dagger remains in the subject. 24 hours later, the subject rises as a troglodyte.

If the subject has 2, or less, class levels, it looses all memory of its past and rises as a standard troglodyte. If the subject has 3, or more, class levels (npc or pc) it loses all racial levels and all ability score modifiers, special attacks, natural armor, special abilities, and all else related to race. The subject then gains all the abilities, hit dice, natural weapons, natural armor, and special abilities of a troglodyte. The subject does not gain any extra experience if the racial levels would leave the subject at a higher ECL than before the ritual. Instead, the amount of experience required to reach the next level increases appropriately.

The Ritual of the Skybold

Discovery

DC 13, dragon blood 2, kobolds 5, transmutation 2

Prerequisites

A kobold without wings.

Costs

1 cup of dragon typed blood per 100lbs of weight, 50gp if the recipient is 5th, or lower, level or 150gp if the recipient is 6th, or higher, level.

Effects

Grant the recipient the Dragon Wings feat and, if the recipient is 6th level or greater, the Improved Dragon Wings feat.

Unknown Rituals

The Ritual of Draconic Purification and Dilution

Discovery

DC 15, dragonblooded 2, dragon blood 5, true dragons 2, transmutation 1

Costs

The blood to be purified/diluted, 10 gp per cup of purified blood produced, and 5 cp per cup of diluted blood produced.

Focuses

An alchemical lab.

Effects

The Ritual of Draconic Purification and Dilution can be used to purify or dilute blood with at least traces of draconic blood. When purifying, blood from dragonblooded creatures (sorcerers, those with the draconic template, kobolds) creates dragon typed blood (wyverns, half-dragons) at a 10:1 ratio, dragon typed to true dragon (roll for metallic or chromatic) at a 10:1 ration, and metallic and chromatic true dragon blood into pure dragon blood (rainbow dragon, near Kyranious) at a 10:10:1 ratio. Purifying takes 1 hour per cup of purified blood produced.

When used to dilute dragon blood, pure dragon blood because metallic and chromatic dragon blood at a 1:8:8 ratio, true dragon blood to dragon typed blood at a 1:8 ratio, and dragon typed blood to dragonblooded blood at a 1:8 ratio. Diluting takes 1 hour per cup of blood being diluted.

The Ritual of Moral Devotion

Discovery

DC 20 or DC 12 with guidance, dragonblooded 3, true dragons 1, draconic essence 5

Prerequisites

A living, non-draconic humanoid.

Costs

A symbolic egg crafted for/by the prospective dragonborn which costs 100 gp to create, and requires a handful of scales from dragons. All the scales must be from either a chromatic or metallic dragon.

Effects

Applies the dragonborn template to the recipient of the ritual.

Dragon Blood Injection

Discovery

DC 17, dragonblooded 5, dragon blood 5, transmutation 1

Prerequisites

A living, non-draconic creature.

Costs

1 cup of dragonblooded blood per 10 lbs of weight, 1 cup of dragon typed blood per 50lbs of weight or 1 cup of true dragon blood per 250lbs of weight.

Effects

Applies the draconic creature template to the recipient of the ritual.

Create Dragon Kind

Discovery

DC 21, dragon blood 5, true dragons 5, transmutation 3

Prerequisites

A living, non-dragon typed creature.

Costs

1,000gp worth of "sacrificial horde" items and 1 cup of dragon typed blood per 10lbs of weight, 1 cup of true dragon blood per 50lbs of weight, or 1 cup of pure dragon blood per 250lbs of weight.

Effects

Applies the dragon type to the recipient of the ritual. They gain the traits associated with the dragon type and if they have racial hit dice, they become dragon hit dice with all the bonuses associated with that and gain +2 natural armor. If the creature doesn't have racial hit dice, it instead gains a bite attack for a creature one size smaller than itself and +2 to strength and constitution.

Draconic Conversion

Discovery

DC 19, dragonblooded 1, dragon blood 5, true dragons 2, half-dragons 2, transmutation 2

Prerequisites

A create that the half-dragon template can be applied to.

Costs

A 5,000gp gem of similar color for chromatic dragons or 5,000gp worth of the metal of a metallic dragon and 1 cup of true dragon blood of the color of half-dragon per 10 lbs of weight or 1 cup of pure dragon blood per 50lbs of weight.

Effects

Applies the half-dragon template to the recipient of the ritual.

Draconic Reversion

Discovery

DC 15, dragonblooded 1, dragon blood 1, kobolds 5, transmutation 2

Prerequisites

A kobold that is not pure scale.

Costs

1 cup of pure dragon blood per 10 lbs of weight.

Effects

The recipient becomes a purescale kobold, losing the penalties to strength and constitution, gaining +2 to intelligence and charisma, gaining a bite attack which deals 1d3 damage and a tail attack that deals 1d3 damage, and the powerful build ability from goliaths. If the recipient is also of the dragon type, it gains the pureblood subtype. The kobold gains an LA of +1.

The Ritual of the Requeening

Discovery

DC 17, insects 5, nen 2, transmutation 2

Prerequisites

At least one nen drone or warrior.

Costs

10gp per hit die effected.

Effects

Select a person to become the nens' queen and a location to serve as the nens' hive. The nen drone(s) and warrior(s) now consider that person their queen and the location their hive.
