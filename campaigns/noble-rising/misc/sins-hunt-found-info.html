Sin's Hunt Found Info

This page contains information that has been given to Sin Typhoon concerning her hunt as to why the hatchlings of the Spelllake Clan had hatched as corrupted monsters.

Cezvoc, god of monsters, is the actual cause of the mutations. The abominations that were birthed from the eggs were abnormally large and aggressive with spiked spines, tails with stingers (like wyverns) and bone blades on their wrists and elbows.

Drow

Prior to the start of the game, Sin traveled to the Underdark in order to talk to the drow about possible information related to the hatchlings being monsters. While the drow don't know much about the minor gods on the surface, or other possible causes, they are well versed in the Red King and his followers. She learned:

  • The blood of aberrations can corrupt normal creatures, turning them into mutated monsters or aberrations. Dead aberrations should be cleansed by burning the corpse to ash.
  • The followers of the Red King have, in the past, developed magic that allows the corruption of creatures. This magic is frequently lost due to the intervention of other forces, but almost all forms of the magic either create a new creature or mutate an unborn child. They view this as unlikely due to the mutation magic being expensive and precise.
  • The Red King likes to whisper to those who seek the powers of destruction. His requests often seem simple, but usually have horrific long term effects.
  • While demons and devils usually work for the interests of themselves and their hidden lords within hell, some will work as forces of the Red King, convincing mortals that they are working for/with a demon king or devil lord when they are actually doing the Red King's bidding.
  • While rust monsters are aberrations, naturally born rust monsters lack the corrupting properties of other aberrations. Magically created rust monsters do possess the corrupting properties, though they can be told apart from natural rust monsters because of their significantly increased aggression.
  • Corruption typically results in some of the following mutations:
    • Extra limbs, such as extra arms, legs, wings or tails.
    • Tentacles on creatures that shouldn't normally have them.
    • Multiple mouths, typically filled with either sharp, dagger-like teeth or saw-like teeth.
    • More eyes than is typical for a normal creature. They may grow near normal eyes, around the head, or all over the body.
    • The ability to speak multiple words at the same time. This is particularly useful for speaking Voidspeak quickly, as the language relies of multiple sentences being spoken at the same time.
    • Murderous madness, usually paired with a strong need for cannibalism.
    • Thick, fatty skin, usually with an oily or goopy secretion covering it.
    • Resistance, or immunity, to damage from strong acids.
    • Poison glands, which usually lessen the target's strength or start to render them insane.
  • Some of the drow that travel to the surface have reported that a Lady Nera Black is known for carrying the unholy symbols of cultists she's killed. She has 2 symbols, one of an unknown god and one of the Red King as of the last reports.
