Whores

A brothel has has 7 bonuses: Comfort, Quality, Quantity, Fetish, Services, Operation Costs, and Customer Base. Comfort is the level of comfort for the customers, quality is the average quality of the "product", quantity is the amount of "product" available to the customers, fetish is the amount of niche fetishes appealed to, services is the number of non-whoring services available, operation costs is the measures that reduce the cost of operating the business, and customer base is the volume or quality of customers available to the business.

To determine the profit for a given month, roll a d20 and add the seven bonuses / penalties. For each point higher than 10, multiply the net roll by the profit multiplier from the table below. If you roll below 10, multiply the net roll by the loss multiplier from the table below. If a 10 is rolled, then the business breaks even.

  • Comfort: 6
  • Quality: 0
  • Quantity: -1
  • Fetish: 0
  • Services: 4
  • Operation Cost: 0
  • Customer Base: -2
