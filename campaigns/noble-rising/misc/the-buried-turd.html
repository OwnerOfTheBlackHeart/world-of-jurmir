The Buried Turd

A brothel has has 7 bonuses: Comfort, Quality, Quantity, Fetish, Services, Operation Costs, and Customer Base. When you roll for brothel profit, add all modifiers together in order to determine the bonus to the profit roll.

  • Comfort: -1
  • Quality: 0
  • Quantity: -2
  • Fetish: 0
  • Services: 0
  • Operation Cost: 0
  • Customer Base: -2
