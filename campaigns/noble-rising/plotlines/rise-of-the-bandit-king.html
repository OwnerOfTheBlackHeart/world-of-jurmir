Rise of the Bandit King

Intro

The number of bandits around Desserr has increased as of late. Unknown to the peoples of Desserr, the throne of Bandit King has been recently declared and the desperate are flocking to the "powerful" new military force.

The Bandit King is utilizing faster breeding races, such as catfolk and kobolds, along with time acceleration magic in order to breed and grow new bandits quickly.

Important Events to this Plotline

The Bandit King is Declared

Unbeknownst to most peoples, a man has declared himself the Bandit King. He has begun attracting desperate, violent, and cruel peoples to his cause. His fortress lies south of Desserr, along the edge of the Howling Peaks.

The Bandit King's First Public Attack

The Bandit King has launched three simultaneous attacks on the villages of Shimmergrove, Mistyard, and Redwharf.
