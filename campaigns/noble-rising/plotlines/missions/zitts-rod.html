Getting a Greater Rod of Maximize Metamagic from the Dark Shrine of Evil

Zitt's Rod

Intro

Sariel has been asked by Zitt, the kobold wizard living in the Eight Great Towers, to acquire a Greater Rod of Maximize Metamagic from the Dark Shrine of Evil.

Note

Virgil the Vile is the vampire that lives in the Dark Shrine of Evil.
