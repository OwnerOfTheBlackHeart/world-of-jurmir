Red Wing's Fang

Intro

Kali and his assigned army have been sent to acquire more kobolds for invasive experimentation. He has with him Austen Austinson (minion) and the two kobolds Arta and Vom. He has chosen to seek out the Strongwald clan as his source of kobolds and for knowledge of how and why they killed a dragon.

Red Wing's Fang is a +2 Flaming Spellstoring Longsword (18,315gp) made from a dragon's tooth around 4 foot in length with a bright red, scaled, leather grip and a hand guard made from a single ruby.

Enemies

  • Dame Quilynn Loreweaver

General Outline

  1. Arrive at the Strongwald Clan's trapped entrance.
    • If the party approaches with caution and attempts to get the attention of the hidden defenders, then a kobold will appear behind them from a side room. If they are able to convince the kobold, which should be easy, then the kobold will take them to the side room and bring them up through the trapdoor in the ceiling.
    • If the party searches for the back entrance, it is a DC 25 spot check to find it
    • If within range for the spell, locate object cast to find the back entrance or secret door will point the party to three boulders.
  2. The party meats with the important members of the kobold clan
    • Party talks to All-Watcher Sorpo (female)
      • Sorpo would like to help with Kali's research, however all the kobolds currently have tasks they are working on
      • Sorpo will need a guarantee that his clan will get the researched ritual(s), likely a contract
      • Sorpo will state that the High Priest, Tiro, has requested the use of 12 kobolds and that if his task is completed, then Sorpo can release the kobolds to Kali's experiments
    • Party talks to Loremaster Dag (male)
      • Dag is happy to tell about how Kyranious chose the Strongwald clan to perform two great tasks:
        To guard her personal horde on the material plane and to cleanse the local area of false dragons
      • If asked about the Heretic Dragon, then Dag will mention that after the defeat of the red dragon Bryndrys, the blue dragon warband was the undisputed rulers of everything north of the Howling Peaks and some of the dragons let that position go to their head, namely the Heretic Dragons Chuzzit The Jealous (masculine herm) and his mate Geldrunis The Clever (also male).

        The two dragons attempted to start by demanding their hordes be lined with the clan's treasure and when the kobolds of Strongwald stated that their treasure belonged only to Kyranious, the two dragons threatened the clan. That night, the kobolds sent out a force of 6 scouts, 4 mounted warriors, 3 priests and 3 sorcerers to find the dragons' lair, they returned that day having only seen that the pair of dragons was moving towards the burrow.

        Using the spells disintegrate and shape stone, the kobolds created an entrance large enough for both dragons to enter and posted 2 kobolds out front of it. When Chuzzit and Geldrunis landed, the kobolds told them that the clan decided to ask forgiveness and has moved their treasure within the newly created entrance. When the dragons entered, there was no treasure and the kobolds destroyed the pillars holding up several rock falls. When the rocks fell, they pinned the dragons by their wings and the kobolds descended upon them from every angle in order to kill them.

        After killing Chuzzit and Geldrunis, every jem, coin, and treasure embedded in their hides were removed and taken to Kyranious's horde and the two dragons' pelts were turned into armor and given to the kobolds who showed the most bravery, prowess, and tactical creativity in the battle. Of the armor suits made, 2 have been enchanted and added to the horde and 3 remain being passed down from great hunter to great hunter.
      • There is also a mural that depicts 4 kobolds (Gett (male), Kilpi (female), Ilgo (female) and Nultu (female)) burning down a forest with 6 hydras in it. The 4 kobolds had found a den of hydras and rather than fighting the hydras directly, the kobolds drew the creatures into a forest that had been facing a drought and lit the forest on fire to kill them.
    • Talks to High Priest Tiro (male)
      • Tiro will happily tell the party that the Strongwald clan is one of the chosen clans of Kyranious, each of which looks over and protects portions of her personal horde. A portion of this duty is hunting down fake dragons.
      • If asked about his request for 12 kobolds, Trio will explain that a weapon, Red Wing's Fang, has been located by the Strongwald clan and that the weapon could empower a ritual to garner further favor with Kyranious. He will explain that it is located within a vault in Baytide.
        • If asked about Red Wing's Fang, Tiro will describe it as a longsword made of pure dragon bone with glowing red runes across the blade
        • If asked about the history of Red Wing's Fang, Tiro will reveal that it was made by a dwarf who found the still warm corpse of Bryndrys. This dwarf, whose name Tiro does not know, hollowed out the chest of Brydrys and used his bones and scales to create a workshop where he carved down Bryndrys's largest fang into a sword and then made stain from the dragon's blood and engraved the bone sword with the blood stain. After its creation, Tiro doesn't know what happened to it.

    • Party investigates Deathfall
      • Party talks to the guards/knights

        • Victaella Duskwalker is looking for people willing to do "jobs"

          • A thieves' guild called the Night Shadows angered the Red Hand Guild and was systematically hunted down because of it. It is said that the Red Hand Guild burned down the "guild house" of the Night Shadows and killed everyone that ran out of the burning building.
          • The Red Hand Guild is generally considered to be one of the most secretive of the thieves' guilds
          • The Red Hand Guild has been a constant thorn in the Order of the Red Sun's side, both because they are so difficult to find any solid evidence on and because when they do manage to, the Red Hand Guild has a relatively powerful military capability.

        • Big Tim always has need of messengers and carriers
    • Party finds Dame Quilynn Loreweaver
      • If asked what she wants in exchange for Tyranttooth, she will give three tasks:

        • Strange bug monsters have come to the surface in response to the portal. She needs some examples to study, alive if possible.
      • If asked about the crime and/or cults, she will say that this many humans in one place is a perfect example of why the breeding curse is in place. The sheer number of crimes and cults mean that the knights and mercenaries can only respond to the most important and most dangerous.
      • If asked why she is here, she will state that Queen Venvyre ordered the people of Asokmoat here 300 years ago, after their defeat and the mass application of the breeding curse. Count Black sent her here 12 years ago when the previous knight failed in his duties.
      • If asked about Ivy Cooke, Victaella Duskwalker, or Lady Vonesti Hunitheplithym, she will respond that she doesn't know of any of those people, but that House Hunitheplithym was killed off 6 years ago by an unknown assailant.
    • Party rests at a tavern, or the like.
      • if the party hasn't run into Ivy, she approaches them, asking if they could help her with something. In disguise of course.
