A Bow for Akkus

Intro

Seeking to gain favor with her god, Nevaeh Byrne has come to Nera's temple with a mutually beneficial proposal: Nera will seek out a bow worthy of Akkus and Nevaeh perform the offering to Akkus.

Enemy CRs

  • CR 5 - 9 (3 large water elementals, 1 large air elemental, 2 adult tojanida, 3 greater barghest)
  • CR 6 - 3 (1 chain devil, 1 lightning bolt trap, 1 belker)
  • CR 7 - 7 (2 pit traps of holding, 2 lillends, 2 huge fire elementals, 1 hellcat)
  • CR 8 - 3 (1 elder xorn, 2 erinyes [devils])
  • CR 9 - 10 (2 greater earth elemental, 1 avoral, 1 dropping ceiling trap, 1 nessian warhound, elder tojanida, 1 incendiary cloud trap, 1 bone devil, 1 wide mouth pit trap, 1 night hag)
  • CR 10 - 1 (1 couatl)
  • CR 11 - 2 (1 elder fire elemental, 1 elder water elemental)
  • CR 13 - 1 (1 ghaele)
  • CR 14 - 1 (1 astral deva [angel])

General Outline

  1. Nera visits her temple
  2. Nevaeh attempts to convince Nera:
    • Nevaeh mentions that this will be a sure fire way to draw Akkus to the temple (possibly mentioning that it will be an offering she can't ignore).
    • If Nera pushes for it, Nevaeh will join her on the quest.
    • If Nera seems uninterested, Nevaeh will offer information on the cult of Cezvoc, specifically about the existence of the Unworded One and the fact that a single cleric seems to be the source of the current spreading of the cult.
  3. Once Nevaeh has convinced Nera, she will reveal the following potential bows to her:

Targets of a crit must make a DC 20 fort save, or be stunned until the end of their next turn.
      • Located somewhere in the swamps of Dhaer'quess's Shal
    • Thalti: A longbow made of black metal, believed to have originated somewhere in Hell. +2 distance seeking composite longbow (20).
      • Located near Kala, Deaavh Hilluk.
    • Yahribel: A longbow seemingly made from undamaged pearl, Yahribel is said to have been wielded by a solar before they met their end to a powerful yuan-ti "hero". Yahribel is said to have been returned to the surface by a mind flayer trading it for safe passage. +2 holy composite longbow (20).
      • Located in one of the towers of the Eight Great Towers.
