Elizabeth's Beauty Tour

Intro

After becoming the first priestess of Ilvith, Elizabeth has decided to do a beauty tour as a way to please her new goddess and recruit for her cult.

This quest is a rebuild quest.

General Outline

  1. Elizabeth decides she would like to go on a beauty tour.
  2. Elizabeth determines the path she would like to travel and the settlements she would like to stop at.
  3. Elizabeth begins pricing out her tour:
    • Flying mount(s)
      • Hippogriff: 100 ft. fly, 150 gp per day, 5,000 gp to purchase a trained adult
      • Griffon: 80 ft. fly, 225 gp per day, 9,000 gp to purchase a trained adult
      • Giant Eagle: 80 ft. fly, 150 gp per day, 5,000 gp to purchase a trained adult
      • Giant Owl: 70 ft. fly, 150 gp per day, 5,000 gp to purchase a trained adult
    • Bag of Holding
      • Type 1: 2,500 gp, 250 lbs limit, 15 lbs
      • Type 2: 5,000 gp, 500 lbs limit, 25 lbs
      • Type 3: 7,400 gp, 1,000 lbs limit, 35 lbs
      • Type 4: 10,000 gp, 1,500 lbs limit, 60 lbs
    • Advertisement
      • Merchant Targeted Advertisement: 10 gp per 10 persons
      • Peasant Targeted Advertisement: 15 gp per 10 persons
      • Noble Targeted Advertisement: 25 gp per 10 persons
      • Merchant/Peasant Targeted Advertisement: 20 gp per 10 persons
    • Price per peasant
      • Makeup, 2 gp
      • Jewelry, 5 gp
      • Clothes, 3 gp, 4 lbs
      • Transformatives, 25 gp, 0.1 lbs
      • Total, no Transformatives: 10 gp, 4 lbs
      • Total, with Transformatives: 35 gp, 4.1 lbs
    • Price per merchant
      • Makeup, 10 gp
      • Jewelry, 50 gp
      • Clothes, 30 gp, 6 lbs
      • Transformatives, 125 gp, 0.3 lbs
      • Total, no Transformatives: 90 gp, 6 lbs
      • Total, with Transformatives: 215 gp, 6.3 lbs
    • Price per noble
      • Makeup, 20 gp
      • Jewelry, 105 gp
      • Clothes, 75 gp, 10 lbs
      • Transformatives, 300 gp, 0.5 lbs
      • Total, no Transformatives: 200 gp, 10 lbs
      • Total, with Transformatives: 500 gp, 10.5 lbs
  4. Elizabeth goes on her tour. At each settlement, Elizabeth needs to roll at least a DC 20 disguise check (take 10 is an option) to successfully tour a settlement. A DC 20 diplomacy check is required to recruit from a settlement, with every 2 points beyond the initial DC recruiting another member of her cult. Using transformatives reduces both base DCs. If 10% of persons are given transformatives, the DCs are reduced to 18. If 25% are given transformatives, the DCs are reduced to 15. If 50%, or more, are given transformatives, the DCs are reduced to 10.
  5. Upon returning, Elizabeth has up to 1 month to acquire housing for the coming cultists.

Treasure

There is unlikely to be any treasure acquired over the course of this mission.
