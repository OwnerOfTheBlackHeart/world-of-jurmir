The Grafted Wolf

Intro

The Teamsters' and Porters' Economic Alliance of Taaria Ruzara has posted a stone grade job, with the following message:

Dear slayers,
 Several members of the Alliance have reported spotting or being attacked by some odd wolf. Most have described it as being scaled, but due to the fact that the majority of them have done so while quite drunk, it may just be a single tale spreading among "concerned" drunkards. I have placed a 500 gold reward for this quest's completion.

Sincerely,
Lyla Hunter, Secretary at the Alliance Hall

Enemies

  • Flying Wolf
  • Scaled Wolf

General Outline

  1. Get the mission from the Slayer's Guild (wood grade quest)
    • A strange wolf has been attacking the northern road
    • The wolf has scales
    • 500gp reward
    • The quest has been put out by the Teamsters' and Porters' Economic Alliance of Taaria Ruzara
  2. The party investigates
    • Party talks to Lyla Hunter
      • The attacks are happening on the northern road, around 2 miles from the walls
      • The stories are usually of 1 or 2 of the scaled wolves
      • She thinks that the wolves being scaly is a drunken tale
      • 6 are confirmed dead, 13 have been attacked and the wolves only attack groups of 4 or less
    • Party talks to the crowd
      • The attacks are happening on the northern road, around 2 miles from the walls
      • The attacks are usually 1 or 2 of the scaled wolves
      • Some report that they saw a wolf flying in the sky before the attack
      • The wolves don't attack large groups
      • One saw a "small person" walking around with a journal go unattacked
      • The wolves are far more aggressive than normal wolves, but ignore runners once there are some corpses
    • Party investigates an attack site
      • DC 15 search - There are 2 distinct sets of tracks
      • DC 20 survival - The second set of starts and ends not far from the corpses
      • 1 body has been eaten to the bone
      • DC 12 spot - there are drag marks into the woods
      • A small cart has been tipped over
      • DC 14 search - There are bite marks across the cart
  3. The party follows the northern road and is attacked by the scaly wolf and a flying wolf
  4. (Optional) The party tracks the grafted wolves to their den
    • The party fights 2 more scaly wolves and another flying wolf
    • Dolph will be in the cave, studying the experiments
  5. The party finishes the mission and returns to the Guild for 500gp
