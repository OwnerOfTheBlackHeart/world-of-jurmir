Journey to the River Cain

Intro

Galadriel has decided to go on a quest to find the River Cain.

General Outline

  1. Party accepts quest from the Slayers' Guild: See quest note
  2. The party may visit Lyla at the Alliance Hall in Desserr. She can reveal:
    • The highest volume of attacks happens on the western side of Desserr
    • The closest associated bandit attack was within 3 miles of Desserr
    • The bandits have shown signs of coordination, such as showing in greater numbers against bigger shipments
    • Members of the Alliance claim that a settlement has been taken over by the bandits
