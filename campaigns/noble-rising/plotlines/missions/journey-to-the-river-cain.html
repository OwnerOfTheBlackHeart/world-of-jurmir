Journey to the River Cain

Intro

Galadriel has decided to go on a quest to find the River Cain.

General Outline

  1. Party reaches Mam Daruhm
  2. Party searches for a way to find the River Cain
    • The party asks for directions to the River Cain: No one knows the way, but all know that the River Cain is not a place for the living.
    • The party gos to Da Ironbash:
      • Da is very much against the idea of mortals going to the River Cain. It takes a DC 20 diplomacy check for her to reveal that the last time that happened, and the mortals survived, they angered Lindo enough for him to hunt the mortals down. During the time that he was away from the River Cain, the number of ghosts and other lost souls caused a flood of incorporeal creatures into the dwarven cities.
      • If Da is convinced to help, she will require a "toy" used by someone with a connection to Lindo. She will also mention that "Hide from Undead" is incredibly useful when nearing the River Cain.
      • Performing the ritual will cost the party 1,000 gold, but will give them a map to the River Cain.
    • There is no graveyards, tombs, catacombs, or churches of Lindo
      • All bodies go to the Bloodworks
      • All executions are performed at the Bloodworks
      • The Bloodworks is run by a local sect of the Church of Zigies, known as the Seekers of the Bloodless God.
      • Most worshipers at the The Clockwork Factorum avoid the Seekers of the Bloodless God.
      • The Seekers of the Bloodless God turn all of the corpses into fertilizer that they sell. This knowledge can only be acquired at the Bloodworks.
    • The party talks to the mayor:
      • He doesn't know where the River Cain is.
      • He will warn that going to the River Cain, and failing, will draw the ire of Sithrak.
      • He suggests the derro might know.
      • He suggests that Da Ironbash, a powerful wizard could potential divine the way.
  3. The party searches for a derro:
    • Three track rolls (DC 18) must be succeeded to find a derro
    • The derro will demand an offering be brought to their shrine
    • The derro's shrine is surrounded by a 50 foot pit trap. If anyone becomes bloodied within the pit trap, the derro will reveal a nearby door to the River Cain.
  4. The party navigates to the River Cain: The party must make 5 DC 21 survival checks in order to navigate.
  5. The party finds the door to the River Cain. It is guarded by a Young Adult Red Dragon Skeleton with 3 heads. Each head can use a negative energy breath attack (10d10 damage, DC 19 fort for half) once per day. The fight is CR 10.
