Amassing Bandits

Intro

Bandits have been amassing around Desserr. Rumor holds that the bandits are being lead by a central force. Some have even reported seeing a fortress having been erected almost overnight.

Quest Note

Dear Slayers,
  I represent the Teamsters' and Porters' Economic Alliance of Taaria Ruzara, and I have placed this opportunity for mutual benefit due to a detestable risk factor to the transporters of the economic lifeblood of our fair county. This risk factor would be a notable rise in the detestable occupation of banditry, particularly of the organized kind.

  These foul bandits have been striking our poor movers of goods on nearly every road we have attempted to send them on and I have even heard word of these foul peoples striking at storehouses. I have also heard rumor that these organized thieves have captured a settlement for their use against the good peoples of Desserr. This settlement poses a serious risk to Desserr's financial state, but the guards refuse to act on so little information against such a large group and so I must offer some of the Alliance's funding for compensation for those brave souls who would find and eliminate this organized group of bandits.

Your Potential Employer,
Lyla Hunter

General Outline

  1. Party accepts quest from the Slayers' Guild: See quest note
  2. The party may visit Lyla at the Alliance Hall in Desserr. She can reveal:
    • The highest volume of attacks happens on the western side of Desserr
    • The closest associated bandit attack was within 3 miles of Desserr
    • The bandits have shown signs of coordination, such as showing in greater numbers against bigger shipments
    • Members of the Alliance claim that a settlement has been taken over by the bandits
  3. The party may visit the bar in the Alliance Hall. A successful gather information check can reveal:
    • DC 10 - The bandits attack with forces matching who they are going up against
    • DC 13 - The most attacked road is the western road from Desserr
    • DC 15 - The bandits are attacking out of a settlement to the north west of Desserr
    • DC 17 - The bandits are attacking out of an abandoned fortress to the north west of Desserr
    • DC 20 - A bandit king has risen
  4. If searching for the bandit hide out the party requires 2 fights to gather enough map pieces to discern the location
  5. The party will face a bandit attack when they get within 1 hex of the bandit fortress
  6. The party raids the bandit fortress
  7. The party returns with the bandit king's crown and gets paid
