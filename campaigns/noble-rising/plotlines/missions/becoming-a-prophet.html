Becoming a Prophet

Intro

Due to an ever-growing need to validate herself as a prophet of Lindo, Nera has decided to take on a journey into the Dead Forest of Lindado. There, Nera hopes, that she will find attunement with her inner necromancer, grow closer to her god, and find solid evidence of her prophethood.

Enemy CRs

  • CR 1/2 - 5 (commoner zombie)
  • CR 1 - 10 (wolf skeletons, ghouls)
  • CR 3 - 41 (adventurer's skeleton, allips, ghasts, shadows, gelatinous cube, King Thys De Lange's Corpse)
  • CR 4 - 16 (elf warrior's ghosts, electrum horrors, gargoyles, vampire spawn)
  • CR 5 - 13 (mummies, gold horror, spawn of Kyuss, bone claws, wraiths)
  • CR 6 - 4 (bone drinkers, King Thys De Lange's ghost)
  • CR 7 - 4 (spectre, cloud giant skeletons)
  • CR 8 - 7 (mohrgs, bodaks, greater shadown, salt mummy)
  • CR 11 - 1 (bone naga)

General Outline

  1. The party travels to the Dead Forest
  2. The party searches for more information on where to find the holiest place in Lindado:
    • The party seeks out the vampire in the Dark Shrine of Evil. He knows:
      • The Dark Shrine of Evil is a training ground, both for necromancers and for lindites.
      • Askomoat is his best guess as to the holiest place.
      • Askomoat regularly spawns undead from the corpses within.
    • The party goes to Shrienhus:
      • The party will be directed to Deadspeaker Ruby Stone.
      • Deadspeaker Ruby Stone will direct the party to Askomoat.
      • She will warn the party that the dead don't stay dead Askomoat.
      • She will mention that the Crypttenders in Askomoat would be able to test whether Nera's faith.
    • The party dives into the Dead Forest without any direction:
      • The party has a 1 in 4 chance to run into a squad of wandering clerics.
      • The clerics will want the party to leave, citing how they have no idea where they are going.
      • Nera or Galadriel can utilize their worship of Lindo in order to have the clerics tell them of Shrienhus.
  3. The party travels to Askomoat.
  4. The party meets up with the Crypttenders:
    • The depths of Askomoat contain the tomb of King Thys De Lange.
    • The spirit of King Thys De Lange cannot rest because his corpse keeps being raised by the latent magic of Askomoat.
    • If she could find a way to put the spirit of King Thys De Lange to rest, that would be proof of Lindo's favor.
    • If the party goes to the Greenflame Crater and dispells the Greenflame, they will lift the breeding curse and remove the constant raising of the dead within Askomoat's walls.
