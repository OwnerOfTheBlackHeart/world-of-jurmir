Description

A scarred hobgoblin in simple ropes with a belt full of wands and potions stands with ready to strike with a vicious smile plastered on her face.

Combat

Verdak chooses her victims carefully, looking to take out casters and other vulnerable, ranged enemies quickly. The higher damage an enemy does, the more highly Verdak will prioritize them. Once she's targeted an enemy, Verdak volleys attack spell after attack spell to kill the enemy as quickly as possible. If she's run out of spells, or the enemy she's targeting is in an extra vulnerable position, Verdak will charge in and attack with her fists.

Verdak is unlikely to fight to the death, and will flee when she hits bloodied, using her escape skills and spells to create distance that her pursuers can't easily make up. Depending on the state of her pursuers, Verdak may come back to strike her enemies with a fresh load of spells.
