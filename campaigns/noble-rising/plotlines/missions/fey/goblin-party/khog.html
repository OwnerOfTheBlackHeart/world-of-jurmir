Description

This well kept hobgoblin woman wears almost gladiatorial looking armor, with only the arms and legs covered while the chest is only protected by a thin layer of magic. Her eyes narrow upon sight and she readies her trident for attack.

Combat

As a follower of Vano, Khog will focus on any drow, or other form of Sithraki, that she sees. Due to her anger as a goblinoid, they will be followed next by humans, human-blooded, and worshipers of the Red King and finally, she will attack any others. If a particular enemy proves itself to be enough of a threat, Khog will ignore her normal priorities and protect her allies.

Khog always starts a fight with her weapon dipped in drow poison.
