Description

A wild and cruel smile haunts the face of this brutal looking bugbear. With lice jumping from her hide armor, she draws a longspear or a bow, ready to fight.

Combat

Starr likes to fight her opponents up close, either wild shaping to fight an enemy adjacent to her or using her longspear to strike over an ally. Regardless, Starr considers her bow her last resort, and will only use it when she can't close the distance in one way or another.

Star is smart, especially for a bugbear, and uses her spells wisely.
