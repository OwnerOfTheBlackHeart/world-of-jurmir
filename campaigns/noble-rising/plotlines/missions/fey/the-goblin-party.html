The Goblin Party

Intro

After the party visits the nymph Anithea, she will speak to Elizabeth and ask her to deal with some goblins that she's seen around. She doesn't believe them to be an immediate threat, but their continued existence could cause problems.

Enemies

  1. Urdes, the Goblin Rogue/Fighter
  2. Starr the (masc female) Bugbear Barbarian/Druid
  3. Verdak the Female Hobgoblin Monk/Wizard
  4. Khog the Female Hobgoblin Blackguard Cleric

General Outline

  1. The party brings Elizabeth to Anithea (022.015).
  2. The first party member to approach Anithea must make a DC 20 Reflex Save or fall on their ass and slide down to the water's edge. From the water's edge, a small mound covered in slippery mud can be seen, but the mound is invisible to those on the other side of it.
  3. Anithea speaks to Elizabeth in Voidspeak:
    • Anithea will refer to Elizabeth as cousin.
    • Anithea calls the Red King Uncle.
    • Anithea has been watching a "slayer party" made up of goblinoids. She doesn't believe that she can handle the party on her own.
    • Anithea is unsure of why the goblin party has formed, but she suspects they might be some kind of advanced force.
    • She's using an enchanted crystal ball that a wizard lost.
  4. Information Anithea will say in common:
    • This is her pond. She will see it become lively again.
    • She would like more space where she could grow a small grove.
    • She will to help restore the soil if given tree and flower seeds for her grove.
    • If minion is there, she'll tell him to go drown in the river, then wake up.
  5. The Party begins searching for the goblin party.
    • Gather Information DC 10 - Hobgoblins are militaristic and all goblins are hostile to non-goblinoids.
    • Gather Information DC 13 - Traders have been disappearing around [[location]].
    • Gather Information DC 15 - A heavily armored hobgoblin was spotted at [[location]], guarding a stone tower in the middle of a road.
    • Gather Information DC 17 - A stone tower has been seen moving along the roads at [[location]], stopping and attacking anyone it comes across.
    • Gather Information DC 20 - A hobgoblin kingdom has formed in the desert north of the Craglorn Mountains, where they wage war with the blue dragons inhabiting the desert.
    • Knowledge (geography) DC 10 and Knowledge (nature) DC 10 - The party would have to be located in a forest away from both elves and logging operations in order to be both undetected and sufficiently fed.
    • Knowledge (geography) DC 12 and Knowledge (nature) DC 13 - The best location for the party would probably be [[location]], but other viable locations include [[location]], [[location]] and [[location]].
    • Knowledge (geography) DC 15, Knowledge (nature) DC 13, and Gather Information DC 15 - Cross referencing viable locations for a goblinoid party to go undetected, movement of local game animals, and various other signs picked up by hunters and explorers, the most likely location for the party would be [[location]].
    • For 280gp, the party can hire a wizard to do cast scrying. If the party goes to Voczec, he will charge 300gp if they don't have Nera and only 200gp if they do.
  6. The party travels to [[location]].
  7. The party fights the goblin party.
  8. The party returns to Anithea (022.015).
