Rats in the Grain House

Completion Note

The party did not act quickly on this quest and another party of slayers solved the problem.

Intro

The Desserr guard is putting a bounty of 200 gold for the killing of, removal of, and prevention of the dire rats infesting storehouses 3 and 5. Upon acceptance of this bounty, slayers may retrieve high dosage rat poison from the Slayer's Guild.

Guard Captain Thunderhammer

Outline

  1. The party gets the quest
  2. The party gets the poison from the guild
  3. The party goes to the storehouses
    • Storehouse 3 has 4 dire rats - 400xp
    • Storehouse 5 has 6 dire rats - 600xp
  4. The party places the poison in each storehouse
  5. The party returns to the Guild for payment
