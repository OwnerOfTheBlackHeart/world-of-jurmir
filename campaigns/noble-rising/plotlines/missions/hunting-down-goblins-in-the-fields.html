Hunting Down Goblins in the Fields

Intro

The party has acquired a quest to go deal with goblins that have been attacking the farms surrounding Desserr. Sent Jaden and family to the kobold clan in the Cracklorn Mountains.

Important NPCs

  • Jaden Taylor, the father of the family, british facial features, reddish skin, blue eyes, brown hair, 5' 10", strongman-esc build, debating trading 2 of his bulls for the youngest Rees boy.
  • Sophia Taylor, the mother of the family, irish facial features, pale skin, blushish-green eyes, red hair, 5' 6", thinly built, freckled
  • Aubrey Taylor, the daughter, takes after her mother, but still recognizable as Jaden's daughter, reddish skin, blue eyes, dark red hair, 5' 3", 12 years old, thinly built, lightly freckled, sorceress

General Outline

  1. The party accepts the quest
  2. The party goes to the farm 2 hexes from Desserr
  3. The party gathers information
    • Talking to the Jaden reveals that he spotted the goblins coming from the north
    • Talking to the Jaden reveals that the goblins tried to steal his cattle
    • Talking to the Aubrey reveals that the goblins made an attempt to steal her
    • Talking to the Sophia reveals that the goblins attack at night
    • Talking to the Aubrey or Sophia reveals that when the goblins attack, howling can be heard
    • Investigating the cattle barn reveals that a cow and a bull have been taken
    • Investigating the cattle barn reveals that several of the farmer's tools are missing
    • Investigating around the cattle barn reveals paw marks
    • Investigating the northern wheat field reveals a path to the west
    • Investigating the western fence reveals a broken section near a forest path
  4. The party tracks down the raiders
  5. The party kills the raiders
  6. The party finds evidence that the raiders aren't alone (here)
    • Give money loot for both fights here
  7. The party finds the raiders' camp
  8. The party kills the raid boss
  9. The party returns the cattle and tools
  10. The party turns in the quest
