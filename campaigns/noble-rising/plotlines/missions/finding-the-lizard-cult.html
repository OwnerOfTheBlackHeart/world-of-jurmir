Finding the Lizard Cult in Desserr

Intro

A cult worshipping the troglodyte god Ulath has formed within the sewers of Desserr. This cult must be found and killed off before it can become a problem to the city as a whole. If the party ignores this problem, the cult will kidnap a noble's child and an adamantine grade slayer will be dispatched.

Optional Start

General Outline

  1. The party accepts the quest from the Slayers' Guild, which has the following information:
    • The section of sewers where the lights have been seen are the eastern sewers near the river
    • The lights have been seen primarily at night, with some at dusk
    • The sewers there are regularly maintained by a catfolk named Kashkerr
  2. If the party visits Kashkerr, they can find out
    • Kashkerr has scales on his arms that he keeps hidden
    • Kashkerr's family recently disappeared
    • Kashkerr's home smells fetid
    • Kashkerr denies anything odd happening while he was working
  3. The party goes into the sewers from dusk till dawn
  4. The party searches the sewers
    • Several sets of footprints can be found in the feces and mud of the sewers (DC 15 spot/search)
    • Some walls have claw marks (DC 20 spot)
    • With enough wandering, the party will find a path of candles that leads to an ambush
    • Following the footprints or claw marks is a DC 20 survival that leads to the "dungeon"
  5. The party finds their way to the "dungeon"
  6. The party fights through the guard
  7. The party finds the worship chamber. If allowed the priest will tell the party that they are standing in the way of the glory of Ulath.
  8. The party fights the bosses and finds the hoard
  9. The party returns to gain the reward
