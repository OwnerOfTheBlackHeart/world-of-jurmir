Finding the Lizard Cult in Desserr

Intro

A cult worshipping the troglodyte god Ulath has formed within the sewers of Desserr. This cult must be found and killed off before it can become a problem to the city as a whole. If the party ignores this problem, the cult will kidnap a noble's child and an adamantine grade slayer will be dispatched.

Optional Start

A 100gp bounty is placed upon an emerald statue of Hrefna Aridottir that has been stolen. The bounty has been put out by Tossarr Burning Cloud. If his home is investigated, the shrine in his garden where the statue was stolen from smells rancid.

Also, this mission can be started by helping Turzk with his search. If the mission is completed without Turzk, then Turzk will meet the party in the sewers. If the party ignores this mission, Turzk may complete it himself or a mission to rescue him may end up on the boards.

GM Notes

They have found a section of sewers that the troglodytes use to travel and used to steal from Tossarr. Galadriel is owed 2 lashings and has forfeited her cut of this mission.

General Outline

  1. The party accepts the quest from the Slayers' Guild, which has the following information:
    • The section of sewers where the lights have been seen are the eastern sewers near the river
    • The lights have been seen primarily at night, with some at dusk
    • The sewers there are regularly maintained by a catfolk named Kashkerr
  2. If the party visits Kashkerr, they can find out
    • Kashkerr has scales on his arms that he keeps hidden
    • Kashkerr's family recently disappeared
    • Kashkerr's home smells fetid
    • Kashkerr denies anything odd happening while he was working
  3. The party goes into the sewers from dusk till dawn
  4. The party searches the sewers
    • Several sets of footprints can be found in the feces and mud of the sewers (DC 15 spot/search)
    • Some walls have claw marks (DC 20 spot)
    • With enough wandering, the party will find a path of candles that leads to an ambush (sewer patrol)
    • Following the footprints or claw marks is a DC 20 survival that leads to the "dungeon"
  5. The party finds their way to the "dungeon"
    • Antechamber/starting room
    • Sitting room
    • Bath room
      • Combat - heavy room
      • This room is filled with thick muck and smells of rot. In the center is a pool of water so brown it could be mistaken for the stained bricks of the walls.
    • Training hall
      • Combat - fight set 1
      • This is a fairly bare bones room with a set of weights, 4 training dummies, and a short agility course.
    • Workshop
      • Combat - fight set 3
      • This room has been made to serve as a sort of smithy. There is a central forge with a casting table and dousing station. Along the walls are 2 work benches which house tools for working with metal.
      • This room contains a set of armorer's artisan’s tools and a set of weaponsmith's artisan’s tools
    • Refectory
      • Combat - fight set 5
      • This room is a single long table with a bench on either side.
    • Larder
      • This room contains 4 cabinets upon which preserved foods sit. All of the meat is salted and larded.
      • This room contains 20lbs of salt, 5lbs of flour, 10lbs of assorted spices (5gp per pound) and 50lbs of meat.
    • Shrine
      • Combat - fight set 4
      • This room contains several non-upholstered wooden chairs that have been set up so to face an iron statue of a troglodyte with a spear crushing a skull in its hand (400 lbs).
    • Barracks
      • Combat - fight set 2
      • This room contains up of 12 filthy bed rolls.
    • Storage/loot room
    • Ritual room
      • Combat - boss fight
      • This large room has a runic circle made of blood in the center, a bookshelf, and a podium. On the bookshelf are 2 books: The Inquiries on Filth and Pleasures of the Cannibal. There is also 14gp worth of misc magical ingredients.
  6. The party fights through the guard
  7. The party finds the worship chamber. If allowed the priest will tell the party that they are standing in the way of the glory of Ulath.
  8. The party fights the bosses and finds the hoard
  9. The party returns to gain the reward
