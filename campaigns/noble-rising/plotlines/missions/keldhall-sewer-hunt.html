Keldhall Sewer Hunt

Intro

After some light investigation in the town of Keldhall, Sin has decided to begin hunting in the sewers. The information that she received was that the town has a cult in the sewers.

General Outline

  1. Sin investigates the sewers:
    • The only signs of goblins are the occasional piece of reused trash.
    • There is a 1 in 10 (1) chance 1d3 goblins will attack Sin during an exploration turn.
    • There is a 1 in 10 (10) chance that Sin will stumble onto the cult.
    • Each turn, Sin can roll a Knowledge (religion) check (DC 17), a gather information check (DC 17) or a track roll to find the cult.
  2. Sin finds the cult base:
    • The entrance is a hole, roughly the size of a mineshaft, carved through the side wall of the sewers and covered over with a rotting wooden door.
    • The entrance tunnel is made of dirt reinforced with hay and clay with a small, open lead pipe through which a stream of sewer water flows.
    • The door is guarded by:
      • CR 3
      • 1 Troglodyte - CR 1
      • 1 Monitor Lizard - CR 2
    • If approached peacefully, the guard will ask if the party has come to join those who have been thrown away.
  3. The party enters the cult base:
    • If entering peacefully, the party will be taken to meet with the priest of the cult. The priest will ask the party why they have come and if they try to convince him that they would like to join, he will make them drink sewer water. Drinkers may become infected with filth fever and are affected by a poison equivalent to Sassone leaf residue.
    • Combats:
      • Boss Room
        • CR 7
        • 1 Ulath Priest - CR 4
        • 2 Heavy Troglodytes - CR 4
      • Room X
        • CR 3
        • 2 Troglodytes - CR 1
        • 5 Commoners - CR 1/8
      • Room X
        • CR 4
        • 2 Monitor Lizards - CR 2
      • Room X
        • CR 1
        • 7 Commoners - CR 1/8
      • Room X
        • CR 3
        • 2 Ulath Worshipers - CR 1
        • 1 Ulath Guard - CR 1
      • Room X
        • CR 4
        • 3 Ulath Abductors - CR 1
        • 1 Ulath Guard - CR 1
