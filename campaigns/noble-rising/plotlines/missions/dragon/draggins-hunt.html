Draggin's Megaraptor Hunt

Intro

Seeking to both test himself and acquire gear to help him improve, Draggin is hunting a mutant megaraptor located near Zo Gedad. With the aid of a caravan of farmers displaced by the fires near Desserr, who are working for the promise of the meat of the kills, Draggin seeks to bring back his chosen trophies from the hunt.

Enemy CRs

  • CR 2 - 12 mutant swindlespitter, fleshraker (MM3 pg. 40)
  • CR 3 - 12 deinonychus
  • CR 8 - 1 mutant megaraptor

General Outline

  1. Draggin travels (with his caravan) to Zo Gedad
  2. Draggin searches for the layer of the mutant megaraptor:
    • Draggin checks at the local bar. The bartender will point Draggin to a hunter who can tell them where the lair is.
    • Draggin checks at the local Slayer's Guild outpost. A quest with the location of the mutant megaraptor is posted for 5,000 gold.
    • Draggin checks with the miners. They will point him to a local hunter that can tell him where the layer is.
  3. Draggin travels to the lair
    • Room 1:
      • 2 mutant swindlespitters
      • 2 deinonychus
    • Room 2:
      • 3 deinonychus
    • Room 3:
      • 4 fleshrakers (MM3 pg. 40)
    • Room 4:
      • Mutant swindlespitter
      • Fleshraker (MM3 pg. 40)
      • 2 deinonychus
    • Room 5: This room is mostly empty with a section of the room dug to be lower than the rest, away from the main thoroughfare of the room. Investigating the depressed section of the room reveals that it is filled with excrement and urine.
    • Room 6:
      • 2 deinonychus
    • Room 7:
      • Mutant swindlespitter
      • Fleshraker (MM3 pg. 40)
      • Deinonychus
    • Room 8:
      • 2 fleshrakers (MM3 pg. 40)
      • 2 deinonychus
    • Room 9: This room is filled with the almost completely eaten corpses of humans and orcs alike, buried within is the loot of the dungeon.
    • Room 10:
      • Mutant megaraptor
