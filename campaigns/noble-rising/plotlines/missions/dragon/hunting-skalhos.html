Hunting Skalhos

Intro

Seeking to acquire the materials of a dragon for Paedir to make into weapons, the party has followed Paedir's research into local dragons to a local black dragon by the name of Skalhos, who lives near Old Castle.

Enemy CRs

  • CR 1/4 - 4 (kobold)
  • CR 1 - 30 (lizardfolk)
  • CR 2 - 16 (shocker lizard)
  • CR 4 - 7 (harpy)
  • CR 5 - 9 (manticore, Lizardfolk Lieutenant)
  • CR 6 - 7 (shambling mound)
  • CR 7 - 1 (Lizardfolk Leader)
  • CR 10 - Zarku
  • CR 11 - 2 (harpy archer)
  • CR 12 - Skalhos

General Outline

  1. The party travels to Old Castle
  2. The party searches for information about Skalhos's lair:
    • The party asks around the Slayers' Guild Outpost
      • Davsire Springwind will tell the party that waking Skalhos is a terrible idea and do what he can to convince them to leave the sleeping dragon as it lay.
      • If the party informs Davsire of their allegiance to Nera, he will write Nera a letter concerning the party's attempt to "wake a sleeping dragon" and send it via magically guided bird.
    • The party asks around the Hungry Bladesman
      • The guards and locals are hesitant to give away the location of Skalhos, suggesting that one shouldn't "wake a sleeping dragon."
      • If the party rolls a DC 25 diplomacy check, they can convince one of the locals to tell them where Skalhos's lair is.
      • If the party rolls a DC 22 gather information check, they can find a ranger that will tell them where Skalhos's lair is, for 500 gold.
    • The party asks Lady Commander Faeleth Dothi
      • Faeleth will reveal that she considers Skalhos to be a security threat, but is in no rush to make Skalhos an immediate threat.
      • If the party rolls a DC 20 diplomacy check, the party can convince Faeleth that they have what it takes to fight a dragon, and she will tell them the location of Skalhos's lair.
    • The party asks Guard Captain Yorrisha Shining Silver
      • Yorrisha will tell the party that she's not going to have Old Castle dissolved because of a group of overconfident slayers who are only interest in themselves and their own glory.
      • If the party can convince Yorrisha that killing Skalhos while she sleeps is the best option for Old Castle (DC 20 diplomacy check), she will reveal the location of Skalhos's lair.

    • Room 15: This delicately tiled, and well kept, room houses what appears to be a private bedroom for a short creature. A small coat rack holds up an equally small set of finely made, black robes, while a nicely kept bed sits next to a stone that, even now, radiates some small amount of warmth. The handful of books scattered about the room are made parchment and leather, filled with writings in draconic that appear to cover rituals, for appeasing both Kyranious and Skalhos, and methods for meditating on the dragon within.
    • Room 16: As one enters this room, the nicely tiled floor quickly gives way to large piles of putrid smelling muck, at the center of each is an indent containing about 3 feet deep of equally putrid water. Within some of the piles lie half-rotted corpses of animals, alongside the crushed bones of prior meals.
