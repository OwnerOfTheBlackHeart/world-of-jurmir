Hunting Skalhos

Intro

Seeking to acquire the materials of a dragon for Paedir to make into weapons, the party has followed Paedir's research into local dragons to a local black dragon by the name of Skalhos, who lives near Old Castle.

Enemy CRs

  • CR 1/2 - 5 (commoner zombie)
  • CR 12 - Skalhos

  2. The party searches for information about Skalhos's lair:

    • Room 5:

    • Room 6:

    • Room 7: Off to the western side of the wall of this room is a stone bathtub filled halfway with acid, above which rests a small faucet. Upon close investigation, the far side of the bathtub has a small bump that extends above the acid line and within the acid rest ten sealed flasks.

      Opening the faucet allows acid to flow into the bathtub, and will take 2 minutes (20 rounds) to fill to the top of the bump. Once acid fills to the top of the bump, the bathtub will begin draining at the same rate the faucet runs. Once drained, the 10 flasks of holy water can be accessed.

      When used within King Thys De Lange's tomb, these flasks instead deal 3d6 damage to undead and 1d6 splash damage to undead.
    • Room 8:
      • 5 Commoner Zombies
    • Room 9: Within this room sit 5 motionless, arachnid statues (all of which appear to have a gold/electrum patina). Compared to the rest of the crypt, this room is the most well kept with nearly perfectly rectangular stonework. As the party approaches, the statues sprint to life.
      • 4 Electrum Horrors (MM2 47)
      • 1 Gold Horrors (MM2 47)
    • Room 10:
      • 3 Spawn of Kyuss (MM2 186)
    • Room 11:
      • 4 Shadows
    • Room 12: Use map "Metal Box Puzzle". In this room is a small, metal box sitting on a cast iron table with four keys. The keys each have a different number of teeth ranging from 3 to 6 while the metal box has four keyholes.

      If a key with not enough teeth is placed into one of the key holes, the box releases a 5d6 damage fireball (DC 14). Once all of the keys are placed into the right slots, the box will open to reveal 10 arrows. These arrows are ghost touch and can crit against incorporeal undead, as well as allow abilities like sneak attack and skirmish to function.

      A key with too many teeth won't go all the way into the lock. The order of the keys is: North 5, South 3, East 4, West 6.
    • Room 13:
      • 4 Wolf Skeletons
    • Room 14:
      • Greater Shadow
    • Room 15:
      • Spawn of Kyuss (MM2 186)
      • 2 Shadows
      • Allip
    • Room 16: At the southwestern corner of the room is a cage of metal mesh holding a gelatinous cube in place.
      • Gelatinous Cube
    • Room 17:
      • Gelatinous Cube
