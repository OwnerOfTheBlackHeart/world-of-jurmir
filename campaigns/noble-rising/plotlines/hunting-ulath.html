Hunting Ulath

Intro

The minor god Ulath, an ascended troglodyte, is beginning to spread cults throughout Taaira-Ruzara. He must be killed to stop the spread of these troublesome cults before they cause too much damage.

Important Events to this Plotline

Attack on Cherryvale

This small settlement in a valley to the northeast of Desserr was destroyed by the time the local knight had gone to collect his taxes. Almost all of the cherry trees were chopped down, the buildings were burnt and no bodies were found.

Attack on Elderfield

One of the oldest farming communities to the east of Desserr hasn't sent their yearly tax and the knight sent to check on why the tax wasn't paid hasn't returned. A heavier scouting force is required to establish what the situation is.

Ulath has burned Elderfield to the ground after killing and eating most of its inhabitants. Some corpses are found with the burnt remains of clothes and flesh, while others appear to have nothing but burnt bone and ash.
