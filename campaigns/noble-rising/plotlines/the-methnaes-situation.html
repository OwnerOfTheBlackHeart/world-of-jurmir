The Methnaes Situation

Intro

Countess Eclavna Ichiir (a drow) of Methnaes has set her sights upon Taaira Ruzara. While the arrival of the Ravens Company has delayed her plans, Countess Ichiir will not be stopped so easily. She plans to use Taaira Ruzara as a means to move slaves to and from the Underdark.

Important Events to this Plotline

War is Authorized

Countess Ichiir has convinced Empress Gilonna to instruct her soldiers to stay out of the coming conflict between Methnaes and Taaira Ruzara. She has placed several diplomatic agents in the court of the empress in order to ensure she stays neutral and out of the conflict.

Declaration of War

Countess Ichiir sends her declaration of war to Count Zesharr.
