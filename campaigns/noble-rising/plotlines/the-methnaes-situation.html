The Methnaes Situation

Intro

Countess/Duchess Eclavna Ichiir (a drow) of Methnaes has set her sights upon Taaira Ruzara. While the arrival of the Ravens Company has delayed her plans, Countess/Duchess Ichiir will not be stopped so easily. She plans to use Taaira Ruzara as a means to move slaves to and from the Underdark.

Important Events to this Plotline

Sariel's Investigation

Sariel Naelio, one of Nera's retainers, is currently looking into the existence of the Methnaes soldiers in Taaira Ruzara. Her plan is to meet with the general of the Taaira Ruzara army, the captain of the Methnaes scouting group, and the captain of the Queen's Army Ravens Company, then determine a further plan of action.

  • Sariel's Investigation

War is Authorized

Countess/Duchess Ichiir has convinced Empress Gilonna to instruct her soldiers to stay out of the coming conflict between Methnaes and Taaira Ruzara. She has placed several diplomatic agents in the court of the empress in order to ensure she stays neutral and out of the conflict.

Declaration of War

Countess/Duchess Ichiir sends her declaration of war to Count/Duke Zesharr.
