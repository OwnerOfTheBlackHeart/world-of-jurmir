Private GM Notes

General Story Notes

  • The court wizard maintains power by making the land ready for a hero, then taking control of the hero after they become the ruler.
  • Use the concept of environment and materials being important to creating magic items. Possibly add in that some items require rituals the party must complete.

Main Threats

  • Red dragon
  • White dragon
  • Orc clan
  • Goblin clans
  • Bandit groups
  • Demon cults
  • At least one undead outbreak
  • Wild animals
  • Enemy soldiers

Side Threats

  • Gnolls tunnelling through the mountains
  • Random animals and people are being grafted
  • Hobgoblin scouts
