Private GM Notes

General Story Notes

  • The court wizard maintains power by making the land ready for a hero, then taking control of the hero after they become the ruler.
    • The court wizard goes by the name Voczec Beliah
    • He is actually minor god by the name of Cezvoc (sez voc)
    • Cezvoc is a god of monsters, conflict and dark magic who is worshiped in an attempt to appease him. He is worshiped with public displays of conflict, with the most sacred ritual being lethal combat against monsters. His symbol is an axe and a spear crossed over each other.
    • A small village worships Cezvoc. The village has a simple dug pit with logs to strengthen the walls that serves as the colosseum that the sacred combat happens in. A rope ladder that is dropped when the rituals are complete is the only means of exiting the colosseum. Due to the colosseum, the village has an abnormal number of warriors.
  • Use the concept of environment and materials being important to creating magic items. Possibly add in that some items require rituals the party must complete.

Noble Mission

Lady Diha Raging Wolf (the tigerfolk) is interested in finding ways to make her "products" more valuable. She has learned from Lord Burning Cloud that someone within the county has been grafting animals and is wondering if Nera could get her in contact with said person. She is will to exchange her subtlety (such as looking for those who are willing to exchange land to have problems solved quietly) and/or a favor.

Diha was planning on seducing Nera in order to get her to a private place to discuss the "job" she has for her.
