Description

Sebas is a bengal tiger who is 3' 2" at the shoulder, 9' 4" long and weighs in at 550 pounds.

Sebas is calm and sassy, enjoying good books more than good company. He is not against mounting catfolk for fun.

Combat

Sebas prefers to have enough warning to cast shield and mage armor upon himself before attacking from the flanks of his enemies. If caught off guard, he will usually cast mage armor before charging into combat.
