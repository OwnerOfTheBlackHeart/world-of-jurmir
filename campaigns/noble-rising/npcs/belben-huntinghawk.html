Combat

Belben is old and more than willing to let others take the glories of battle for themselves. Typically he will sit back and cast buff and healing spells, or he will join the melee, using the Aid Another action. However, if Belben is faced with a foe his allies can't fight effectively, he will cast Divine Favor, Divine Power and Spiritual Weapon before rushing into the fight.
