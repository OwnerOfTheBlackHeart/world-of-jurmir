Truzk, Lizardfolk Fighter

A towering, 8 foot tall, dark green lizardfolk who wears full plate and a shield made of enchanted wood and wields a longsword made of wood with saw-like teeth embedded in the blade.

He is looking to learn about the fates of the ancient lizardfolk empire from which he hales, as he was put into a magical slumber that he only recently awoken from.
