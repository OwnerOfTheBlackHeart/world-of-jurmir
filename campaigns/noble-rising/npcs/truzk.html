Description

A towering, 8 foot tall, dark green lizardfolk who wears full plate and a shield made of enchanted wood and wields a longsword made of wood with saw-like teeth embedded in the blade.

He is looking to learn about the fates of the ancient lizardfolk empire from which he hales, as he was put into a magical slumber that he only recently awoken from.

History and Motivation

He is looking to learn about the fates of the ancient lizardfolk empire from which he hails, as he was put into a magical slumber that he only recently awoken from.

Truzk was born in a time before gods when druidry was the only magic and the lizardfolk ruled all, before even the mammals were born. His armor and weapons were granted to him by the council of shamans before he was sealed away.
