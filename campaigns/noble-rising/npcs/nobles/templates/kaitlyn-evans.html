Kaitlyn Evans

Kaitlyn Evans is a 4' 9" tall, female human with D cup melons, ruddy skin, blue eyes, and ass length blonde hair. She typically wears colorful cotton, wool, canvas, and hemp dresses along with simple jewelry made from wood, cornstalk, and iron. Her favorite is a necklace with a wooden carving of a rabbit. Kaitlyn is friendly and confident.

She works as a tailor and leatherworker.

Compared to the last time that Nera met her, Kaitlyn has become more confident and social. She also has somewhat of the aura of a leader.

Since giving birth to Skrutenicencul, Kaitlyn has become a Red Princess.

Kaitlyn accepted becoming a Red Princess because it was an easy way to earn acceptance and appreciation. That, and Brayden told her that it would make her more confident.
