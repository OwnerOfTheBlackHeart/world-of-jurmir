Countess Vonesti Hunitheplithym

Vonesti is 5' 7" tall elf with shoulder length, brown hair, emerald eyes, pale skin, C cup titties, dark red lips, and a slender form. She normally wears a dark purple silk dress with platinum woven into it so finely that from a distance the whole dress appears to sparkle, with 3 jeweled silver necklaces and several golden rings with rubies and emeralds inlaid into them. When she feels that she might be threatened, Vonesti wears a bluish-purple mithral breastplate with decorative leather pauldrons and skirt, and matching silk robes underneath.

Vonesti is the countess of Verth Di Lokipk.

Countess Vonesti Hunitheplithym is both Ivy Cooke's adoptive name and her disguise that she uses to rule over Verth Di Lokipk without suspicion.
