Description

Juliette is 5' 1" and 125lbs with with short blonde hair, a ponytail that reaches down to her back, blue eyes, pale to tanned skin (depending on how much she's been outside) and soft features, though her stare can occasionally be intense. Juliette has a typical build for a human female, though her arms, legs and belly are muscular.

Juliette's typical armor is a suit of chainmail with a black overshirt (possibly died recently in order to improve her odds of being accepted into Nera's service) with a set of armor plates that protect her neck and shoulders. She carries a longsword on her left hip.

While Juliette doesn't regret her life choices, she does feel some level of guilt for leaving her family and the harm she has done as a bandit. Because of this, Juliette intends to use her service to one of the saviors of Mirstone as a means of atonement. Other than that, Juliette treats people with the respect given to her, though she can be a little on the greedy side.

Juliette particularly enjoys rabbit stew, baklava, melopita (honey pie) and oral (whether giving or receiving).

The one thing she won't do is whore herself out. It's not that she is unwilling to do sexual deeds, she just refuses to do so with a stranger.

Combat

Juliette doesn't charge forward in combat unless ordered to. Normally she will stick to her current charge, typically Nera.

History and Motivation

Juliette was born in Mirstone, growing up in the village and eventually becoming a guard. It didn't take her long, however, to become frustrated with the poor pay and lack of care the local mayor showed to his guard force. After a particularly loud fight with the mayor followed by a similar fight with her parents, Juliette left Mirstone, taking her few belongings and her skills with her.

It didn't take her long to fall in with a group of bandits that preyed on the Cherryvale/Elderfield roads, which she stayed in for roughly 2 years before the bandit group fell for a trap setup by the Desserr guard, which wiped out most of that group. After that, Juliette jumped around with various small bandit groups, always seeming to know just when to need (Phidove believes that she had an informant in the guard telling her when to run).

Juliette made a movement towards Desserr after a particularly narrow escape, with a small group of guards hot on her tail. Once in the city, Juliette managed to evade the guards pursuing her (Phidove confirms that Juliette used Nera's child network to evade capture). After her evasion of the guards, Juliette made her way to the Winking Eye and gained a pardon from, the then, Baroness Nera and entered her employ (Phidove believes that Juliette choose to seek pardon from Nera because of Nera's involvement with saving Mirstone from Ulath and because Nera has a reputation for treating her servants well).
