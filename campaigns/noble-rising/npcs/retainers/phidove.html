Description

Phidove is 4' 9" and 92 lbs in weight with light brown hair, deep green eyes, lightly tanned skin, and soft features for an elf. She keeps her hair about shoulder length and straight. When preparing for combat, she wears leather leggings and her leather armor with a sturdy leather cloak. When acting as a civilian, Phidove wears either simple commoner clothes or a bright set of blue, green and yellow entertainer's clothes.

Phidove prefers two instruments: Fiddle and mandolin.

Combat

Phidove prefers to avoid combat when possible, but when she is forced into combat, Phidove tries to stick to ranged combat. She uses anyone she can to protect her or to disrupt her opponents.

History and Motivation

Phidove was born in the county of Methnaes 131 years ago to a soldier and a farmer. She lived most of her life with her soldier father, eventually joining the military once she was old enough. She was then deployed as a unit with the intent to scout Taaira Ruzara, during which mission she was captured and then indoctrinated by Nera.
