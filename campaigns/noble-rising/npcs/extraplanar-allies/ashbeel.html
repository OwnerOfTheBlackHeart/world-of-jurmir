Description

Ashbeel is a 5' 10" tall female erinyes with ass-length, flowing black hair, chestnut skin, D cup titties, red glowing eyes, large, deep red feathered wings, and a statuesque build. Ashbeel normally wears a leather cropped vest with a mid-calf length skirt, with a set of belts for holding her equipment and no boots or shoes.

Ashbeel is what one might expect of a devil, harsh and confident in action, with a perverse love of inflicting pain on those weaker than herself. However, Ashbeel is also cautious, studying a situation, or person, closely before acting decisively. When forced to think on her feet, Ashbeel is likely to strike fast and hard, seeking to either remove the current problem or force it back long enough to give her space to think and plan.

Combat

Ashbeel prefers to stalk her prey before striking, learning the general attitudes and strengths of her foes. From there she will plan out how to best strike her opponents to ensure their swift destruction. Once engaged with an enemy, Ashbeel will use charm monster to turn the weakest willed amongst her enemies against each other before raining down flaming arrows and possibly diving down to strike with her sword to finish off a foe. Ashbeel may occasionally attempt to drag opponents into the sky and drop them, or use her flight to allow her to bull rush enemies off of elevated locations.
