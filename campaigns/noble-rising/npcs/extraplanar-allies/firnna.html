  • Firnna has a contract with Nera that stipulates that so long as Nera maintains her mortal coil, Nera can summon her at will, that she serves Nera, that she can freely act on the Material Plane, and that every sin she commits on the Material Plane is Nera's sin instead of hers.

Description

Firnna is a 6' 7" tall female erinyes with shoulder length, spiky black hair, mocha skin, DD cup titties, red glowing eyes, large, black feathered wings and a statuesque build. Firnna normally wears an outfit that can best be described as a belt harness, to which all of her equipment is attached when not in use, which does nothing to cover her crotch and breasts. Firnna doesn't wear any shoes or boots, but does paint her nails, both finger and toe, vibrant blues, yellows and reds.

Firnna is surprisingly playful for a devil, at least around Nera, however she is ready at any given moment to unleash hellfire, held back only by the need for an excuse. Being that Firnna doesn't currently have to concern herself with committing sins, she's more than happy to do whatever she wants to, though she has been working towards turning Durn against Sithrak in the hopes to unbind him from Hell.

Combat

Firnna likes to maintain her range during a fight, opening the engagement by unleashing a volley from her staff before using charm monster to turn her enemies against each other while engaging with her bow. If she has a solid reason to, Firnna will swoop in to engage her enemies with her longsword.
