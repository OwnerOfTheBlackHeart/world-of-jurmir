Description

A towering, 8 foot tall, dark green lizardfolk who wears full plate and wields a halberd made of wood with an alchemically hardened and sharpened stone blade.

He is looking to learn about the fates of the ancient lizardfolk empire from which he hales, as he was put into a magical slumber that he only recently awoken from.

Combat

Turzk approaches combat with a thought process of "kill it first", often charging in himself or bracing for an opponent's charge. This does not mean that Turzk is without tactics. He knows that magic users must be targeted first, even if that means engaging with other fighters to open up the battlefield to monks and rogues. If threatened with ranged fire and unable to reach the attacker in a timely manner, Turzk will seek cover that can be used to get close to the ranged attacker.

History and Motivation

He is looking to learn about the fates of the ancient lizardfolk empire from which he hails, as he was put into a magical slumber that he only recently awoken from.

Turzk was born in a time before gods when druidry was the only magic and the lizardfolk ruled all, before even the mammals were born. His armor and weapons were granted to him by the council of shamans before he was sealed away.
