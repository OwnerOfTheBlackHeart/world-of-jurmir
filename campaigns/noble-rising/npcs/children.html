Children of the Party

Cera Black

Cera is a feminine hermaphrodite catfolk-human hybrid with glossy, black hair, purple eyes, tanned skin, cat ears and a tail, both covered in fur matching her hair. Cera's nails are sharper than a normal humans, though only slightly so.

Cera is the child of Nera and Camilia.

Aniera Elizabeth Freebrooke-Omorfia

Aniera is a feminine hermaphrodite half-fiend human with dark red hair, amber eyes with red flecks, sunkissed skin, small, still silvery, metallic looking horns that are yet to have fully grown in and a pair of bat-like, silver wings that are each as wide as she is tall. Aniera's hands have long, sharp, claw-like nails. Aniera is supernaturally cute, despite her demonic features, and is doted on even by those who don't normally dote.

Aniera is the child of Elizabeth and Ilvith.

Guinevere Arlais Freebrooke-Omorfia

Guinevere is the child of Elizabeth and Ilvith.
