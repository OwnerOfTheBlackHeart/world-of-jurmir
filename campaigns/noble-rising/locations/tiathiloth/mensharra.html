Organizations and Monsters

The Slayers' Guild

The Slayers' Guild is an organization responsible for managing a social caste known as "slayers" who are hired to kill monsters, criminals, and enemies of the state or retrieve objects from monsters and/or dungeons. All legitimate slayer jobs in Mensharra go through the Slayers' Guild and only those who are registered guild members may accept such work. While criminal slayers do operate within Mensharra, they are most often members of the Slayers' Guild who aren't picky about their employers.

Due to the prominent economic position of slayers, the selection of which jobs are prioritized is a source of power, as is the ability to "deploy" slayers to cause/redirect problems. This position of power allows the Slayers' Guild to bully local businesses, and some nobles, into actions that benefit the guild or its benefactors. The Slayers' Guild is able to survive without legal repercussion primarily because of their good standing with Count Black.

Within the Slayer's Guild there are several grades of slayer, based off of the usefulness of the material in weapons. The highest rank is adamantine, of which there is currently only 5 active adamantine grade slayers, then is mithral, of which only 23 active mithral grade slayers, then steel, then iron, which is the grade most slayers will reach over their careers, then bronze, copper, stone, and finally the starting rank of wood. There is also 2 honorable ranks, gold, which indicates that a person is a frequent bringer of jobs, and platinum, which only Count Black and the headmaster of the Slayer's Guild hold.

Important People

Count Nesserr Black

Nesserr is a massive panther catfolk standing at 6' 6" and weighing 220 pounds. He is muscular for a catfolk and wears heavy perfume along with brightly colored furs, silks, and jewelry, all in copious amounts.

Nesserr is greedy count who generally hungers for power and wealth, but is known to be kind to his immediate family. However, he does still see them as a possible threat and is always seeking for a way to neutralize the threat they pose without harming them. For this, he has come up with the idea of having his children take over their own lands so that they will not seek to take his throne from him. Outside of his own family, the Count only considers how beneficial people are to him when determining how much he values them.

Count Tenarr Black

The prior count of Mensharra, Tenarr Black achieved his office by saving the city from several different issues during his life. Tenarr is also one of the most important slayers in Mensharra's history, which has lead to the Slayers' Guild having a good relationship with the Count and his family. He is often depicted as being giant for his race with a greatsword that he has passed down to his son.

Tenarr Black died of old age.

Notable Locations

The Slayer's Guildhall

The guildhall of the Slayer's Guild was built from the original castle of the slayer Count Therrass Black, the father of the current Count Black. The castle itself if made of trees grown together by druids from all over the Count's lands and has since been enchanted to be under the effects of a constant ironwood spell. The main complex is surrounded by a 20 foot tall stone wall that is manned by slayers unable to take work. The guildhall does not have a moat around its walls and sits on the eastern wall of Mensharra.

The castle that makes up the Slayer's Guildhall is made up of four distinct sections that form a cross with 2 rear courtyards and a large front courtyard. The rear section of the castle holds the sleeping quarter's for the adamantine and platinum grade guild members as well as the vault where the guild's money and job rewards are stored. The front section is a single, massive room adorned with trophies from slayers past and present, with the biggest being the head of an ancient gold dragon killed by Therrass Black himself. The front room is mainly used for guild gatherings and for job postings, with boards for each grade. The left hand section, from the entrance gate, holds the offices where the administration of the guild happens, from accepting new jobs to answering slayers' questions. The right section, from the entrance gate, holds the barracks where slayers who don't own their own homes live as well as the kitchen and several training and meeting rooms where the slayers can gain new skills or plan for jobs.

The front courtyard of the guildhall is a beautiful grove kept up by the druids within, or hired by, the guild. The courtyard behind the offices is a bush garden with several chairs, benches, and tables that the guild's administrators can use to relax or work comfortably with customer or gold members. The courtyard behind the barracks has 6 long tables where guild members can eat in peace as well as several dummies that guild members can practice on.
