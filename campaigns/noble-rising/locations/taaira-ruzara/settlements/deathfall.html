GM Notes

Deathfall is a mining village operated by the Order of the Red Sun. Outside of the knights, dames, and some of the guards brought in, the village of Deathfall is almost entirely human. Most of the inhabitants are descend from the prior inhabitants of Asokmoat and have a bad tendency to form dark cults in the isolation of the village. For this reason, and also because the village is frequently attacked by orcs, the Order of the Red Sun watches Deathfall carefully.

The Ang (iron) River, which flows from the Mount of Decay, is the the river that Deathfall is built on the southern edge of.

Notable Locations

Fort Ashton (ash ton)

Much like the rest of the town, the outer wall of Fort Ashton was excavated from the surrounding rock, however, the wall has the trademark perfect geometry of dwarven stonework. Within the wall, the fort itself has several longhouse style barracks and a sturdy, wooden command mansion. Fort Ashton belonged to Dame Quilynn Loreweaver before her death.

Man's Best Drink

The inn is a single storey half-timbered building, with a reinforced wooden door and unusually high ceilings. It was once a barracks, and has a collection of arms and armor. Accommodations consist of several hammocks in the common room.

Hunitheplithym House

The Hunitheplithym House sits in a high section of Deathfall, by Fort Ashton, and is protected by a wall of thorns growing around a 10 foot tall iron fence. The house itself is a 4 story building grown from 10 distinct pine trees with fine glass windows and a coat of lavender paint. In the yards between the fence and the house are meticulously kept hedge gardens with flower gardens within them.

Inside the Hunitheplithym are several servants' and guests' quarters, with the guest quarters being appropriate for noble guests. The house has 4 public rooms: A large kitchen with an aging iron oven, a ballroom, a sitting room, and a planning room. There are also 3 offices scattered about the house, Ivy's private office being locked at all times.
