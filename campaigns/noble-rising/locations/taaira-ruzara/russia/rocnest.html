Rocnest, the Flying Town

The town of Rocnest maintains less than 10 rocs, but uses the rocs as high speed cargo haulers to other major settlements in Russia. They train the rocs in the feats Reinforced Wings (RotD, 101), Heavyweight Wings (RotD, 100), and Hover.
