Organizations and Monsters

Followers of the Pearl Cat

The Followers of the Pearl Cat, a cult worshiping a pearl statue of a cat and the ideal of generosity, is based in Silverwood. The cult is relatively small and only a scant few have spread beyond Silverwood.
