Organizations and Monsters

The Slayers' Guild

The Slayers' Guild is an organization responsible for managing a social caste known as "slayers" who are hired to kill monsters, criminals, and enemies of the state or retrieve objects from monsters and/or dungeons. The Slayers' Guild is the largest source of jobs for slayers in Desserr, but has been suffering from a reduction of slayers as of late. It uses the same ranking system as the Slayers' Guild of Mensharra.

Teamsters' and Porters' Economic Alliance of Taaria Ruzara

The Teamsters' and Porters' Economic Alliance of Taaria Ruzara is an organization made up of several guilds across Taaria Ruzara that cover porters and teamsters who handles collecting and distributing transportation jobs, both singular and recurring. The Alliance also serves to ensure that trade routes and roads are maintained and protected, though the guild does not offer any mercenary services to help in this task. For protection, the Alliance typically relies on local guards and slayers.

Notable Peoples

Count Zesharr Black

Zesharr is an old catfolk with black fur that is white around his face, hands, and feet. He stands at 5' 5" and weighs 141 pounds. He keeps his hair shorter and styles it into spikes using specially prepared animal fat and he often smells of a mix of said animal fat and the sweet perfumes he prefers.

Zesharr has a friendly disposition and generally likes to help those around him. Zesharr is the uncle of Count Nesserr Black of Mensharra. Zesharr runs his court later in the day, usually starting between 10 and noon and ending after dark, in order to make his court more convenient to working people.

Nasshurr Diving Hawk

Nasshurr is a tall (6' 1") and muscular catfolk who looks similar to a panther. He keeps his hair shoulder length in a braided ponytail and his fur shiny. He typically wears brown bear furs when he isn't wearing a cheap set of canvas work clothes with a leather apron.

Nasshurr is not the friendliest catfolk on the planet and prefers to be left alone while he works at the forge... unless business is involved. Nasshurr always has time for business.

Fang

Fang is a medium sized dog with a thick golden coat and soft, pointed ears. Unlike his master, Nasshurr, Fang loves people and is more than happy to talk, gossip, and receive all the pets he is allowed in a day. Fang does not wear clothes, but he does have an enchanted collar with a green gem in it that allows Fang to speak and use 2 spectral hands.

Rish

Angry irish halfing. About 2' 6" with crazy red hair and emerald green eyes with perfect teeth and many wrinkles on his forehead. Has a gay, lizardfolk butt buddy with slate scales and a patterning of white that makes him look like a snowy cliff. Rish works at the Horseshoe Tavern during the night as the cook.

Arthur Pendragon

Arthur Pendragon is a 6' 4" tall human with shoulder length brown hair and blue eyes. He has tanned skin and a musculature shows his hard, physical work as a blacksmith. Arthur also has a beer belly.

Arthur is generally friendly and likes to hear tall tales and stories of gods and heroes. While he does recognize that other weapons have their usefulness and place, he believes that swords are the most noble of weapons.

Jorel Sylwynn

Jorel is a powerful elven druid (lvl 12) who serves as the royal gardener. She is 4' 8" tall and athletically built with olive skin and flowing, jet black hair down to her butt. Her face is soft for an elf and her eyes are a deep, emerald green. Jorel wears a yellow and red dress made from fibers she collected working the garden and colored with dyes made from the fallen flowers. She has a lazy tiger as an animal companion.

Jorel believes that Zesharr is a pushover and that the court wizard, Voczec, has too much control over him. While she does appreciate the safety of Zesharr's way, she would like a stronger leader that doesn't waste all their resources helping the unhelpful. Jorel always keeps her promises and doesn't make a promise she has any reason to believe she can't keep. Joel is NE.

Jorel was recruited as the castle gardener by Vesherr Black, the great grandmother of Nera, after they expanded the living quarters of the castle to include the royal guards' barracks, which was originally in the northern courtyard. She is bound by a promise to keep her position so long as a Black sits on the throne.

Leomaer

Leomaer is a 4' 11" tall, male elf with short, brown hair, light green eyes and perpetually sunburnt skin. He is fat for an elf, appearing almost as wide around as a human a foot taller than him. When he is working the stables of No, We're Not A Petting Zoo he typically wears a thick leather shirt with matching pants that is extremely bland (he claims the stables ruin clothes too fast to have nice working garb). When not working, the attire he wears is usually blue, yellow, or purple and made of silk or wool, depending on time of year.

Leomaer tries to be approachable and friendly, but this will usually turn to general annoyance by the end of the day.

Leomaer is a masculine herm.

Reyrona Dathana

Reyrona is a 5' 2" elven woman with porcelain skin, lower back length dark brown hair, light green eyes and a manicured physique. She dresses in bright purples and pinks with the occasional orange, gold, or yellow, and everything she wears is made of the highest quality leathers, furs, and silks. She owns Desserr Tailoring.

Reyrona is known to have a high opinion of herself and judges people by the amount of gold they appear willing to spend. She is a noble in Zesharr's court and is friendly with most people she has seen within the court, but also rude to those who have disrupted court.

Reyrona goes to court every other weekday.

Reyrona owns 5 silk farms, 2 leather farms, and 3 sheep farms. She also has connections with various local sources of fabrics.

Emmie Burton

Emmie is a young, 5' 4" human female with caramel skin, long black hair kept in a tight pony tail, brown eyes and a manicured physique similar to Reyrona's. Emmie typically dresses in yellow, green and red silks during the warmer months or elven quality furs during colder months.

Emmie is kind, gentle, and friendly. She is curious and likes to hear about places she hasn't been or events that wasn't witness to. She is the adopted daughter of Reyrona and works the counter at Desserr Tailoring while Reyrona is at court. When Reyrona is working, Emmie is usually making functional clothing. Emmie is the adopted daughter of Reyrona.

Urmmir Ungert

Urmmir is a 4' 2" male dwarf with gray hair in long braids, dark blue eyes, and light brown skin. He wears undyed linen and wool with several rings, necklaces, and beard jewelry made from iron, silver, and gold. He is fat, the jolly round kind.

Urmmir is suspicious of new customers, especially those without money. However, with enough business his trust can be won over. Urmmir is always happy to look at jems and metals that are brought into his store. He owns Pretty Gold Things.

Urmmir is a masculine herm.

When working in the shop, she wears an undyed linen shirt and pants with sturdy leather apron. Outside of work, she typically wears dresses that are grassy greens with embroidering that looks like flower fields. Prilin doesn't have eyebrows.

Lyla Hunter

Lyla is a 5' 6" human woman with just a touch of fat on her bones. She has dusky skin, brown eyes, black hair in long braids, and soft facial features. She is normally wearing a sky blue, silk dress that closely fits her flat torso while widening out at the hips as well as makeup. When it is cold out, she will add a wolf pelt shawl and a wool cloak. She works as the secretary for the Teamsters' and Porters' Economic Alliance of Taaria Ruzara.

Lyla does want to become a woman, or at least a feminine hermaphrodite, but doesn't have the money to pay for the magics involved to make such a change permanent.

Lyla is a feminine male.

Tossarr Burning Cloud

Tossarr is a 5' tall, plump catfolk with fur patterns like those of a cheetah. He wears pants and a shirt that clearly mark him as male, yet his build and face can make determining his sex difficult. His hair is kept in a greased mullet. Tossarr is known as a capable hunter and his ownership of animal farms which provide meat, milk, and leather.

Tossarr does want to become a true man but doesn't have the money to pay for the magics involved to make such a change permanent.

Tossarr is a feminine male.

Notable Locations

Count Zesharr Black's Castle

In the center of town, on a raised hill is the castle, which is made of round stone. The castle itself is made up of two towers on the southern face and a main building with a northern and southern courtyard. The main building houses the living areas, the dungeons, the court itself, and storage. In the northern courtyard has a small brick building that serves as the kitchen and a main garden that can be looked over by a balcony on the main building. The southern courtyard has several benches, the gatehouse, the entrance to the main building, and plenty of tree cover.

Slayer's Guild HQ

This sun-baked brick building, resembling a church, is roughly 3 stories tall and is made up of a main hall and and a small suite of offices off to the left of the main hall. In the main hall is a small dining area, several empty planning tables, and boards of various grades upon which jobs are posted. The offices, which are accessed via a door next to the bar, is where jobs are turned in at as well as where new jobs are taken to be posted.

Arthur's Swordworks

Owned by the human smith Arthur, this large mud brick building is about the size of a great hall and is filled with swords of all sizes and shapes: Curved, straight, single and double edged, short, long and great, and even more.

Eye of Newt

A simple white mud building that offers a weaved twig front door. Entering the shop reveals a gnome (no one has seen the same gnome twice) sitting in a soft chair with a table and second chair for customers. The shelves are made of magically carved stone that has been enchanted so that only the currently working gnome can remove objects from them.

No, We're Not A Petting Zoo

No, We're Not A Petting Zoo is made up of two parts: The first is a barn containing 10 stables that can handle 2 horses each with a fenced off yard big enough to hold 6 wagons. The second is a large, warehouse like building with several animal stalls, a shop, and a second yard with several ready wooden vehicles, half elven works of art and half human made. The store buys and sells animals, with a few work and riding horses available at all times. It also sells animal feed, land vehicles, wheels, barding, and more animal related goods. Leomaer, the owner, can also be payed to purchase, and provide short term care, for animals he doesn't have in stock.

No, We're Not A Petting Zoo was previously named Horses N' More, but after too many people treated the store's stock as a petting zoo, he changed the name of the shop out of frustration.

Desserr Tailoring

Desserr Tailoring is a long building that has been grown out of 4 trees using elven magics. The building itself could be considered a work of art with a big, mahogany arch around an artistically carved door of the same wood, carved murals on all the walls, and near perfect glass windows. Inside the building is a show room with clothes, ranging from leather farm clothes to extravagant silks, and a beautiful redwood counter. The clothes are grouped into "human quality" and "elven quality". There is also two changing rooms and a back room for fitting and making clothes. A shelf towards the back of the showroom has different fabrics that can be purchased in spools.

Desserr Tailoring is owned by Reyrona Dathana and also worked by Emmie Burton.

Pretty Gold Things

A smaller building made of cut stone, Pretty Gold Things is clearly dwarven. From the precise cut of the stone to the masterfully worked iron sign, the work of a dwarf is obvious. Inside the shop is a stone counter with steel and glass displays filled with gems, rings, necklaces and other forms of jewelry, all which can only be opened from behind the counter. Pretty Gold Things is owned by Urmmir Ungert.

Oops

Oops is a stout building barely sized to fit humans made of mud with a poorly cared for white wash. Inside the building, similarly white washed pillars, with plenty of scaring, support the low roof. In the main room are alchemically reinforced glass shelves filled with ingredients and alchemically created materials, with a scorched wooden counter that Prilin Fockle normally works behind. In the back are three smaller rooms: A mixing room for combining ingredients, a firing room for burning, firing, and baking materials, and a storage room where the base ingredients are kept away from fires and explosions.

Explosive, reactive, and quickly decaying materials are made upon request and are only kept in storage for 24 hours.

Hawk and Fang Goods

Hawk and Fang Goods is a general and adventuring goods store which sells mundane, trade, and basic alchemical goods. It is owned by Nasshurr Diving Hawk, but the front is normally staffed by Fang, who happily greets anyone who walks in the door.

While Hawk and Fang Goods appears as a small shop from the front, it is actually closer to an emporium with a large main room that Fang can easily watch over from the elevated counter that houses the vast majority of the goods. At the back of the main room is a door which leads to the smithy where Nasshurr is typically working and where most of the armor and weapons are located.

Horse Shoe Tavern

The Horse Shoe Tavern is a single story building made of wattle and daub with a round stone main fireplace (viewable from outside the building) and a depressed, sun dried brick building in the back. The wattle and daub section holds the tavern, filled with tables, a comfortable fireplace and a bar opposite the entry door. The brick section holds the kitchen with a cooking fire pit and spit as well as food storage and plenty of food prep tables. The sign is of a horseshoe with a mug within the horseshoe.

Behind the tavern is a group of 6 outhouses, a courtyard, and the Horse Shoe Motel. The motel is 2 stories tall with 10 rooms a floor. Each room has a single bed, a kitchenette centered around an oven that doubles as a heater, an area for a chamber pot, a door with a good lock, a comfortable sitting chair and a table and chair setup like a desk.

Alliance Hall

The main building for the administration of the Teamsters' and Porters' Economic Alliance of Taaria Ruzara in Desserr, the Alliance Hall is a beautiful log great hall with a surrounding wooden plank building. The great hall contains a kitchen, bar, and resting area where members of the Allaince can relax, exchange trading information, make deals, and pick up orders. Two doors, one on each side of the great hall, lead into the plank wood building that wraps around the hall, which contains the offices for the Alliance.

Lyla Hunter works as the secretary for the Allaince Hall.
