Organizations and Monsters

The Holy College of the Deciphered Mystery

The Holy College of the Deciphered Mystery is the school of the arcane that serves as one of the largest attractions in Antys for non-elves. The school's day to day operations are managed by the worshippers of Kriotz, while the large decisions are either brought to the Council of Knowledge and Wisdom or, if they pertain to classes or less than 3 schools of magic, are made by the appropriate head mages.

Notable Peoples

Aelar

Aelar is a 4' 11" tall, male elf. He has shoulder length, black hair that is kept straight with braided bangs that frame his feminine face and his pale green eyes. He typically wears a bright green, long sleeve crop top with tight, bright blue thigh shorts and a bear fur cloak that has been tie died.

Aelar runs Chic Armors.

Notable Locations

The Holy College of the Deciphered Mystery

The Holy College of the Deciphered Mystery is the largest great tree Throbrar (sage crafter), 150 levels, within Antys and the great tree which stands in the center of the grove. The first 20 levels are dedicated to servicing non-mages and new mage onboarding while the next 10 levels are dedicated to the church of Kriotz, the next 30 floors are dedicated to living space and shopping for the students and staff, and the top 15 floors are dedicated to the government of Antys with the rest of the levels being laboratories, classrooms, and libraries of the college.

Yonna works the front desk as secretary. The Holy College of the Deciphered Mystery is the only great tree in which anyone is allowed to access every level of the tree.

When within the Holy College of the Deciphered Mystery, all wizards are required to wear the robes appropriate to their specialization. If a wizard is found to be without college robes, or wearing the wrong school's robes, their spellbook is burned and they are imprisoned for two weeks.
