Barrett Citadel

Barrett Citadel is an old castle placed on the northern side of the Ang River's estuary. The castle is made up of an old, dwarven square stone wall, a 3 story dwarven square stone main building, a 14 ship dock with a dry dock, and a splattering of supporting/servants' buildings.

Barrett Citadel is commanded by Lady Commander Faeleth Dothi.
