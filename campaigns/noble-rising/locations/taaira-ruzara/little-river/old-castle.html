Notable Locations

Barrett Citadel

Barrett Citadel is an old castle placed on the northern side of the Ang River's estuary. The castle is made up of an old, dwarven square stone wall, a 3 story dwarven square stone main building, a 14 ship dock with a dry dock, and a splattering of supporting/servants' buildings.

Barrett Citadel is commanded by Lady Commander Faeleth Dothi.

Barrett Citadel Smithy

Located just off the docks of Barrett Citadel, the smithy is the largest supporting building, made primarily of cob. Only slightly bigger than a normal house, the smithy is open on one side with a single storage room, primarily for holding charcoal and iron, with the smithy and storage racks visible through the open wall. The storage racks are commonly filled with nails, spears, swords and arrowheads.

The Barrett Citadel smithy is worked by Sylvie Fisher.

Baxter Family Supplies

Baxter Family Supplies is a 2 story wattle and daub building covered in standard white plaster with a large window with a counter, through which the displays of various potions and alchemy ingredients can be viewed. The visible room of the building is shallow, but tall.

Baxter Family Supplies is owned and operated by Rhivu Baxter.

Flour, Rice, and Ice General Goods

Built into a wattle and daub building 2 stories tall with 4 front doors, Flour, Rice, and Ice has a simple shop window and a coat of green paint over the plaster. Through the shop window can be seen shelves of food stuffs and stands holding several tools, as well as a single display rack holding up 4 cloaks, which regularly rotate.

Flour, Rice, and Ice General Goods is owned and operated by Orilee Buthenis.

Slayers' Guild Outpost

Made out of a repurposed inn, the Old Castle Slayers' is a larger, 2 story, wattle and daub building painted pale yellow with an iron sign of a sword crossed over a bow hanging above the door. The ground floor contains a small kitchen and a equally small bar, primarily serving the few clients that come to post quests, as well as an office counter for Guild functions. The second floor contains 4 rooms with a 5 person bed each.

The Old Castle Slayer's Guild Outpost not offer quests, only support, such as housing, food and drink, and communication with the Desserr branch.

The Slayers' Guild Outpost is run by Davsire Springwind.
