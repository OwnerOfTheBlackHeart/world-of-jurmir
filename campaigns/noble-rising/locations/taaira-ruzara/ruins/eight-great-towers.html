The Eight Great Towers

  • A circle of 8 towers wizards built while competing with each other
  • The towers stretch 200 to 500 feet tall, with most floors being 10 feet tall
  • The wizards were/are competing to build the tallest tower
  • Mainly researched magically created/augmented materials
  • 3 of the wizards are still alive (2 elves, 1 metallic dragonwrought kobold)
  • The 5 dead wizards are 3 catfolk, 1 tiefling, and 1 orc

The dragonwrought kobold is named Zitt.
