GM Notes

The highest leader of Antys is the Arch Mage of Antys who handles immediate problems as they come up. While the Arch Mage has executive power, legislative power is held by the Council of Knowledge and Wisdom, of which the Arch Mage serves as the chairman. The Council is made up of the Arch Mage, the head mage of each of the eight schools of magic, the high priest from each of the 5 elven churches, and the 4 elder druids.

Aelar

Aelar is a 4' 11" tall, male elf. He has shoulder length, black hair that is kept straight with braided bangs that frame his feminine face and his pale green eyes. He typically wears a bright green, long sleeve crop top with tight, bright blue thigh shorts and a bear fur cloak that has been tie died.

Aelar runs Chic Armors.

Despite his flamboyant appearance and personality, Aelar is actually bisexual.

Notable Locations

Chic Armors

Located on the lower levels of Antys, Chic Armors is painted red and purple with a white washed wooden sign of a chainkini. The display window shows both a chainkini and a suit of sexy full plate armor. The interior of the shop has 18 mannequins whose armor and clothes change as Aelar makes and sells wares with a painted yellow wooden counter. A small storage space exists behind the bar.

Chic Armors sells sexualized and aesthetics driven armor, most of which is enchanted, and enchanted clothing and is owned by Aelar.
