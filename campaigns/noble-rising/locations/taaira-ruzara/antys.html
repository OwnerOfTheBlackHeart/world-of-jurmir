GM Notes

The highest leader of Antys is the Arch Mage of Antys who handles immediate problems as they come up. While the Arch Mage has executive power, legislative power is held by the Council of Knowledge and Wisdom, of which the Arch Mage serves as the chairman. The Council is made up of the Arch Mage, the head mage of each of the eight schools of magic, the high priest from each of the 5 elven churches, and the 4 elder druids.

The lower levels of Antys are available to all who remain civil within Antys, however the upper levels are only available to elves and those who have direct elven ancestry that is willing to testify that they are elven (such as a parent or grandparent). Other exceptions may be applied for, in which case the non-elf is given a pass to be allowed to enter a specific section of the upper trees.

Organizations and Monsters

The Holy College of the Deciphered Mystery

The Holy College of the Deciphered Mystery is the school of the arcane that serves as one of the largest attractions in Antys for non-elves. The school's day to day operations are managed by the worshippers of Kriotz, while the large decisions are either brought to the Council of Knowledge and Wisdom or, if they pertain to classes or less than 3 schools of magic, are made by the appropriate head mages.

Students and instructors of the Holy College of the Deciphered Mystery wear robes whose color is determined by their specialization: purple (Abjuration), yellow (Conjuration), white (Divination), gold (Enchantment), red (Evocation), brown (Illusion), black (Necromancy), blue (Transmutation), and gray (Universalist).
