Titan Towers Productions

Titan Towers Productions is a troupe of bards who perform anything from plays to concerts to dances, all with the added flair of bardic magic. Their ability to use bardic magic to make realistic and fantastic performances, as well as the the low ticket prices, has made Titan Towers Productions highly popular among the populace of Barakuir.

The symbol of Titan Towers Productions is a titan climbing two towers.

Titan Towers Productions is chaotic neutral.

Titan Towers Productions wields both economic and political power, with the ability to build and destroy reputations, plant ideas in the minds of the populace, and other social tricks. For the most part, Titan Towers Productions is more than happy to sustain itself off of "sponsored" performances, however TTP becomes very aggressive when other bard troupes attempt to setup shop within Barakuir, either driving them out or absorbing them into Titan Towers Productions. TTP also doesn't tolerate particularly skilled professional performers that aren't a part of their troupe.
