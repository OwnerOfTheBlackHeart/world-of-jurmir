Howlingcliff Kobold Clan (Kingdom of Howlingcliff)

Location

Built at the lowest peak of the Howling Peaks and appearing as if the entrance was a fortified cave. The fortifications at the front are boulders that have had snipers nests and arrow slots carved into them. The trapped entrance to the Howlingcliff clan burrow is designed to allow the kobolds to harass, with many arrow slits, trap doors, and manually triggered traps, all of which can only be accessed from within the burrow.

Kobolds of the Howlingcliff clan enter through the normal entrance and are allowed through by "commandos" into the clan entrance behind the false horde.

Temperament

The Howlingcliff clan treats itself like a proper government with a standing army.

Greatest Objective

The kobolds of the Howlingcliff clan wish to be accepted as a legitimate nation, and to become truly self-sufficient (have a controlled source of food, etc.).

Interactions

Told of Elderfield (now Blackhide) and Cherryvale, established a deal to slowly grow Howlingcliff's forces. They have given Nera 6 kobolds, 1 runner kobold, and Mex (see roll20).

Important Persons

King Norba

King Norba is a male kobold who rules over the Kingdom of Howlingcliff. He is 2' 7" in height, rusty red in color with glowing yellow eyes. He typically wears layered furs with heavy jewelry. He has been king for ~50 years.

Duke/General Ruple

Duke Ruple is a masculine hermaphrodite kobold who serves as the general of King Norba. He is 2' 6" in height, dark red in color with red eyes. He typically wears a green clothe uniform with 23 metals on it. Duke Ruple has been serving King Norba for over 20 years.

Tactical map is from 19.27 to 28.19

Duchess Kove

Duchess Kove is a feminine hermaphrodite kobold who is in charge of the assignment and training of sorcerers. She is 2' 5" in height, dull red in color with glowing purple eyes. She typically wears a hooded black robe. Duchess Kove has been serving the position for a little under 5 years.

Count Rett

Count Rett is a male kobold who is in charge of the mines. He is 2' 8" in height, dull brown in color with light red eyes. He typically wears undyed, heavy cloth shirt and heavy leather pants. When not working among the mines, Count Rett wears a blue died set of leather clothes with heavy silver jewelry. He has been serving in his position for ~13 years.

Count Snar

Count Snar is a male kobold who is in charge of managing the eggs of the clan and the training of the hatchlings. He is 2' 3" in height, yellow-red in color with dark brown eyes. Count Snar typically wears a poncho made of blackscale lizardfolk hide. He has been serving in his position for a little over 84 years.

Count Gegs

Count Gegs is a male kobold who is in charge of the storage and distribution of food within the clan. He is 2' 8" in height, deep red in color with glowing yellow eyes. Count Gegs typically wears a leather apron and gloves along with heavy copper jewelry. He has been serving in his position for about 43 years.

Bishop Turba

Bishop Turba is a male kobold who serves as the political representation of the church. He is 2' 6" in height, rusty brown in color with blotches of silver scales and deep blue eyes. Bishop Turba typically wears a set of cloth robes that has a patch for each of the 10 true dragon colors sewn into it. He has been serving in his position for almost a year.

Sergeant Rov

Sergeant Rov is a male kobold who serves as the sergeant of the gate guard during the day shift. He is 2' 7" in height, dark brown in color with dull red eyes. Sergeant Rov typically wears heavy leather armor (equivalent to studded leather). He has been serving in his position for a little more than 5 years.

Sergeant Vitt

Sergeant Vitt is a female kobold who serves as the sergeant of the elevator guard during the day shift. She is 2' 1" in height, red clay in color with bright yellow eyes. Sergeant Vitt typically wears heavy leather armor (equivalent to studded leather). She has been serving in her position for about 7 years.
