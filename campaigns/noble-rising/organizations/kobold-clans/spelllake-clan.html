Spelllake Kobold Clan

Location

The Spelllake kobold clan makes their burrow in the hilly forest to the southwest of Spelllake and the Eight Great Towers, built under the largest tree for miles around. The entrance is disguised as a cave beneath the tree, with small view ports carved into the stone that are disguised with an illusion spell. A 6' 6" door that leads to the trap entrance is also covered by an illusion spell.

If the kobolds on watch see a creature they wish to let in, they will mechanically open a section of stone large enough for a wagon to fit through. If they are unsure, but want to send out a representative, the kobold chosen will then exit using the door that leads to the trapped entrance. The kobolds that work the watch holes typically use crossbows and may have a sorcerer on hand.

The trapped entrance of the Spelllake clan is made of magically carved hallways and rooms that descends 20 feet deeper into the stone. Most of the traps in the trapped entrance are magical, however there are several hallways with oil canals and plenty of arrowslits along the walls.

Temperament

The Spelllake clan is in a "competition" with the wizards of the Eight Great Towers, despite the wizards not even being aware of the Spelllake clan. The Spelllake clan, despite their competition, works through disguises to make sure that they go undiscovered by the bigger folk, usually through trade and the manipulation of the laws of the big folk.

Greatest Objective

The powerful blood of the dragon is that which powers the magics of the Spelllake clan and which is the source of their great focus: To draw out the dragon within them through their magics and to make the most of their inner dragon.

Important People

All Watcher Zor

Zor is a 2' 6" tall, female kobold with deep red scales, a ridge of short horns running from between her brow running over the top of her head to the base of her neck, and pale brown eyes. Due to her position, Zor rarely wears clothes, though she occasionally wears her ceremonial robes. For jewelry, Zor wears a silver necklace with 3 rubies and 4 sapphires set in it with a pair of gold rings that each have alexandrite set in them.

Dragonspeaker Idi

Idi is a 2' tall, female kobold with rusty brown scales, horns that form a crown above her brow, and red eyes. She normally wears a set of red robes with gold stitching. Idi doesn't wear any jewelry of her own, randomly selecting jewelry from the clergy's "private" hoard.

Dragonspeaker Idi serves as the highest priest of Kyranious within the Spelllake Clan. She leads the clergy through rituals, handles passing out the clergy's jewelry each day, and, when an emergency or accident happens, manages clerical deployments.

Elder Eltu

Eltu is a 2' 4" tall, aging, male kobold with dull reddish-black scales, a pair of bone ridges that look like small mohawks behind each eye, ending at the back of the head, and cataracted red eyes. Eltu normally wears a set of dark purple and gold robes, and carries a silver plated iron rod that he uses as a cane. Eltu wears a golden ring on each finger, with the following gems embedded in each ring, in order: pink diamond, emerald, aquamarine, garnet, red spinel, moonstone, amber, golden yellow topaz, white opal, and zircon.

Elder Eltu is the eldest sorcerer of the Spelllake Clan and is often serves as a "High Sorcerer" of sorts, helping other sorcerers with ensuring their proposals are accurate and of appropriate quality before they are presented to the All Watcher as well as aiding in the administration of several sorcerous projects at once. Occasionally All Watcher Zor has Eltu make personnel recommendations for projects that involve sorcerers.

Kali would remember back when Eltu's scales had an almost metallic shine to them, with vibrant red tinting.

Elder Gos

Gos is a 2' 8" tall, aging, male kobold with dark brown scales, a set of 8 thagomizers, 4 on each side and not big enough to be used as weapons, on the end of his tail, and bright, watchful yellow eyes. Elder Gos wears an undyed, sleeved apron when he works with inks, but doesn't wear clothes otherwise. Gos gave up on wearing jewelry years ago.

Elder Gos is the eldest eggkeeper in the Spelllake Clan and often serves as a foreman of sorts, keeping track of which eggs are where, which eggs have hatched, when each egg was laid and when was the last time that the fire was fed fuel. Due to his advancing age, Gos rarely tends the eggs himself, instead handling the records of the eggkeepers, and occasionally reporting to All Watcher Zor when needed.

Kali would remember back when Gos's scales were far lighter, an almost sandy color.
