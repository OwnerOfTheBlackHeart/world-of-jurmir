The Followers of the Pearl Cat

Description

The Followers of the Pearl Cat is a cult worshiping a pearl statue of a house cat, which is dedicated to the ideals of charity, kindness and teamwork. The cult consists of two priests, the original brother and sister twins that found the statue, with the rest of the followers being little more than disciples with their own personal interpretations of the Pearl Cat's teachings.

The primary sacrifice to the Pearl Cat is small gems, which the Followers believe the statue eats when it comes to life at night.

Tenants

  • If you show generosity to all who need it, when they no longer need it, they will become generous in turn.
  • One may only look upon the Pearl Cat during the day.
  • The Pearl Cat must always be clean before nightfall.
  • Destroying evil is the job of the strong and powerful, and is to be left to them.
