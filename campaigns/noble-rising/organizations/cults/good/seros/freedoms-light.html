Freedom's Light

Description

Freedom's Light is a cult worshipping the ghaele (eladrin) Seros, devoted to hunting down corruption and throwing off the chains of oppressors. Freedom's Light prefers to operate quietly, working to push good aligned leaders into power, assassinate evil leaders and support and guide well intentioned rebellions. Freedom's Light lacks any form of structure.

Tenants

  • Evil in power is the worst kind of evil.
  • A poorly handled rebellion can make a tyrant worse than the original.
  • A scalpel is often better than a sword.

Relations

Freedom's Light acts on their own most of the time, though they believe that the Followers of the Pearl Cat are wonderful, and support them whenever they can. While Freedom's Light believes that Muriel's Cleansers methods are crude and blunt, they appreciate them and provide support when it won't cause collateral damage.

Seros

Seros is a ghaele (eladrin) who seeks to aid the powers of good and throw off the chains of the oppressors. He uses his cultists to find those who are oppressed and aid them in freeing themselves.

Seros teaches his followers that freeing people from the grasp of tyrants and giving them the tools of self defense is the best way to protect against evil.
