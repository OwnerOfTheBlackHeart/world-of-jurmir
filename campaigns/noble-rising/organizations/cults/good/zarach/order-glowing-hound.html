The Order of the Glowing Hound

Description

The Order of the Glowing Hound is a chivalric order that worships/follows the hound archon Zarach, devoted to the protection of the innocent via protecting vulnerable locations from evil. This can range from setting up fortifications, to physically defending an area, to weeding out corrupting in a local government, etc. Regardless of the form, the Order of the Glowing Hound puts protecting the innocent before destroying evil.

Outside of their main fortress in XXX, where Zarach typically resides, the Order of the Glowing Hound operates in layers:

  • Team
    • 3 to 6 knights/dames
    • Self organizing
    • Handles the protection of a single small settlement or minor location
  • Squad
    • 6 to 12 knights/dames or 2 to 3 teams
    • Lead by an acolyte or sergeant
    • Handles the protection of a town or moderately threatened location
    • When part of a platoon, it is called a section
  • Platoon
    • 6 to 8 teams or half that of squads
    • When the platoon has a mix of squads and teams, each squad is equivalent to 2 teams
    • Lead by a pastor or lieutenant
    • Handles the protection of a city or majorly threatened location
    • Currently the largest formation that the Order of the Glowing Hound can field
  • Company
    • 3 to 5 platoons
    • Lead by a rector or captain
    • Handles the protection of a county or a large, defensive manuever
    • Companies normally only possess 1 platoon of Order of the Glowing Hound troops, with the rest being donated by local polities
    • Companies may also be organized so that every platoon is a mix of Order troops and donated troops
  • Battalion
    • 3 to 5 companies
    • Lead by a deacon or colonel
    • Handles the protection of a duchy
    • Order troops usually serve as leaders and special forces, with the rest of the battalion being donated
  • Legion
    • 3 to 5 battalions
    • Legions must be lead directly by Zarach or another archon
    • Legions handle the defense of entire kingdoms
    • The Order of the Glowing Hound has only ever called one legion into action

The symbol of the Order of the Glowing Hound is a dog with a sun behind it, or in some groups a glowing aura emanating from it. Depictions range from the dog pointing like a pointer to the dog howling while standing on 2 legs.

Tenants

  • If the innocent are protected, evil will starve.
  • The life of an innocent is the most valuable in the world.
  • Hunting evil means that you cannot defend against it.

Relations

The Order of the Glowing Hound is willing to work with almost anyone in the defense of the innocent, even evil organizations. However, the Order of the Glowing Hound believes Muriel's Cleansers focus on hunting down evil over protecting the innocent is causing more harm than good.

Zarach

Zarach is an improved hound archon who seeks to protect the innocent from danger. He uses his cultists to protect locations he believes will come under attack by dark forces.

Zarach teaches his followers to protect the innocent over hunting down evil, as actively hunting down evil only serves to leave the innocent vulnerable to the forces of evil.

Zarach dislikes Muriel, believing that her overly aggressive approach to dealing with evil does more harm than good and takes valuable guardians away from those who need them most.
