Seekers of the Better Path

Description

The Seekers of the Better Path is a cult worshiping the avoral (guardinal) Jeha, who guides the Seekers in a quest to find a better way to keep the Void locked shut, preferably without sacrifices. The cult is organized into 3 roles: Speaker, Keeper, and Seeker. The Speakers communicate with Jeha and distributes her commands among the Seekers of the Better Path. The Keepers have two roles: To manage and protect the Grand Atheneum, and to protect writings and artifacts as they are brought to the Grand Atheneum. The Seekers hunt down new information that can further the goals of the Seekers of the Better Path, as well as research and study options new and old.

The Seekers of the Better Path view the power and influence of Sithrak to be a problem and aim to create a reality in which he is not needed.

The Seekers have a strong fascination with pre-god magics.

Tenants

  • Hunting one evil gives another time to grow, but removing the evil before you allows you to hasten to your task.
  • Rare is the complete evil. If it can be done, remove only the evil and nothing more.
  • Seeking the Better Path is the true goal, and must be relentlessly pursued.

Relations

PLACEHOLDER

Jeha

Jeha is an avoral (guardinal) who seeks to find a better way to keep the Void locked shut, preferably a method that doesn't require any form of sacrifices. She hopes that by doing this, she can lessen Sithrak's power and influence among mortals and gods, and to be able to rid the world of his creations. Jeha uses her cultists to seek answers on pre-god magics as much as to "surgically remove the evil," as Jeha puts it.

Jeha teaches her followers to focus on their main task, but to remove of evil should it become convenient.
