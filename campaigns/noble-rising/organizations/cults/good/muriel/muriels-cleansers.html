Muriel's Cleansers

Description

Muriel's Cleansers is a cult worshipping the astral deva Muriel, whom they worship for her pure goodness. Outside of the two "branches," the Stalkers and the Wave, cult lacks any internal structure. The Stalkers, which make up the majority of the cult, are the hunters of the Cleansers that actively seek out and destroy evil, either on their own or in small teams. The Wave serves Muriel directly, serving both as her personal army and as keepers of her shrines and safe houses.

Tenants

  • Evil is not to be allowed to grow.
  • Inaction is as bad as the actions the inaction allowed.
  • So long as their is evil, evil must be hunted.
  • The best way to protect the innocent is to destroy that which threatens them.

Relations

Muriel's Cleansers have a strong dislike of Order of the Glowing Hound because they are stagnant and unwilling to do what needs to be done. Muriel's Cleansers appreciates the information that agents of Freedom's Light passes to them, though they don't always trust Freedom's Light's intentions.

Muriel

Muriel is an astral deva (angel) who seeks the destruction of evil. She uses her cultists to relentlessly hunt evil.

Muriel teaches her followers that it is better to destroy evil before it can harm the innocent than to try and protect all who are innocent from evil.

Muriel has a strong dislike of Zarach, and views the archon as being stagnant and unwilling to do what needs to be done.
