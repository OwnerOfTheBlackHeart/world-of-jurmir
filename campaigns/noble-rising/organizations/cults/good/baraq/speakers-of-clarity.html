The Speakers of Clarity

Description

The Speakers of Clarity is a cult worshiping the couatl Baraq, devoted to turning those who are evil to good. The cult likes to serve as examples of positive behavior and to offer guidance to those straddling the line between good and evil.

If a speaker is unable to turn someone they believe to be redeemable, they will report it back to the Speakers to either dispatch another member or to have the issue handled by a couatl.

Tenants

  • Evil redeemed can do great good.
  • Evil is to be destroyed only if necessary to save good and innocence.
  • Do not hate evil, understand it.

Relations

While the Speakers of Clarity find most other "good" organizations to be misguided and dangerous, they get along with the Followers of the Pearl Cat and the Seekers of the Better Path.

The Speakers think the Followers of the Pearl Cat's devotion to charity is commendable.

The Speakers believe that the goals of the Seekers of the Better Path serve to help reduce the number of temptations that draw people towards evil.

Baraq

Baraq is a couatl who seeks to turn those who are under the influence of evil over to the side of good. He uses his cultists to serve as examples of positive behavior, to find and report on those who are redeemable, and to offer guidance to those who straddle the line between good and evil.

Baraq teaches his followers to only destroy evil when necessary to protect good and innocence, otherwise, evil is to be taught how to be good.
