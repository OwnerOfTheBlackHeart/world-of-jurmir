The Liberation Guild

Description

The Liberation Guild is a cult worshipping the cornugon Vano, whom they worship for his promises of freedom from Sithrak. The Liberation Guild has three distinct branches: The Priests who publicly serve Zoltan and offer liberation from one's sins, which is secretly a method to recruit new members, the Rangers who actively hunt down the forces of Sithrak and kill them, and the Wizards who seek to find a way to kill Sithrak.

Tenants

  • To be captured is equal to being killed.
  • Retreat only in full, never break a battle line.
  • A powerful foe must never be allowed to escape.

Relations

The Liberation Guild will work with other cults when it is convenient, but they generally prefer to work on their own. The exception to this is the Fallenfeather Sisterhood, who Liberation Guild members will work with whenever possible.

Vano

Alignment: Lawful Evil

Domains: War, Trickery

Portfolio: Cold war, unseen battles, deception, plotting, and transformation

Symbol: A triangular shield with a pair of leathery wings

Favored Weapon: Bident or trident

Colors: Black and dull green

Vano is a cornugon (horned devil) who was summoned to the Material Plane 100 years ago by an unknown force, however when he was summoned, he ended up locked in a ruined fortress. A few years ago, Vano was able to escape and has begun a personal crusade against the forces of Sithrak. Vano uses his cultists to hunt down and kill Sithraki forces, such as clerics and true drow.

Vano teaches his servants that destroying Sithrak will allow the forces of Hell to march onto the Material Plane and allow them become demons/devils themselves.

Vano is willing to work with goblinoids, usually promising them the chance to become demons/devils if they serve him in life.

Vano is happy to exchange knowledge on the path of the blackguard in exchange for service, which seems to never stop being extended.
