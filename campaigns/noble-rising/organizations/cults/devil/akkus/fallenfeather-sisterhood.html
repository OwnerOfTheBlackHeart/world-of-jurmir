Description

The Fallenfeather Sisterhood is a cult worshipping the true erinyes Akkus, devoted to protecting her from the forces that would take her to Hell and working towards making Akkus a god. All members of the Fallenfeather Sisterhood are female, with the rare feminine hermaphrodite being called a breeder.

The Fallenfeather Sisterhood is a highly structured military organization with room for the most capable to ascend the ranks. The Sisterhood is broken into four branches: The Furies, who serve as the assassins of Akkus, the Kides, who are in charge of protecting Akkus and the Sisterhood, the Katas, who are the spies and seekers of knowledge, and the Fones, who guide the worship of Akkus. Interbranch competition is almost unheard of within the Sisterhood, but intrabranch competition is a constant, with the need to be acknowledged by superiors causing individuals to challenge themselves and seek out new ways to please their goddess, even forming small teams to accomplish certain tasks.

Tenants

  • The commands of Akkus are law.
  • Knowledge is power, Victory is honor, Structure is life.
  • Being unintentionally discovered is as deadly as any blade, and must be dealt with quickly.
  • The interests of Akkus come first, your superiors second, yours third and your inferiors last.
  • Merit, obedience and loyalty are to be rewarded.

Relations

The Fallenfeather Sisterhood is not above working with other cults, evil, good or somewhere in between, however, their general paranoia and mission means that the Sisterhood only works with cults of similar secrecy and avoids, or destroys, cults that draw attention to themselves.

While the Fallenfeather Sisterhood considers the Servants of Perfect Beauty to be a chaotic mess, they maintain a positive relationship with them and will offer shelter and assistance to the Servants, though they expect these boons to be available when the Sisterhood needs them.

The Fallenfeather Sisterhood works closely with Liberation Guild agents whenever they can.

The Fallenfeather Sisterhood consider the rare opportunity to work with the Second Order a pleasure.
