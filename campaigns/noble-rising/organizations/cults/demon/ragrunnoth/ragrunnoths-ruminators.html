Ragrunnoth's Ruminators

Description

Ragrunnoth's Ruminators is a cult worshipping the glabrezu Ragrunnoth, whom they worship for his promises of power and wealth. The cult lacks any form of structure without internal clout coming from one's ability to control others and/or their personal wealth.

Tenants

  • Power and wealth are finite.
  • "Earning" power and wealth is slow, but taking it is quick.
  • Anything is legal if you never get caught.

Relations

Ragrunnoth's Ruminators view all others as tools, thought they disdain the Calmers of the Flame Lord, viewing both the cult and Kugmoxon as lazy and unworthy of their wealth and power.

Ragrunnoth

Ragrunnoth is a glabrezu who is worshiped for his promises of power and wealth. He uses his cultists to create perpetual schemes that slowly drain communities of their wealth and bring about economic ruin.

Ragrunnoth teaches that power and wealth are finite resources that must be taken, as "earning" them takes too long to be meaningful.
