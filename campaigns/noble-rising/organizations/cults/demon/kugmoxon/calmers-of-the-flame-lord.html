The Calmers of the Flame Lord

Description

The Calmers of the Flame Lord is a cult worshiping the balor Kugmoxon, who worship him for his strength and power. The cult lacks structure, with status coming from the personal power of the individual and/or from the amount of "donations" they have made to Kugmoxon.

Tenants

  • Freedom is the path to power.
  • If it grants you more power, it is right.
  • Kugmoxon is not to be allowed to go hungry.

Relations

The Calmers of the Flame Lord care not of the existence of other cults.

Kugmoxon

Kugmoxon is a balor who is worshiped for his strength and his power. He uses his cultists to constantly feed himself and live in luxury, though he is willing to give up all of it if it would keep him out of Hell. Kugmoxon is lazy, but he's always up for a "good old fashioned killing."

Kugmoxon teaches that power is the path to freedom and that anything that grants power makes right.
