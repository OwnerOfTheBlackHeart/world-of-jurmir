The Servants of Perfect Beauty

Description

The Servants of Perfect Beauty is a cult worshipping the succubus Ilvith, whom they worship for her beauty and charisma. The cult lacks any real structure, with social and political clout coming from ones beauty, how often they've personally served Ilvith and how much Ilvith has used them as a canvas for her art. The Servants live in the XXX of XXX, trying to pass themselves off as simply a settlement of beautiful people.

Tenants

  • One should strive to be more beautiful than the rest of the room, not the most beautiful in the room.
  • A silver tongue and a platinum form can subdue nations, but a sword can only subdue the weak.

Relations

The Servants of Perfect Beauty are generally fairly isolationistic, preferring to hide the fact that they are a cult, but they may silently ally themselves with those they view as being attractive and avoiding those they view as being unappealing.

While the Servants of Perfect Beauty consider the Fallenfeather Sisterhood to have a collective rod up their asses, but to be useful friends to have. Thus, the Servants are more than happy to offer members of the Fallenfeather Sisterhood shelter and assistance. It doesn't hurt that the sisters of the Fallenfeather Sisterhood are typically beautiful.

Ilvith

Picture of Ilvith

Ilvith is a succubus who is worshipped for her beauty and charisma. She uses her cultists as servants, playthings, and art pieces.

Ilvith teaches that beauty is what is to be strived for most and that her worshipers should do anything they can to become more beautiful.

Ilvith wants to impregnate Akkus with her child(ren).

When the party first meets Ilvith, she'll be looking to have them aid her in making someone fall in love with her strong enough that they will kill their family for her. She intends to make this person her first cleric.
