The Blackguard

The Blackguard is an chivalric order in service to Baroness Nera. The members of the Blackguard are made up of retainers of Nera whom she has entrusted with the noble title of Knight/Dame.

The Blackguard technically only have authority and power within The Barony of Rivers Plains.
