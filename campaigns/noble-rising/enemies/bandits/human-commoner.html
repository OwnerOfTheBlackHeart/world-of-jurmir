Description

This human commoner wears armor that looks like a thick quilt. They hold a single weapon that they are trained in the use of and cower in place, hoping to return to their family.

Combat

The human commoner doesn't willingly fight on their own. If there is no source of "encouragement" the commoner will run at their first chance or otherwise surrender. If they have encouragement, they will attack in groups in an attempt to overcome their opponent.
