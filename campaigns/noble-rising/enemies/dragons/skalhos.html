Description

Skalhos is a black dragon who stands at about 7 feet at the should, when walking, and roughly 32 feet from nose to tail. She is a normal black dragon in appearance, with a skull-like face framed by horns. Skalhos's wings are heavily scared.

While Skalhos moves almost silently and carefully, she does so without any form of grace.

Combat

Skalhos prefers to start any fight hidden within shallow waters, striking the enemy from behind, using either her breath weapon or a volley of natural attacks, whichever can do the most damage in the opening assault. After the initial ambush, Skalhos will attempt to draw the enemy deeper into any number of prepared ambushes or traps, designed to draw out the suffering of the enemies for as long as possible.
