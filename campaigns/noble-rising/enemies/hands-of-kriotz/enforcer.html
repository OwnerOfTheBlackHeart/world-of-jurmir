Description

This elven warrior of Kriotz wears a purple chainshirt with pale blue leathers beneath it. The elf carries a heavy wooden shield that they can quickly dispose of if needed.

Combat

The Kriotz enforcer would rather attack from a range, trying to mitigate the opportunity for closer range spells to be used against them. If needed, or if a priest is nearby, the enforcer will move to melee range.
