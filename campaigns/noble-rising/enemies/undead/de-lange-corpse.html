Description

Standing at a hair under 6' tall, the corpse of King Thys De Lange gives little evidence as to what the king looked like in life, except for the obvious signs of a healthy diet. The half-rotted zombie is clad in a crude, by modern standards, steel breastplate that's been hastily thrown over his vibrant purple court clothes. The corpse wields a ceremonial ranseur with a flame that never burns out.

Combat

Lacking any sense of tactics, the corpse of King Thys De Lange charges upon seeing living creatures, attacking with its ranseur whenever it can, otherwise slamming its opponents.
