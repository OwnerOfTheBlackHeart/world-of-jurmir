Description

This creature has the body and eyes of a wolf, but it has short, bristly fur that fails to hide the armor like scales that coat this wolf's body. The chest and legs of this wolf are slightly large than a normal wolf's.

Combat

A favorite tactic is to send a few individuals against the foe’s front while the rest of the pack circles and attacks from the flanks or rear.
