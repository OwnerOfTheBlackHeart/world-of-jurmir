Description

This immense gray wolf seems as big as a horse. It has fiery eyes and a thick coat of black fur. It is about 9 feet long and 850 pounds.

Combat

Mutated dire wolves are more aggressive than normal dire wolves and far more combative. They start a fight by charging against the smallest creature on the battlefield and attacking with their bite. Mutated dire wolves have no sense of self preservation in combat and ignore damage.
