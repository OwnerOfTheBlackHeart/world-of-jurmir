Description

This hobgoblin stands proud in his well kept banded main. With a shining greatsword, this hobgoblin challenges you to fight him.

Combat

Hobgoblin Lieutenant lead their forces through their own strength. While almost incapable of stealth within their armor, hobgoblin Lieutenant understand the importance of an ambush and will often act as bait, or wait in a dug in position until the fight has started.

Hobgoblin Lieutenant love to lead their troops from the front, forming a barrier against the enemy that allows their troops to manuever.
