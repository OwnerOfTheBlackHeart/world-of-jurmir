The Amof are a race common to forests and deserts, but are capable of living anywhere. Primarily carnivores of small animals and large insects, Amof are capable of eating just about anything, including berries, bark, and carrion.

The Amof resemble humanoid coyotes, ranging from 2' 5" to 2' 8" in height and weigh from 15 to 44 pounds. Amof also have a long and fluffy tail, generally around 16 inches when adults. Amof have long legs for their size that allow them to move fast for their size.

Call the taller species biggers.

Racial Traits

  • Strength -2, Dexterity +2, Wisdom +2
  • Size Small
    • +1 bonus to Armor Class, +1 bonus on attack rolls, +4 bonus on Hide checks, -4 penalty on grapple checks, lifting and carrying limits ¾ those of Medium characters.
  • A amof’s base land speed is 30 feet
  • Darkvision out to 60 feet
  • Low-Light Vision
  • Scent
  • Amof have a bite natural attack that deals 1d3 damage
  • +1 natural armor bonus
  • Automatic Languages: Amof, Common (if city dweller)
  • Bonus Languages: Common, Draconic, Elven, Goblin, Orc, Gnoll
