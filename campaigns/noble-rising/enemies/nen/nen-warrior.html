Description

Nen warriors stand between 8 and 12 feet and weight between 800 and 1,200 pound, the typical nen warrior resembles a cockroach standing on its back pair of legs. All nen extremities have 3 digits (2 fingers and a thumb for hands, 2 forward toes and 1 backward toe for feet) and are typically thick and sturdy. Nen exoskeletons are both thick and durable, allowing the nen to shrug off attacks as if they were wearing full body armor and survive in otherwise hostile conditions (such as deserts).

Combat

Nen warriors are highly aggressive and go after the largest target in their sight. They usually prefer to carry as large of weapons as they can get their claws on. When they don't have a weapon, they will gladly rip someone apart with their claws.

When nen warriors are away from their hives, they may be more calm or more aggressive than normal.

Nen Warriors As Characters

  • +14 str, -2 dex, +12 con, -4 int (min 3)
  • Large size. -1 penalty to Armor Class, -1 penalty on attack rolls, -4 penalty on Hide checks, +4 bonus on grapple checks, lifting and carrying limits double those of Medium characters.
  • Space/Reach: 10 feet/10 feet.
  • A nen warrior's base land speed is 30 feet with a climb speed of 15ft.
  • Darkvision out to 60 feet, Tremorsense out to 30 feet.
  • Racial Hit Dice: A nen warrior begins with six levels of monstrous humanoid, which provide 6d8 Hit Dice, a base attack bonus of +6, and base saving throw bonuses of Fort +2, Ref +5, and Will +5.
  • Racial Skills: A nen warrior's monstrous humanoid levels give it skill points equal to 9 × (2 + Int modifier, minimum 1). Its class skills are Intimidate, Jump, Listen, Climb, and Spot. Nen warriors have a +8 racial bonus on Climb and a +4 racial bonus on Jump, Spot, and Listen checks.
  • Racial Feats: A nen warrior’s monstrous humanoid levels give it three feats.
  • Weapon Proficiency: A nen warrior is proficient with the greataxe and all simple weapons.
  • +8 natural armor bonus.
  • Natural Weapons: Claws (1d6).
  • Automatic Languages: Nen, Undercommon. Bonus Languages: Common, Abyssal, Giant, Ignan, Infernal, Terran.
  • Favored Class: Barbarian.
