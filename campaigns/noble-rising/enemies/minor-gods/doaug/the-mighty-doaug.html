Tenants

  • The Mighty Doaug is always referred to as the Mighty Doaug. His name is the Mighty Doaug, not Doaug, the Mighty Doaug.
  • The ultimate honor is to be faced with the chance to either challenge the Mighty Doaug to battle or to find and aid the Mighty Doaug in a monster hunt.
  • Spells that empower or protect are the favored spells of the Mighty Doaug, while spells that weaken or impede an enemy are viewed as ruining the challenge.
  • If a follower of the Mighty Doaug runs into someone with a curse, they must challenge them to a fight. If the cursed person fights with valor, the follower must remove the curse.
  • A follower of the Mighty Doaug is not allowed to enter someone elses' personal fight. They are allowed to buff the party they support.
  • A follower of the Mighty Doaug must honor makers of weapons and armor, as they create the vessels that focus martial strength.
  • A follower of the Mighty Doaug must bury or cremate foes that fought with honor and valor.
  • A follower of the Mighty Doaug must always honor their word, but they are under no expectation to be honorable outside of battle.

Description

The Mighty Doaug is a male german shepard with dark blue scales instead of fur, a vicious looking maw, clawed front paws, and rippling muscle. He is always near, or wielding, his +3 bastard sword, Nabela.

History

The Mighty Doaug was born as a typical blue, half-dragon german shepard. He wandered the world for a while, fighting anything that caught his eye and conquering settlements he encountered. After having conquered 5 villages through intimidation alone, Kyranious granted him minor godhood under her as a god of conquest, honorable combat and war.

When the Mighty Doaug conquers a settlement, he does nothing to defend it or to maintain power over it. He conquers solely for the purpose of proving his strength.

Combat

The Mighty Doaug dives into fights without any concern for himself, or those around him. Once in a fight, he attacks with his bastard sword and claws, stopping to cast buffing magic if he feels threatened. The Mighty Doaug may also stop fighting and start buffing if he is present to witness incredible valor.
