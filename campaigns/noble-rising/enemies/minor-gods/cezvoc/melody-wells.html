Description

Melody is a 6' tall, female aasimar with neatly kept, mid-back length flowing pearly hair, lightly tanned skin, E (DD) cup titties, golden eyes and a motherly figure. Melody normally wears a colorful dress with a set of vivid scarves that she can move about as she performs, along with shining gold jewelry studded with cheap, but shiny, gems. Her combat garb is a beaten, but well made, mithral breastplate paired with a flaming, two-handed flail and a high draw weight longbow.

Melody's normal appearance is partially illusion. When the illusion is dropped (such as when Melody rages), Melody has a wild and violent look to her with reddish-black eyes, unkempt hair and a powerful, muscled build.

Melody has an attractiveness of 9.

History and Motivation

Melody was named for the fact that her cries upon birth were melodious and beautiful, which made her parent believe her to be a gift from Lindo. As Melody grew older, her parents pushed her to study and practice among the local lindite church, which she excelled at; However, the whole time Melody struggled with a terrible temper.

When she had hit puberty, Melody calmed down, gaining far better control of her emotions. Her parents and the lindites thanked Lindo for her sudden maturity. The reality, however, was that Melody had found the local Cezvoc cult and joined them, letting out her vicious nature in the battle pit and fully falling, using her bardic magic to conceal the evidence of her fall.
