Description

Yeshana is 5' 9" tall catfolk-aberration dragon hybrid with matte black fur, ass-length black hair in a braided pony-tail with a tuft of black hair covering from her shoulders to the tops of her D-cup breasts (which hides a second, large mouth), four arms, and golden-yellow eyes with cat-like slit pupils.

Yeshana typically wears her "princess dress," a small, silver dress with red accents that mostly covers her waist with deep cleavage that goes to just above the knees with a split in the sides. For jewelry, Yeshana wears a silver choker with a ruby in the center, a silver signet ring, 2 silver bracelets, and a pair of silver rings with inset red spinel. When she goes to court, Yeshana wears a golden chain over her chest mouth.
