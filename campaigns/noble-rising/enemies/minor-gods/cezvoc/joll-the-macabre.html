Description

Joll is a 6' 3" tall, male orc with shaggy, black hair, deep green skin, red eyes and a light build. While Joll normally wears his plain full plate armor when traveling, while working a settlement to form a new cult, he will either wear his Cezvoc worship robes or whatever clothes the locals have adorned him with.

History and Motivation

Joll started life as one of the tribal raiding orcs scatter throughout the land; However, the recent work to "civilize" wild orcs caused Joll's clan to be captured and sent to the mines. Weakened by a lack of food, water and sleep, and held in place by chains, Joll was visited in his dreams by Cezvoc, who offered to grant him power if he was to "finish" the monster plague, namely by spreading it over the whole world.

Joll accepted this power and used it to escape his imprisonment. Now, he wanders for settlement to settlement, planting Cezvoc cults in order to power up his god and gather enough worshippers to perform the final ritual to spread the monster plague over the world.
