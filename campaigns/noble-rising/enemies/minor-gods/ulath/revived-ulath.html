Disease (Su): The filth built up on the claws of Ulath posses a magically empowered version of filth fever. When an opponent takes damage from Ulath's claws, they must roll a DC 23 fortitude save or become infected with an enhanced version of filth fever that deals 1d4 dex and 1d4 con damage and takes immediate effect, dealing the damage upon infection. The DC of the enhanced filth fever is 23 and the enhanced version of filth fever appears as a necromany effect to detect magic. Dispelling the infection turns it into a standard case of the filth fever.

A creature already infected by filth fever, or an enhanced version of filth fever, is unaffected by this ability.

God of Destruction (Su): Being that Ulath is a god of destruction, his attacks and spells bypass 10 damage reduction, hardness, or energy resistance, regardless of if they normally would.

Godly Description

Domains: Pestilence (SC 278), Destruction

Portfolio: Lizards, lizardkin, cannibalism, fetid locations

Symbol: The skull of a lizard

Colors: Green and Black

Favored Weapon: Spear

Gaining the favor of Ulath provides the following boons: +2 con, +2 str, -2 dex, -2 int, immunity to non-magical diseases and poisons, and +2 natural armor.

Description

Ulath appears as a 8 foot tall, 14 foot long tauric combination of a troglodyte and a massive monitor lizard, with the pale blue scales he had in life and a mix of oily slime and refuse covering his body. The troglodyte torso of Ulath appears almost bursting with muscle, while his lightly glowing yellow eyes bare down on all with a cruel and hateful gaze.
