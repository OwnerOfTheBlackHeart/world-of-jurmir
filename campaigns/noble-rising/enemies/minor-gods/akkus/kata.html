Description

Typically armed only with a few sets of clothes, a bag of coins, and a mission, katas serve as the spies and knowledge seekers of the Fallenfeather Sisterhood. While katas occasionally put on their war gear, they only do so because they are working with a fury to clear out a "noisy" cult or because the appearance will grant them some form of advantage.

Combat

Kata's only fight when they have some form of "tank" to draw attention away from them. Once the "tank" has began drawing fire, the kata(s) will surround a single opponent and begin unleashing as many sneak attacks as they can.
