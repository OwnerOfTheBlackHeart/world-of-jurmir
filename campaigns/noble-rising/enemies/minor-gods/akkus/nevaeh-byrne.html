Combat

While Nevaeh is capable of fighting an opponent head on, she prefers not to. Instead, Nevaeh likes to catch opponents off guard, when possible, but is more than happy to settle for any other position that grants her sneak attack. As such, Nevaeh is hesitant to go into a fight alone, preferring to have at least 2 other warriors who she can flank with.

If forced into a fight where she can't engage in melee with sneak attacks, Nevaeh will keep her distance and bombard her opponent with arrows for as long as she can.

History and Motivation

Born the daughter of bandits that preyed exclusively on the wealthy, Nevaeh grew up on the move, constantly running from one set of guards to the next set of assassins. While this was simply life to the young Nevaeh, she slowly realized who her parents truly were as she grew older, and she began to grow resentful of their greed and how it never seemed to bring her anything but trouble. Still, Nevaeh saw the usefulness of the skills her parents held and what they could do for her.

Nevaeh's spite of her parents came to a head when they took her out on her first raid. She had been told to use her beauty to convince a wealthy merchant to hire her on as a caravan guard, then let her parents in during her shift to steal what they could take and disappear into the night. Nevaeh had no problem convincing the merchant to hire her, for a fair bit more than she was worth, and travelled with the caravan for 2 days. During her time with the caravan, Nevaeh found herself enjoying the structure and peace of the job, as well as her own pay. When her parents finally did come in the night, rather than grabbing what she could and running off with them, Nevaeh sounded the alarm, killing her mother while her mother was frozen in shock and let her father die to a hail of arrows.

After the caravan job, Nevaeh accepted her pay and decided to journey for a short while, looking to understand the world her parents had hid from her. Once she had a year's travel under her belt, Nevaeh decided to join the Queen's Army, though she only stayed one term.

After her time with the Queen's Army, Nevaeh found herself desiring a strange mix of freedom and structure, a place where she could advance by her own merit, at her own pace, but with a clear sense of purpose and chain of command. She search for a while, eventually coming upon a small group of Fallenfeather sisters.

After a night of drinking with the sisters, Nevaeh was offered a chance to help them clear out a cult, for an equal share of the loot, which she graciously accepted. The raid went successfully, and Nevaeh found herself being given several thinly veiled tests. With each completed test, Nevaeh learned more and more about the Sisterhood, until her final test was given: To stand before Akkus and be judged.

Nevaeh found Akkus and the Fallenfeather Sisterhood offered the curious mix of freedom and structure that Nevaeh desired and, without hesitation, joined the Fallenfeather Sisterhood when Akkus found her worthy.
