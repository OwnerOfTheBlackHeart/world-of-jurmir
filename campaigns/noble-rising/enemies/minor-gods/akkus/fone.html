Description

Clad in beautiful full plate, this elven woman looks like a well accomplished slayer or an experienced noble, but the cruel looking morningstar on her hip hints to the darker truth.

Combat

The leaders of the Fallenfeather Sisterhood, the fones command their sisters in battle, creating organized formations and battle plans, making good use of their magic and the skills of their underlings. As they prefer to lead by example, most fones will charge into the fray once they have a buff spell, or two, place on themselves.

Fones generally avoid ranged combat, though they will use their heavy crossbows to attack past enemy battle lines.
