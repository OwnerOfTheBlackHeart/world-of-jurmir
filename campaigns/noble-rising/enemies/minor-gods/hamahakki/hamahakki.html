Hamahakki, Goddess of Venomous Spiders

Alignment: Neutral Evil

Domains: Death, Spider (SC 280)

Portfolio: Venomous spiders, ambushes, lethal poison, painful death, hopelessness

Symbol: A thin bodied spider with long legs evenly spread out

Favored Weapon: Bite or dual daggers

Colors: Shadowy/misty gray and pale blue

Tenants

  • A painless death is a mercy, and should only be granted to those that have earned it.
  • Prey with hope is a failed hunt, even if they are killed.
  • An unprepared hunter is the prey.
  • To kill is natural, to hunt is glorious.
  • A follower of Hamahakki may not deal non-lethal damage, that is what paralytic poisons are for.

Description

Hamahakki appears as a 29 foot long spider made of swirling, gray mist with a thin, 5 foot tall torso, 24 eyes that glow a pale blue, and unnaturally long (even for her size) chelicerae/mandibles. Even when her fangs are retracted into her chelicerae, the tips of the obsidian black fangs can be seen, dripping venom. The mere presence of Hamahakki, or her avatar, causes dense spider webs to form rapidly.

History

Little is known about Hamahakki's life before becoming a minor god, as the goddess does not care to tell stories. The first records of Hamahakki's presence indicate that other minor gods managed to force her out of prior home, a point of convergence between the upperdark and underdark that was filled with a perpetual mist, which caused Hamahakki to move into the caves within the lands of Methnaes.

The arrival of Hamahakki immediately proved problematic for Methnaes, as her cults quickly began hunting and killing, and her presences brought with it a rise in venomous spiders. Initially believing them to simply be half-mad mages practicing dark magics, Methnaes simply killed or sacrificed any followers of Hamahakki they found. However, as the problem became progressively worse, Methnaes realized it was the work of a minor god and began hunting down Hamahakki and her cults.

These hunts were initially successful, managing to kill or capture most of the weaker Hamahakki cults. The hunts only left the stronger, more capable cults behind, causing Methnaes to reorient its focus to killing Hamahakki, which caused Hamahakki to grow stronger as she fed upon her hunters. Realizing their mistake, Methnaes swapped back to trying to eliminate Hamahakki's cults while they look for a way to trap or kill Hamahakki.

At the current moment Methnaes continues to actively hunt Hamahakki's cults, though they are only able to take them out fast enough prevent the situation from worsening, and the fact Hamahakki so easily attracts drow to her worship is not making the situation any easier.
