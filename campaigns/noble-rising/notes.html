Letter to Nera

Dearest Nera,
 I may tell you that you are only to help your great uncle Zesharr, but I am sending you there for a reason greater than just helping my hapless uncle. It is time that you gain your own lands, as your brothers before you have. I would advise that you take your time to grow in strength, influence, and trust before taking your great uncle's throne. And don't worry about rightful heirs, for he has never taken a bride or even a concubine.

 Also, go knowing that I love you and have every expectation that you will live. If you feel the need to seek advice in the art of slaying, your grandfather's old friend Reth'Mailynath at the Slayers' Guild will be more than willing to give you what instruction you may need.

Love,
Dad
