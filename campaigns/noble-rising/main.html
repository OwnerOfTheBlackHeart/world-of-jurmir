This is the Story of a Noble Rising

Nera Black was once just a young count's daughter, barely old enough to be considered an adult. However, one day her father gave her the chance to go and take her own land in the way her grandfather had. Now she journeys to her great uncle's land with the intent to cleanse it and assert herself as the heir to said land.

Nera has recruited three outstanding individuals to aid in her quest: The aasimar favored soul of Lindo Laidee Galadriel Lamaria, the elven scout Sariel Naelio, and the dwarven duskblade Paedir Gryffonhammer.

Along their travels, the party picked up the draconic gnome wizard Kali Torvold
