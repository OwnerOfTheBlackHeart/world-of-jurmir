Skellyball Powers

DR Boost (EX)

Taking this power increases Skellyball's DR/bludgeoning by 2 or DR/- by 1.

Breath Attack (SU)

Skellyball gains a breath attack, usable once per day. This attack deals one of the following damage types (chosen upon taking this power): Fire, cold, acid, or lightning that is a 30ft cone or 60ft line (also chosen upon taking this power). It deals a number of d6s equal to half of Skellyball's hit dice, rounding up.

When taken again, one of four options may be chosen from: Increase uses (1/day to 3/day to 1d4 rounds recharge), add a new damage type that can be selected instead of the original when using this power, gain the ability to use either a cone or line on use, or increase damage dice (d6 to d8 to d10).

Bigger Claws (EX)

When this power is taken, increase claw damage as if Skellyball is a number of sizes larger than medium equal to the number of levels in this power (large at Bigger Claws 1, huge at Bigger Claws 2, etc.).

AC Bonus (EX)

Skellyball gains +2 natural armor.

Teleport (SU)

Skellyball gains the ability to teleport 30 ft as a standard action or half that as a move action. Taking this power again increases the standard action teleport range by 30ft per level (30ft to 60ft, etc.) to a maximum to of 120ft.

Spellstore (SP)

Skellyball gains the ability to store a single spell as per a spell storing weapon, which can be used upon command. Taking this power again grants one of two effects (up to 3 times): Skellyball can store another spell (up to 3) or gain the ability to store a 6th level spell.

Incorporeal (SU)

Skellyball can become incorporeal as per a ghost.

Energy Immunity (SU)

Skellyball gains energy immunity to one of the following energy types: Fire, acid, lightning or sonic. Taking this power can be taken a second time, selecting another energy type.

Chuck Range (SU)

Skellyball gains an extra 30ft of throw range while in his ball form. This power can be taken until his throw range is 120 feet.

Turn Resistance (EX)

Skellyball gains +2 turn resistance per level of this power, up to +8 turn resistance.

Spell Resistance (EX)

Skellyball gains spell resistance equal to 6 + its hit dice. Taking this power gain increases the spell resistance to 9 + its hit dice and up to 11 + its hit dice upon taking this power a 3rd time.

Touch Through (SP)

Can cast touch spells and abilities through Skellyball. The initial range at which this power can be used is 30 feet and increases 30 feet per level of this power with a maximum range of 120 feet from the dread necromancer.

Corporeal Anchor (SU)

When Skellyball has Corporeal Anchor, any Incorporeal within 5 feet of Skellyball must succeed a wisdom based will save (10 + 1/2 hit dice + wisdom modifier) or become corporeal until they leave Skellyball's Corporeal Anchor range. The range of Corporeal Anchor increases by 5 feet per level of this power with a maximum range of 15 feet.
