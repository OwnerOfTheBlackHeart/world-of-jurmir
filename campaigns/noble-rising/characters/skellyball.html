Description

Skellyball is a 25lbs, 5' 6" tall, male, human skeleton that can compress into a ball roughly the size of a basketball. When controlled by a dread necromancer a skellyball gains black fire in their eyes. Skellyball has had 2 t-rex arms grafted onto him as well as the lower jaw of a skum.

Combat

Skellyball fights as ordered to, which is typically by chattering up to his target and clawing them with his razor sharp claws. Skellyball gains a new power every odd level, and every 4th level he gains 3 ability score points to distribute as wanted.

Skellyball can wear light armor, a light shield, and a light weapon before he can no longer roll into a ball.

History and Motivation

Skellyball is a skeleton that was turned into a magic item by a lost necromancer, which was then stolen from the necromancer by a polar bear. He is motivated by whomever currently has him commanded. In the hands of a dread necromancer, he becomes something more than just a normal skeleton.
