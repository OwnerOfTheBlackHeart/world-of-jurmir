History and Motivation

Zarsra was born to house Strisek (bones of adamantine) in the underdark beneath Desserr to a low born mother. Being that her mother had no real standing, neither did Zarsra, which caused her to join the military of her house in the hopes of gaining training and influence. Her hopes were dashed within only a decade, when house Kenlyl (sworn to the blade) killed or enslaved every member of her house, herself included. Due to her low birth and low status, Zarsra was given a choice: Go to the surface as a slave or be sacrificed to the seals of the void.

Zarsra's first concern currently is to survive. She currently feels no binding to any group or philosophy as it currently stands. However, in time she would like to return home to cast judgement down on house Kenlyl for slaying her house.
