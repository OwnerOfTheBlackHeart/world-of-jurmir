Description

Lessiarra is a 19' tall and 3.2 ton female "tigerfolk" with "F (DDD)" cup titties, a sturdy build and a state perpetual happiness. Lessiarra normally wears a light brown-gray garment resembling a shirt with elbow length sleeves and exposed shoulders with a matching skirt that drapes down to her mid-shins, though the whole outfit looks like a dress unless one looks closely. When travelling through terrain that might damage the skirt, she'll swap out the skirt for leather shorts or pants, depending on the season.

Combat

Lessiarra generally doesn't like to fight, as it makes her worry about all the "cute little babies," but when she has to fight, Lessiarra softens up enemy units with her siege bow before charging in with whatever vaguely club like object she can find. While in melee, she likes to keep her distance and use her natural reach to limit the retaliation ability of her opponents, or to reach past her allies and crush oncoming enemies.

If too much harm comes to her "cute little babies," Lessiarra will suddenly become silent and stoic, focusing entirely on destroying whatever threatens her allies. This stoic state is mechanically her barbarian rage.

History and Motivation

Born as a tribal catfolk among the forests of what is now Rivers Pass, Lessiarra was among the many of her kin who protected her tribe from wild animals and other tribes, what some might call barbarians.

After the reorganization of Taaira Ruzara into a duchy, Lessiarra's tribe came into conflict with the, then under construction, New Castle. After Countess Diha Raging Wolf pulled together a force of her own soldiers and hired mercenaries, she was able to force Lessiarra's tribe to the negotiation table. As part of the agreement, Lessiarra was turned over to Diha.

After acquiring Lessiarra, Diha took her to Zigni, who was able to bring the 7' 9" tall catfolk up to 14 feet tall. While Diha was pleased with this, she wanted Lessiarra to be even more of a giant and tasked a small cabal of alchemists and wizards to accomplish the task. Needless to say, they succeeded.

With Lessiarra brought to a size that Diha was happy with, she left the massive catfolk at Blackhide to wait for Nera.

A "casualty" of her tribe's "loss," Lessiarra is currently at a loss of what to do with herself, though she does worry that if she doesn't do what the "victors" want of her that her tribe may be in danger.
