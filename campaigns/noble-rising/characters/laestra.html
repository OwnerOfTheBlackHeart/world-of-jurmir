Description

Laestra is a 5' 5" tall drow female with straight, ankle length, silver hair, B cup titties, coal black skin, a muscular build (for a drow), and a hateful gaze coming from two pure white eyes. In areas that see more sun, such as Laestra's face, the color lightens, up to a chalky gray.

Laestra is currently clad in a sleeved, sack cloth dress which covers all but her feet, which are wrapped in cloth "shoes," and head. It is clear that this dress is uncomfortable.

History and Motivation

Laestra was born in Methnaes among the population of "surface drow." For the most part, she lived a relatively normal childhood, commanding over males and being taught the ins and outs of being a matron of her home. She even joined the Methnaes army and participated in the early Hamahakki hunts.

Laestra ended up being the only survivor of a Hamahakki hunt, having been close enough to the entrance of the cave the hunt was in to have not been caught in Hamahakki's webs. At first, this hunt had terrified Laestra, but with every hunt there after, of Hamahakki or her cults, Laestra felt drawn further towards the goddess of venomous spiders.

On the day Laestra's tour of duty was complete, she walked out to the cave where she encountered Hamahakki and called out to the goddess, who responded. Laestra's memory of the response, and several hours afterwards, are blurry at best, but she walked out of that cave a cleric devoted to Hamahakki.

Freshly anointed as a possessor of the voice of Hamahakki, Laestra marched to the nearest settlement to the cave and settled down, forming a new cult that managed to avoid notice for nearly 30 years. However, it was found and hunted. While Laestra managed to avoid being caught for almost another decade, the Methnaes army caught up to her and managed to capture her.

Thanks to the deal that Nera made with Duchess Ichiir, Laestra was spared from the headsman's block, and instead sent to serve Nera as a slave. Laestra believes that she was chosen to do so in the hopes that her presence in Taaira Ruzara will cause an outbreak of worshipers that would, in turn, draw Hamahakki away from Methnaes.
