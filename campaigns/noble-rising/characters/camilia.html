She has a perfect mix of muscle and fat that make her appear as almost the perfect female form with three claw mark scars: one on her upper right arm, near the shoulder, at the base of her back, and on her left leg. Camilia is unfamiliar with most common phrases, needing some translation to equivalent gnollish phrases.

Combat

Camilia generally prefers to stay out of combat, using her bow to pick off farther targets and tag escaping enemies. When all the enemies are in melee with her allies she will move to support her allies by either buffing her allies with spells or buffing herself and joining the melee.

History and Motivation

Camilia was born in the gnoll lands beyond the southern mountains of Taaira-Ruzara, where gnolls are the dominant race that have forced humans and elves to live in small, nomadic tribes. In the meetings with other tribes, friendly gnolls, and elvish tribes Camilia heard of lands beyond the mountains that promised wealth and power to those that sought them and quickly found herself leaving her tribe to find a better life for herself, tribe be damned.

Back in her tribe, Camilia was being groomed and instructed to become a druid shaman. After gaining her druidic powers, she served as a midwife and a hunter until she left.

After arriving in Taaira-Ruzara, she wandered from settlement to settlement until she reached Desserr, where she met Nera and was quickly recruited via "generosity." Camilia is now part of a blood pact with Nera.

Camilia has formed a blood pact, a magical contract, with Nera where in exchange for food, housing, and guidance around the civilized world, Camilia will guard and aid Nera. Should Nera wish it, she may command Camilia to mate.

Camilia partially came to Taaira-Ruzara to seek a better life for herself, but also to seek out the hero of the next cycle. The elven elders told her of the cycles in many stories of the rising of activity in monsters and disreputable sorts shortly before a hero rises to slay them and take control of the land, creating peace and stability. One also told Camilia that she would find this cycle's hero in a person whose true nature was masked in black robes.
