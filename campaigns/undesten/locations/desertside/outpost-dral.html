Outpost Dral

Built from wood and standstone, this outpost is well built for its size and made in the Ming style hollow fort. A large, reinforced door is present on the north of the outpost while the southern side has a personnel door. Four towers provide the fort the ability to quickly spot and react to incoming enemies.

While the outpost is unlikely to survive siege weapons, it is more than sturdy enough to handle the normal threats expected within the deep deserts.
