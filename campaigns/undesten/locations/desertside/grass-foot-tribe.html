Standing as a massive contradiction to the goodly races' perceptions of gnolls, the Grass Foot tribe's "camp" has been setup in the ruins of an old catfolk city. The sturdy, old stone foundations and homes carved into trees have been taken over by gnolls who have started building their own buildings among them, including crude mud huts, stacked stone houses, plank wood homes and even semi-permanent tents. The massive buildup of gnolls has lead to a boom city the size of any thriving metropolis, made up not only of gnolls looking to trade their raid loot, but also evil races such as the drow and hobgoblins, who find it difficult to trade with the goodly races, and even some catfolk tribes.

The Grass Foot tribe marks themselves by painting their feet green with a grass based dye.

Organizations and Monsters

The Albnk

The Albnk are a group of catfolk bankers that embedded themselves into the Grass Foot tribe's city early on and serve as loansharks and coinchangers within the city. They are particularly brutal in stamping out any competition, though their true motivation at the end of the day is to get their money, and they are willing to go to any lengths to do so.

Most of the Albnk's power comes from the fact that nearly every business owes them something, and the chance to get a chunk of debt excused for a simple task is usually quite convincing to the debtors. When the Albnk need to exert force, they can rely on a number of thugs and mercenaries all too happy to take the money ask no questions beyond what needs done.

Notable Locations

Chucklenutz (Bar and Grille)

Built from unplastered wooden planks painted in bright blues and greens, the inside of Chucklenutz is filled with tables and booths, with a bar for ordering at the far end of the room. Several windows allow light in while glass canisters filled with glowflies provide the barest amount of light during the night. Most of the food sold is meat entrees and sandwiches, served with strong grog, fine wine or locally made mead.

Owned and operated by Chucklenutz.

Gobless Goods

Gobless Goods is a medium sized building with a stone foundation and damaged wooden walls, using large hide tent sheets pinned in place with bone nails to cover the roof and holes. A counter is placed on the street side that allows a customer to see the goods without being able to grab them, behind which Heero works.

Gobless Goods is owned and operated by Heero.

Scavenger's Paradise

Scavenger's Paradise is a stone smithy with a leather tent extension that makes it almost a warehouse in size. Within the extension, which has flaps facing the road, are piles of weapons and armor pieces strewn about with no care for display or ease of grabbing. In the smithy side of the building, various slaves work to create custom orders, repair damaged goods or forge new pieces from scrap metal. Fire Rib watches over the warehouse side with a careful eye from her metal tube and wool sheet chair.

Scavenger's Paradise is owned and operated by Fire Rib.

Glowy Shit

A smaller, mud hut style building, Glowy Shit is purposefully bland from the outside. Inside the hut is a small customer space separated from the rest of the shop by a counter with large, folding wooden shutters that are hooked to the ceiling when the shop is open. The shop space holds small, semi-sorted piles of magical items layed out on the ground and, sometimes, on tables made of fired clay.

Glowy Shit is owned and operated by Dreaming Dogwood.

The Soft Pillow

The Soft Pillow is a carved tree house that has been expanded with a dried mud addition and a plank wood addition, with a courtyard and a leather awning over a sitting area, which includes a pool and bath. The inside of the building starts with the carved tree house, in which a basic counter serves to allow Shiva to deal with customers, a set of stairs lead to Shiva's private quarters and a door leads into the rentable rooms. The rooms range from public rooms with beds that sleep up to 5 and are popular among merchants looking to share news, to private suites that can be rented by day or by week.

The Soft Pillow is owned and operated by Shiva Nasirian.

The Coal Bin

Made from imported, carved stone bricks with murals of the Underdark etched into the walls, the Coal Bin is as ominous of a building as it is intriguing. Entering in the mushroom wood door leads into a dark, and surprisingly cool, room filled with a handful of cages hold drow. A finely made wooden pedestal stands at the back of the room with a large book sitting on it, the slave registry.

The Coal Bin sells only pureblood drow sourced from nearby underdark cities.

The Coal Bin is run by Pharavi Enzu.
