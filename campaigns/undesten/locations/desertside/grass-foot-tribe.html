Organizations and Monsters

Important People

Chief Elder Oak

Chief Elder Oak is a large, old female gnoll with long, mangled mane and cold, cruel eyes. She normally wears a suit of leather and bones that is as much armor as it is ceremonial, and carries a heavy battleaxe.

Oak is an 11th level ranger with a dire hyena animal companion.

Bonekeeper Ribwort

Bonekeeper Ribwort is a smaller, female gnoll with stark white fur, blood red eyes and no discernable main. The bonekeeper wears a suit of wooden armor with a light wooden shield, a vicious morningstar and two necklaces around her neck. One of the necklaces is a small ball of fire carved from a sapphire, the other being a drawn bow.

Ribwort is a 7th level cleric of Kriotz and Hrefna.
