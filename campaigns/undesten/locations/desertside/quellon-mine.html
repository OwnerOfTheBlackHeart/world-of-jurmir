Quellon Mine

The Quellon Mine is a complex containing three smaller mines: An iron mine, a quartz mine and a quarry. The three mines share a single administrative center and a fortified workers' barracks, with a large contingent of hired guards that guard the mines themselves as well as regularly patrol the surrounding area to keep the gnoll population down.

Organizations and Monsters

Rands the Rust Monster

Rands is a four and a half foot long rust monster that "works" at the Quellon Mine. Trained by the mining crew, Rands performs two main jobs: Scaring off gnolls and sniffing out new veins of ore. He is also used to dispose of that ore which is too impure to be useful. He is normally kept in a wooden kennel and is known to be gregarious.

Notable Locations

Admin Building

The Admin Building is hardly impressive when compared to the Workers' Barracks, being a five room, single floor wooden building with a stone foundation. The main room serves as the offices for the mines, with the guard captain's office and the holding cells attached directly. The two remaining rooms serve as flexible spaces that are used for whatever they are needed for when they are needed, usually storage.

Worker Barracks

A large, sturdy stone building with tower-like protrusions that offer firing ports, the workers' barracks is a keep on its own. The first floor of the building is the storage, while the second floor houses the workers themselves and the third is home to the kitchen. The inside of the barracks is mostly unadorned aside form the dining area that sees near constant use, serving also as the cards room and social area.
