Rooms, Building 1

This building appears to be the home of someone important to the lab's operation. While simple in construction, it is rather spacious, especially given the city surrounding it.

Living Room

The main room of this house, a general living space with a dining table and a pair of couches facing each other with a coffee table between them. A normal hallway leading to the back yard and a storage area are also part of this room.

Bedroom

Easily the fanciest room of the house, the bedroom is dominated by the silken sheeted, king sized bed. Aside from the bed, the furniture is finely made, but simple. At night, Maral sleeps here.

This mid-sized room is mostly empty, save for a large device that dominates the room's center.
