Temple of Khon

Temple to a local minor god of local megafauna that was attacked by gnolls. The minor god was killed and his worshipers eaten.

The temple is a stepped pyramid surrounded by the ruins of houses and outbuildings made of rammed earth.

The current inhabitants of the Temple of Khon are a group of hobgoblins trying to breed elven and catfolk tieflings in order to create a new vessel and revive the minor god Khon, under their control as a god of war.

Khon

Khon, whose body is that of a huge humanoid deinotherium, an ancient elephant-like creature with downward facing tusks and a short, thick trunk, that also carries a pair of demonic wings. The reborn Khon, using the distraction and damage the party has caused, has killed off the hobgoblins in this room.

Completing the Temple of Khon starts the Hobgod questline.
