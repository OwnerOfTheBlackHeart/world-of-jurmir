Temple of Khon

Temple to a local minor god that was attacked by gnolls. The minor god was killed and his worshipers eaten.

The temple is a stepped pyramid surrounded by the ruins of houses and outbuildings made of rammed earth.

The current inhabitants of the Temple of Khon are a group of hobgoblins trying to breed elven and catfolk tieflings in order to create a new vessel and revive the minor god Khon, under their control as a god of war.
