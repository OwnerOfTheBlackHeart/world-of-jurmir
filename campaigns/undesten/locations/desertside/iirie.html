A large, ramshackle city made of hastily constructed buildings ranging from rough hewn log to plank to dried clay brick. Within the dirt-mound backed log outer walls of the city is a maze of winding, narrow streets and scattered markets with the river running through the center of the city being the only semi-straight travel way. A number of drawbridges allow passage over the Lostbass River during the day when they have operators. The city entirely lacks churches or temples.

Docks are present on either side of the city as well as a small boat dock within the city proper.

There is no slave market or open slave trade in Iirie, however slaves are not a rarity within Iirie. Those familiar with Iirie, or who have hired a local dracoman to aid them (5gp per day), gain a +2 to checks to find a slave for purchase. Those extremely familiar with Iirie, or who have hired a veteran dracoman (25gp per day), gain a +4 bonus instead.

Iirie is secretly a massive Red King cult, pulling humans from all over to converge and prepare for the death of the gods.

Notable Locations

Sadar Tajik's Palace

The Palace of Sadar Zari Tajik is a large building molded from dried clay with decorative wooden latices and blinds placed across the faces of the building, along with fabric awnings over most of the windows. The palace itself is painted in vibrant yellows, reds and purples. The inside of the building is a maze of hallways and offices adorned with decorative columns, frescos of legendary warriors including one that seems to be fighting fire, water, wind and earth all at once, gold and silver engravings, and plentiful patterned draperies.

While most of the Palace is hard to find or navigate, the court of Sadar Tajik is set in the center of the palace in an open air courtyard that incorporates rope controlled fabric roofs that can be extended for shade or cover from the rain.

Sadar Zari Tajik lives in, and rules from, her palace. Kalantar Akbar Atlasi and Emira Luta Dagdottir work from the palace, but have their own homes.

Perinone Pawn

Perinone Pawn is a basic swamp log building with an open counter to the street with a simple, dark red sign denoting the store as Perinone Pawn. Random goods can be bought and sold here, though most of the stock is used clothes and weapons, with the occasional piece of restored armor being on display in the back of the showroom. For obvious reasons, Perinone Pawn is popular with adventurers looking to get a cheap replacement or sell off the goods of their latest kill.

Perinone Pawn is owned and operated by Dakota Harrison.

Blood Red Beats

One of the more intentional looking buildings in the second layer of Iirie, Blood Red Beats is a plastered, wood plank building with a 3 foot tall foundation of stacked round stones, a basic street facing counter and a blood red painted iron sign in the shape of a beat. Blood Red Beats sells food ranging from fresh produce to dried travel rations and, when Lauren decides to make them, popular pastries.

Blood Red Beats is owned and operated by Lauren Parker.

Maroon Meads

Maroon Meads is a small stacked stone kitchen with a wine cellar and an open air bar with equally open seating area that uses tarps held in place by thick wooden poles to provide shade during the day and cover during storms. A basic stage made from several logs that have had two sides flattened allow performers to do everything from small plays to band performances. Maroon Meads serves beer, mead, wine and ciders, as well as basic food, and is popular among adventurers.

Maroon Meads is operated by Kar Lodmundsson.

The Scarlet Sauna

The Scarlet Sauna is a multipart complex constructed of carved marble buildings with molded clay baths exposed to the open air, as well as a few saunas, and plentiful guards. Tonna, when she isn't busy with a customer, sits at the entrance to take payments, while a guard will take the position when Tonna isn't available.

The Scarlet Sauna is the main base of the local bather's guild, but exists to sell bathing services. The Scarlet Sauna provides public baths, private bathes, mixed baths, saunas, bathers, including Tonna herself, and professional companions. The private baths range from single person to rentable group baths.

The Scarlet Sauna is owned and operated by Tonna Thorvidottir.
