Elder Tower

A thick based tower ruin in the marble style of the oceanside ruins, save for the fact that any clockwork devices seem to have been looted long ago. Now the tower has fallen into ruin, with only two floors remaining, occupied by aberrations and undead.

Meat Room

A filthy mockery of a butcher, this room is filled with the corpses of animals and humanoids that the creatures here have managed to capture and have started "processing". Some look to have been eaten into without regard while others have been flayed and prepared, while others still have been stripped down to the bone and had their meat and offal sorted.
