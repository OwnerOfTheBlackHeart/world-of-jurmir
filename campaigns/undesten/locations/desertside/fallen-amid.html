Fallen Amid

The remains of the village of Amid. The majority of the buildings were made of wood and paper, while the occasional cob or plastered brick building remains.

The current residents of Fallen Amid are mephits, gricks and river drakes. Non-human cultists of the Red King poke around the ruins of Fallen Amid seeking a rumored way to turn themselves into humans, thus surviving the coming of the Red King.
