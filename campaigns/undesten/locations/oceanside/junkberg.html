Junkberg, The Capital of Yuru Khan

Floating in the depths of the Oceanside, Junkberg is a small scrap island topped with a rounded dome hut.

House, Small

The surface house is a small, two person house complete with kitchen and a single bed. The outer shell of the house is made from scavenged old metal slabs layered over each other and sealed with tar.

Bunks

Bunks provide housing and limited storage for up to 10 people. Though hardly private, this space typically includes beds or cots, linens, small chests with poor locks, and chamber pots.

Lodging

Bath

A Bath contains a single large bathtub or multiple smaller basins, along with a stove for heating water. After spending 1 hour in this room, you gain a +2 bonus on your next ongoing Fortitude save against disease.

Fishing Net, Small, Starter

A simple, yet effective, fishing net capable of providing three person day's rations per day, though it needs to be operated by a single person to prevent the fish within from becoming rotten.

Fishing Net, Small (3)

A simple, yet effective, fishing net capable of providing five person day's rations per day, though it needs to be operated by a single person to prevent the fish within from becoming rotten.

Farmland (3)

Floating farms that are a mix of above water and submerged farms capable of providing five person day's rations per day, though it needs to be operated by a single person to prevent pests and weeds from overtaking the farm.

Solar Distillery, Small

Crafted from the remains of an old clockwork projector, this small solar distillery, when operated by a semi-trained individual, produces up to 5 peoples worth of water per day.

Dock

This is a series of walkways and sturdy posts used to safely moor a water vessel such as a boat or ship. If attached to Storage, it allows you to easily move cargo to and from the water.

Alchemical Engine

A salvaged alchemical engine which allows Junkberg to become mobile. The boiler of the alchemical engine burns one 20 lbs unit of firewood per day and allows Junkberg to move 6 miles over water per day.
