Miri's Palace in Iirie

Built opposite the Slayers' Guild in Iirie, Miri has had a large portion of historic urban housing demolished so that she can build a mansion and temple to her god, Ilvith.

The public parts of the mansion are made of fine stones, such as marble and granite, painted typical drow colors of black, purple and red, while the rest of the campus is covered in a wide range of colors and beautiful murals.
