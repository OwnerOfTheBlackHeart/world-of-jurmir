Coal Bin

A slave shop located in the Grass Foot Tribe.

Made from imported, carved stone bricks with murals of the Underdark etched into the walls, the Coal Bin is as ominous of a building as it is intriguing. Entering in the mushroom wood door leads into a dark, and surprisingly cool, room filled with a handful of cages hold drow. A finely made wooden pedestal stands at the back of the room with a large book sitting on it, the slave registry.

Obzu Nymo works as the seller. Generates 9.3gp per day.
