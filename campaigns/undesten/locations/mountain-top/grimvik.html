Built into the face of one of the many mountains among the Mountainside, Grimvik connects just far enough onto the surface to be able to service the Lostbass River. Most of the buildings of Grimvik are either carved from stone are built from stone bricks carved from waste stone from the mines and carved buildings. While the laws of Grimvik are strict to most visitors, they are quite liberal by the standards of the dwarves.

Organizations and Monsters

The Party for the Fair Distribution of Space

The Party for the Fair Distribution of Space, or simply The Party, is a political party in Grimvik that claims its platform to be one of fair distribution of land and living space; However, The Party frequently shows favoritism to aristocrats and Party members with seniority. While Grimvik's constitution technically allows for other parties to come to power, the fact that only the aristocracy can vote on the ruling party and that The Party has nearly every aristocrat supporting it, other parties have an uphill battle to achieve even a mild chance at taking power. The Party further uses the fact that all land and buildings within Grimvik are owned by the city to aggressively discourage the formation and joining of other political parties in Grimvik

All positions of leadership within the Party are only available to the nobility. The highest position within the Party is Coordinator, a role which is in charge of coordinating the Party and determining the high level distribution of property, and is only available to those who have provided Grimvik and the Party with a great service.

The current Coordinator is Coordinator Anna Kardan.

Notable Locations

Najafi Manor

Najafi Manor is a three story manor built in the space where Grimvik transitions from being underground to being on the surface, built of square cut stone bricks. The outer walls have a sandstone square lattice attached to them, allowing a number of vines to grow up the face of the building. Surrounding the manor is a wrought iron fence featuring a diamond lattice, a jagged top and an arched entrance gate, with rose bushes being grown along the fence. The front yard of the manor is an arboretum with chairs and benches made from fallen branches that have been woven together.

The inside of the manor heavily features natural materials, with rugs made from slain beasts, wooden busts and fine paintings that smell of fruit among stone statues and displayed plate armor. The entrance leads into a foyer that connects to a ballroom, which then branches out to the kitchen, bar, sitting room, offices, servants quarters and the path upstairs. The second floor is dedicated to guests while the third floor holds Anna's personal bedroom and several empty bedrooms that are notably nicer than the guest or servant rooms.

The staff serving the manor are all drow slaves who have their heads shaved. Enchanted collars keep them docile and obedient.

Najafi Manor is the home of Coordinator Anna Kardan.

The Cell on the Left

The Cell on the Left is an open roofed warehouse carved into the lower city of Grimvik, down by the mines. The building mostly blends into the surrounding structures, save for the large sign and the crier that stands out front during periods of high foot traffic.

The inside of the building features a raised stage where cages are placed to hold the goods while customers shuffle about the middle of the floor. Along the walls are several more cages where lower value slaves are placed for easy sale. Two sales podiums, one beside the door in and the other on the opposite wall, allow customers to purchase the slave of their choice.

The Cell on the Left sells both criminals and enemies captured in raids or during attacks on Grimvik. This makes the slave contents a mix of dwarves, ratfolk, drow, duergar and kobolds.

Cota Ezen serves as the Slavemistress for The Cell on the Left.

Stoneskinned Threads

Stoneskinned Threads is a relatively small, two story building carved into the underground second of Grimvik, only two blocks down from the wall that separates the surface and underground sections of Grimvik. The outer walls contrast against the carved stone of the neighboring buildings due to the bright yellow, orange and pink patterns painted all across the building and the large glass window showing examples of Buua's work.

The inside of the shop is a simple room with a counter and a handful of displays showing clothes immediately ready for purchase, while a pair of changing rooms allow for measurement and adjustment in privacy, or even just trying on clothes before one buys. A locked room in the back is where Buua does the majority of the tailoring work.

Stoneskinned Threads offers both premade outfits and custom tailoring work, all done in the traditional dwarven style.

Buua Oyochin is the Party authorized operator of Stoneskinned Threads.

Clock and Steel

Clock and Steel is a small campus build next to the mines of Grimvik, with a smaller carved stone building at the "front" of the campus, six forges made from stone brick and a large stone brick temple at the back of the complex. The front building serves as the shop and administrative center for the campus, where orders are made and fulfilled, along with a small storefront to service those in need of horseshoes, nails and warhammers.

Clock and Steel sells blacksmithing services as well as clockmaking. It also serves as a church to Zigies.

Nongrot Smit serves as the storefront operator of Clock and Steel.

Flashgit and Herbs

Flashgit and Herbs is a one story, warehouse style building in a clean and cool underground portion of Grimvik that looks to have originally been carved out of the stone, but now features many sections that have been repair with stone bricks, round stone patches or even packed stone powder. The sign in front of the wrought iron double doors proudly displays a bottle of medicine next to a mining charge. The storefront of the building is shallow, but protected enough from the street to offer customers privacy as they address medical issues or order other alchemical concoctions, with the basalt counter giving a pharmacy feel to every purchase.

Flashgit and Herbs sells alchemical goods and ingredients, including medicines and explosives.

Bati Alkhimi is the licensed alchemist in charge of Flashgit and Herbs

Daily Necessities

Daily Necessities is a three story carved stone building built into the wall separating the above ground and underground portions of Grimvik, with a counter on either side of the wall that can view into the current stock of the shop. A flight of stairs on the underground side of the wall offers access to the rentable living quarters above the store.

Daily Necessities is a general goods store selling basic goods that are needed daily, including food, tools and aprons, as well as basic first aid medications and bandages. Four apartments are available for rent, each containing a small kitchen, two bedrooms with two beds each and a small living space. Each apartment can be rented for 85 gp per week or 270 gp per month.

Khorijin Khool is the operator of Daily Necessities.

Stout and Whiskey

Stout and Whiskey is a simple two story stone brick building located in the outside portion of Grimvik, next to Beds Soft and Stiff. A door barely large enough for an orc offers entrance into a large, open room with rugs and pillows scattered about surrounding short tables meant to be used while sitting on the floor. A communal fire pit in the center of the room allows customers to take meals purchased from the bar in the back of the room and cook said meals to their preference. The bar lacks chairs, but has a large selection of beer, lager, ale and whiskey.

Stout and Whiskey is a bar, and sells both meals and booze.

Dodai Arkhi is the barkeep of Stout and Whiskey.

Beds Soft and Stiff

Beds Soft and Stiff is a large three story building made of precarved chunks of building that were brought to its location in the outside portion of Grimvik, next to its dwarfed neighbor Stout and Whiskey, and dropped into place. The first floor has several small rooms for rent and a basic foyer where rooms can be rented from. The second floor has 12 average rooms that are popular with traveling merchants and budget conscious adventurers. The third floor has 4 luxury suits that are rented out at 50 gp a night, but include private heated baths, a full, lightly stocked kitchen and enough space to setup 4 bedrooms... each.

Beds Soft and Stiff is an inn, renting out small rooms for temporary accommodations.

Erke Or is the innkeeper of Beds Soft and Stiff.

The Durnhan

The Durnhan is a large, stone brick church that dominates the view of the outside of Grimvik with its ostentatious use of marble, granite and gold, the large, perfectly made panes of stained glass, large spired towers, pentad of domes and the adamantine plated entry doors. The inside of the Durnhan is equally ostentatious with large, beautiful murals painted in the geometric style the dwarves are so fond of with an astounding amount of open space, leaving the large, pearl colored statues of Durn and his saints clear for any visitor to see from any point in the Durnhan.

The Durnhan serves as the Church of Durn in Grimvik.

Father Ong Lam Nar is the current Father of the Durnhan.

The Anti-Rust League

The Anti-Rust League building is a complex of three large buildings made from stone bricks, the smallest of which is the only building open to the public. Inside the smallest building is a small guild hall with a bar for members and patrons only, a quest counter and several offices for meeting with Anti-Rust League associates. The hall is rarely empty and those within carry stone weapons and wear leather armor.

The Anti-Rust League is a group dedicated to clearing out mines and tunnels when they become infested with monsters. This is most often rust monsters, but can include goblins, kobolds, drow and various beasts of the deep.

Chakha Barig is the Chief Relations Officer of the Anti-Rust League.
