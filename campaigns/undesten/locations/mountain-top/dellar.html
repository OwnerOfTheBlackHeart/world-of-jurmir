Built from the surrounding forests, Dellar is a sprawling wooden town built around massive farms and orchards. One of the major sources of food for the town of Grimvik, Dellar is a rapidly growing town and a center of druidic power. Dellar is one of the few locations where elves are more common.

Organizations and Monsters

Mothers of the Trees

Dedicated towards using their druidic teachings to guide the people of Dellar towards sustainable lives that alleviate their impact on the natural world. An oddity of the Mothers of the Trees is that they are purely women capable of bearing children.

Important People

Head Mother Vulsao Mel

Vulsao is a 2 out of 10 female elf with light brown hair, emerald eyes, a slender frame and A cup titties.

Vulsao is the current Head Mother of the Mothers of the Trees.
