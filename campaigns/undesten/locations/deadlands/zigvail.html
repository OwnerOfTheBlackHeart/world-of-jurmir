Zigvail is a small town of aberromancers built into the ruins of the long lost buildings of Zigvail. The central building is an ancient skyscraper in which the administrative facilities and church to the Red King with smaller two to five story buildings surrounding the skyscraper in which most of the town live. Around the town is not a great wall, instead a series of magical devices that ward off uncommanded aberrations.

The buildings are made of ancient concrete, glass, and steel with patches made from fitted bricks and carved, fitted concrete chunks. Unlike the cold gray buildings that make up most of the Deadlands, the buildings of Zigval are brightly painted and seemingly full of life.

Organizations and Monsters

The Pearl Saints

A group of powerful aberromancers, the Pearl Saints provide the people of Zigvail with both spiritual guidance and protection. Worshipping the Red King and believing that it is their mandate to build up the Red King's army in preparation for the return of the Primordials (which they believe the current gods incapable of holding). To do this, they use the people of Zigvail to serve as both aberration incubators and as soldiers, requiring all women born in Zigvail to give birth to two aberrations before they are considered adults and citizens, while men must serve in the military for at least two years for the same rights.

The Pearl Saints maintain a strick law code and keep order like a military, though they are few in comparison to the citizens and the citizenry have a less strict culture which is far laxer concerning the law... save those in active service, who other citizens act properly around.

The Pearl Saints are lead by Grand Saint Reshef, with Kadlin Geirdottir serving as the High Paladin.

Ruin Raiders

The Ruin Raiders are the closest to adventurers that the people of Zigvail have, occasionally leaving Zigvail to delve into the surrounding ruins to gather materials, parts and scavenge food. Their expeditions outside Zigvail also provide scouting services for the Pearl Saints.
