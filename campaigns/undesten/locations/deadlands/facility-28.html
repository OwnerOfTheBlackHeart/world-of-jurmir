Facility 28

Looking like a large, flat concrete pad from the surface, Facility 28 is a collection of underground facilities connected by now collapsed tunnels. Remnants of unknowable machines litter the facilities, suggesting the facilities were for either research or manufacturing.

Dungeon where Farzin's father Dal-Kye has setup his experiments.
