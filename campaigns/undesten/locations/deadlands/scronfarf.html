Scronfarf

Scronfarf is a collection of shorter skyscrapers which struggled to reach 10 floors when they were built. The tight clustering and intermingling of storefronts suggest Scronfarf originally served as living space. Strange rods stretch from Scronfarf into the rest of the Deadlands, the purpose of which is unknown.

Scronfarf is a quiet, peaceful place seemingly devoid of aberrations, at least the hostile kind. A single goblin mechanic has taken up residence in the old train station, using the location as an impromptu workshop.
