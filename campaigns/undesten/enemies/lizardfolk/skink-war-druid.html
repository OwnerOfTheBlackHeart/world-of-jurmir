Death Roll (Ex)

When grappling a foe of its size or smaller, a crocodile can perform a death roll upon making a successful grapple check. As it clings to its foe, it tucks in its legs and rolls rapidly, twisting and wrenching its victim. The crocodile inflicts its bite damage and knocks the creature prone. If successful, the crocodile maintains its grapple.

Hold Breath (Ex)

A crocodile can hold its breath for a number of rounds equal to 4 times its Constitution score before it risks drowning.

Sprint (Ex)

Once per minute a crocodile may sprint, increasing its land speed to 40 feet for 1 round.

Description

This 3 1/2 to 4 foot tall lizardfolk has an impressive, colorful crest on the back of its head. Its small body is a deep green to dark blue and is covered in heavy scales for a humanoid of its size. Wood reinforced hides further protect this small lizardfolk during battle.

War Druids are those skinks that possess the blessings of Zaltys and wield the druidic power provided in defense of their tribes and their lands. Due to this, War Druids are rarely seen on their own, leading bands of skinks, and occasionally larger lizardfolk, to battle against those who would dare threaten lizardfolk lands.

Combat

War Druids fight in one of two ways, depending on their animal companion: If blessed with a crocodile/alligator, the War Druid will stay back with its sling, pelting the enemy, summoning animals, and supporting their animal companion while the crocodile/alligator handles ripping the enemies to shreds. If blessed with a deinonychus, the War Druid fights mounted, making full use of the mobility the dinosaur provides the War Druid with and supporting the mount with magic and attacks of the War Druid's own.
