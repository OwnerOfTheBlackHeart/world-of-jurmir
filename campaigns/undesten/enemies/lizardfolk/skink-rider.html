Hold Breath (Ex)

A lizardfolk can hold its breath for a number of rounds equal to 4 times its Constitution score before it risks drowning.

Description

This 3 1/2 to 4 foot tall lizardfolk has an impressive, colorful crest on the back of its head. Its small body is a deep green to dark blue and is covered in heavy scales for a humanoid of its size. It carries a polearm ending in a wooden club with sharp teeth placed in a mockery of a blade and wears armor made from leather and wood.

Skink riders serve as the cavalry of the lizardfolk, riding quickly around enemies striking with their long polearms and shortbows. The preferred mount of skink riders is the humble deinonychus, which they appreciate for the dinosaurs' speed, aggression, and trainability.

Combat

Skink riders work effectively with skink solders well both on foot and on deinonychus, serving as a well trained backline polearm user or as mobile cavalry that can strike at enemies already locked down by skinks or other lizardfolk. Like other skinks, skink riders will target mages with extreme prejudice before moving on to other soft targets.
