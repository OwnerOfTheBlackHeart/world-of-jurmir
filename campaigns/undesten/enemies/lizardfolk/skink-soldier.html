Hold Breath (Ex)

A lizardfolk can hold its breath for a number of rounds equal to 4 times its Constitution score before it risks drowning.

Description

This 3 1/2 to 4 foot tall lizardfolk has an impressive, colorful crest on the back of its head. Its small body is a deep green to dark blue and is covered in heavy scales for a humanoid of its size. It carries a wooden club with sharp teeth placed in a mockery of a blade and a large wooden shield.

Skink soldiers make up the bulk of how skinks are used in battle by the lizardfolk.

Combat

Skink soldiers move in groups, with roughly half keeping their bows at the ready and the other half keeping their terbutjes at the ready. The melee skinks advance on their foe while the ranged skinks pelt the enemy with arrows, focusing on mages and other easy targets that have outsized impacts on the battle.
