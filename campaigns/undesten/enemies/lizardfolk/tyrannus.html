Hold Breath (Ex)

A lizardfolk can hold its breath for a number of rounds equal to 4 times its Constitution score before it risks drowning.

Description

This 10 to 12 foot tall lizardfolk is built like a brick shithouse and covered in dark green scales. When readied for war, usually by another lizardfolk, tyrannus's wear armor made from leather and wood enchanted by lizardfolk druids, and carry sturdy, well made terbutjes or greatclubs.

Tyrannus are normally kept near lizardfolk villages, but are brought along with raids when they need to deal with fortifications, making full use of the tyrannus's great size and strength to break down walls and reinforced doors.

Combat

It is rare to see tyrannus operating alone, as the lizardfolk normally guard tyrannus's with skinks and normal lizardfolk. They will generally follow the instructions of whoever the tyrannus believes to be the most intelligent around them, but when left to their own devices, a tyrannus erupts from the water and attacks at full power until either they or their enemies are dead.
