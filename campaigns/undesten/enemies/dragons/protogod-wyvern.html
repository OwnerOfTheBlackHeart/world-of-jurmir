Poison (Ex)

Sting—injury; save DC 18; frequency 1/round for 6 rounds; effect 1d4 Constitution damage; cure 2 consecutive saves.

The save DC is Constitution-based.

Power Lift (Ex)

A mythic wyvern can expend one use of mythic power to withdraw as a move action (instead of a full-round action), moving up to its speed instead of double. It can even move straight up. If it’s grappling a creature, the wyvern can bring the grappled creature with it. Usually a wyvern uses this ability to carry off its prey and drop it from above onto a hard surface. Since this is a move action, the wyvern can damage its target, withdraw, and drop its target all in the same round.
