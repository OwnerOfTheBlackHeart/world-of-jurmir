Hobgod

Svala has been watching the activities of the local hobgoblin empire and has notice something unnerving: There is a sect of hobgoblins that have dedicated themselves to creating a God of Goblins, preferably under their control. Having a god, even a minor one, behind them would make the hobgoblins an even worse threat than they already are, and would certainly make them far more bold.

Iryu Clan of the Voksit Empire
