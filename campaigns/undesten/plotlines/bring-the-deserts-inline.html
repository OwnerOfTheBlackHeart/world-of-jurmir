Bring the Deserts Inline

Censor Fu has had enough of the deep desert peoples failing to fall inline and obey the demands of the Ming. The Censor wants to see the deep desert folk be made obedient by force, and pay their tributes willingly.

Quest Outline

  1. Talk to Censor Fu
  2. Head out into the desert to find a deep desert tribe
  3. Talk to the deep desert tribe chieftain
  4. Bring the chieftain inline or join the chieftain in resisting the Ming
  5. Report back to Censor Fu
