Track the Cause

Bonekeeper Ribwort has started to note an outbreak of a disease with a magical aura to it. She doesn't know if finding and destroying the source will cure those already suffering from the disease, but it should prevent further spread, and so she would like an extra pair of hands to track down the source and deal with it.

Quest Outline

  1. Talk to Bonekeeper Ribwort
  2. Track dawn the current source of the disease (local tribe testing a new weapon)
    • Gnolls
    • Hyenas
    • Gnoll sergeant ()
    • Catfolk observer
    • Tiefling wizard
  3. Clear the area out and follow the trail to find the actual source
  4. Destroy the source of the disease and end the plague
    • A catfolk seeking to develop a way to rid the lands of the gnolls
    • Humans, aasimars and tieflings are supporting the catfolk
  5. Return to Bonekeeper Ribwort
