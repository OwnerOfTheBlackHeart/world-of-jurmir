Become Godsoul

Becoming a godsoul is a difficult process, as few are the bodies which can withstand the power of a divine soul. Instead, those who truly wish to command a godsoul must first ready their body for such a soul.

Quest Outline

  1. Gather special plants to help strengthen the body
    • Bladeleaf
    • Catbite
    • Keert
    • Rock Apple
    • Revenant Grass
  2. Optional: Get an artificial heart meant to handle the power of a godsoul.
    • Clockwork Artificial Heart (Dwarven) - Crafted by dwarves. Require half as much food and water, +4 on Fortitude Saving Throws and +2 on Death Saving Throws. Gain Vulnerability (+50% damage taken) to Electricity.
    • Dragon Heart - Taken from a dragon. Gain Energy Resistance 10 vs the element of the dragon, gain Elemental Focus for the dragon's element as a bonus feat (or Greater Elemental Focus if you already have Elemental Focus) and gain a +2 caster level bonus on spells with a type that matches the element of the dragon.
    • Outsider's Heart - Taken from an outsider. Gain Damage Resistance 5, bypassed by the opposing alignment of the outsider the heart is stolen from. All natural and weapon attacks now count as being from the primary alignment of the outsider.
    • Fae Heart - Crafted by a fae. Only 4 hours of sleep are required for a full night's rest, +4 on Saving Throws against mind affecting and polymorph spells, +2 on Saving Throws against paralysis, poison and sleep effects.
    • Aberration's Heart - Taken from a Guardian Naga, Gug or Tentacled Horror.
      • Guardian Naga - A creature with a guardian naga's heart can spit its venom up to 30 feet as a standard action. This is a ranged touch attack with no range increment. Opponents hit by this attack must make successful saves (see following sentence) to avoid the effect. The poison is a contact poison, frequency 1/round for 6 rounds; effect 1d4 Con damage; cure 2 consecutive saves, and the save DC is Constitution based. If the creature has a bite attack, the poison is also applied by the bite as an injury poison.
      • Gug - A creature with a gug's heart gains a climb speed equal to half their normal movement speed and any claw attacks they have gain an extra 5 feet of reach.
      • Tentacled Horror - A creature with a tentacled horror's heart gains fast healing 5, though the fast healing can only heal the creature to half health and is canceled on any turn they take cold or fire damage. The creature also gains the ability to commune peacefully with goblins and bugbears.
  3. Acquire the blood of a powerful magical creature, such as a dragon or a strong magical beast (CR 10+).
  4. Perform the ritual to become a godsoul.
