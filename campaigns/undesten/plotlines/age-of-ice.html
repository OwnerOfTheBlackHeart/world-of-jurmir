Age of Ice

Miri has discovered that something is causing creatures of cold to begin appearing in Undesten. There are also signs of winter coming, something that would be disastrous for Undesten. Sokhatai Ikaya, a shifter who worships the primordial Perform, is trying to prevent this as they believe that Perform loves nature and life, and that this terrible, heretical misuse of her power.

Ragna Namkidottir, a warpriest in service to the beheaded primordial, seeks to use the power of Perform to bring winter to Undesten, thus giving herself enough elemental energy to pull the Moon from the sky and reunite it with Perform's body, which she believes will end suffering, both of the god and of those who will be granted a quick death.

Quest Outline

  1. Party is approached by Sokhatai Ikaya
    • The winter wolves are a sign of someone trying to connect Undesten and the Plane of Ice
    • She seeks to find whoever is causing this and halt their plan
    • Her goddess loves life and is thus against this event
    • She is looking for help in gathering a device known as the Planar Monitor from the lesser deadlands
  2. Access the Planar Monitor from the Elder Tower
    • The Planar Monitor's primary focus runeboard is damaged
    • The Planar Monitor's planar alignment crystal is currently attuned to the Plane of Air
  3. Return to Sokhatai Ikaya
  4. Acquire a replacement primary focus runeboard from Shahin Miri in Jarut
  5. Acquire a replacement planar alignment crystal or find someone that can retune it
  6. Return to the Elder Tower
  7. Find the location of three planar portals (CR 6)
    • Deal with the first portal
      • Encounter 1
        • Selkie, CR 5, 1,600xp
        • 10/10, male selkie, 5" penis, Thorbjorn
        • Will be first encountered in his human form where he will ask the party to retrieve his coat, which was stolen by the drake. Can end up friendly, neutral or hostile.
      • Encounter 2
        • Frost Drake, CR 7, 3,200xp
      • Hints
        • The cold winds lack the salty wetness of Plane of Water
    • Deal with the second portal
      • Encounter 1
        • Ahlinni, CR 4, 1,200xp
      • Encounter 2
        • 2 Trollhounds, CR 5, 1,600xp
      • Encounter 3
        • 2 Ice Trolls, CR 6, 2,400xp
      • Hints
        • While the trolls have brought snow with them, they are creatures not native to the Plane of Water
    • Deal with the third portal
      • Encounter 1
        • Emperor Walrus, CR 8, 4,800xp
      • Hints
        • While this amphibious creature is native to the Plane of Water, it frequently lives in places where the Plane of Water mixes with land
  8. Return to the Elder Tower
  9. Identify the location of Ragna Namkidottir
    • She is located near a portal to Frostgard called the Trifrost
    • A frozen, artificial lake has formed around the Trifrost
    • The lake has not been around long enough for life to begin populating the area
  10. Deal with Ragna Namkidottir and her cult
  11. Return to Sokhatai Ikaya
