Hunt Them First

Svala has picked up the scent of the Iron Serpents and is looking to hunt down and destroy the cults before their activities draw the attention of inquisitors, which Svala calls "baby-burners", and cause trouble for everyone.

Quest Outline

  1. Identify the first Iron Serpents cult
  2. Perform a threat assessment of the Iron Serpents
  3. Infiltrate and/or destroy the first Iron Serpents cult
  4. Collect information to identify other Iron Serpents cults
  5. Track down and destroy 3 Iron Serpents cults
  6. Gather information on the origin of their gods
  7. Kill off the serpent gods
  8. Find a way to prevent more serpent gods from occurring
