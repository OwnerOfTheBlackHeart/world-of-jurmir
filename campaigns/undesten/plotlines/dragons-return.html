Dragon's Return

Pildrylth the Calm has made her way to Undesten after a dragon convergence brought too many competitors to Taaira Ruzara, forcing her to leave. She followed the sea convoy to Undesten only to find it barren of good forest. Instead, Pildrylth has decided that she will have far greater success making a new territory rather than trying to find one or continue using the busy swamps.

Quest Outline

  1. Be approached by Pildrylth
  2. Be sent to kill a dragon slayer as a test of strength
  3. Find a suitable lair for Pildrylth in the desert
  4. Clear out the lair
  5. Report back to Pildrylth
  6. Travel to the lair
  7. Find a means to reinvigorate the soil
  8. Find a means to bring water to the desert
  9. Begin deploying the means to bring water to the desert
  10. Gather a "forest starter pack" of seeds
  11. Find/create a magic item to accelerate the forest's growth
  12. Hunt down thieves stealing from the forest
  13. Track down the group trying to prevent the forest from growing
  14. Kill their minor god
