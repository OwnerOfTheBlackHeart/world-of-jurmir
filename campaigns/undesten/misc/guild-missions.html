Mission Board

Clear out gnolls (one hex)

Success DC: 25
Required Successes: 3
Travel Time: 1 days
Activity Time: 1 day
Rewards: 15,000xp and 5,000gp
Type: Conquest
Modifiers:

  • At least 1 combat party member: +2
  • Per combat party member (max 4): +1
  • At least 1 nature party member: +1
  • At least 1 divine party member: +2
  • At least 1 stealth party member: +1
  • Every party member beyond 5th: +1
  • No nature party member: -2
  • No combat party member: -2
  • Per social party member: -1

In order to settle a savannah hex, one must first secure the hex. While there are plenty of threats among the savannah, gnolls are the primary one which must be dealt with.

Maintain Hex Security (one hex)

Success DC: 20
Required Successes: 1
Travel Time: 0.5 days
Activity Time: 6 days
Rewards: 2,500xp and 500gp
Type: Security
Modifiers:

  • At least 1 combat party member: +2
  • Per combat party member (max 2): +1
  • At least 1 nature party member: +2
  • Per nature party member (max 2): +1
  • Every party member beyond 5th: +1
  • No nature party member: -2
  • No divine party member: -2
  • No combat party member: -2

Until the settlement within a hex is Small Town or larger, they will be incapable of defending themselves from gnolls and lizardfolk. Supplemental support in maintaining the security of their controlled area and the regular culling of threats are required to prevent attack.

This mission must be performed once per month on any controlled, dangerous hex (savannah or swamp), or the settlement will come under attack and possibly be destroyed.

Profitable Slave Trading

Success DC: 18
Required Successes: 5
Travel Time: 0 days
Activity Time: 1 days
Rewards: 250xp and 1,000gp plus 10gp for every point rolled above the success DC
Type: Trading
Modifiers:

  • At least 1 stealth party member: +2
  • Per social party member (max 3): +1
  • No social party member: -4

Slave trading, while a profitable business, requires savvy trades and the utilization of a carefully curated network. Assigning personnel to operate the business and forge and maintain connections goes a long way to keeping things afloat.

This is a relatively safe and unrushed task, allowing the party to Take 10 on the Success DC.

Offer Slave Improvement Services

Success DC: 25
Required Successes: 2
Travel Time: 0 days
Activity Time: 1 days
Rewards: 1,000xp and 3,000gp
Type: Arcane Services
Modifiers:

  • At least 1 arcane party member over level 5: +2
  • Per arcane party member (max 4): +1
  • Per divine or nature party member (max 2): +1
  • At least 4 arcane, divine and/or nature party members: +1
  • No arcane party member: -2
  • No social party member: -2

Utilizing the Bath of Ilvith it is possible to improve the aesthetic value of a slave. If the means by which this are done are kept quiet, or it is carefully done to slaves who have been purchased with intent to resell, the secret of the Bath of Ilvith can be prevented from becoming public knowledge.

This is a relatively safe and unrushed task, allowing the party to Take 10 on the Success DC.
