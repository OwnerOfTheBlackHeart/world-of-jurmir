Zoe's Hounds

For reasons and through means currently unknown, the private forces of Zoe Von Nalbalgrab have managed to reach Undesten. At present, their actions have been minimal, avoiding local populations and scouting around the Desertside and Deadlands.

When rolling sex for a member of the Heck's Hounds, start by rolling a D3. On a 1, roll sex normally. On a 2 or a 3, they are a feminine hermaphrodite.

Deployment

Zoe's response to finding the portal to Undesten has been a muted affair. The deployed force is small, with only two 3 man cells in camo twin fangs to serve as the heavy hitters with the rest of the deployed units being recon forces. The recon team is lead by Asukai Hatsu, the fox-girl summoner, and Woya the Dragonguard commander. Hank has been informed and has also informed Letia Pretre, his tamed lamia red lady, and Dame Angele Desmarais, the dragon dame.

Heck's Hounds are trying to establish a base and a covert means of generating an army, as well as working to establish a secure location for housing their forces.

Hank is interested in running experiments on how Letia handles the realm and also... MAGIC!

Races of Zoe's Hounds

Hellhound, 20 RP

The standard modified human within Heck's Hounds, the typical Hellhound is a tough bitch and a team player that is absolutely loyal to Zoe Von Nalbalgrab.

Example: Hellhound Soldier

  • Type: Monstrous Humanoid (3 RP)
  • Size: Medium (0 RP)
  • Base Speed: Normal, 30 feet (0 RP)
  • Ability Score Modifier: Advanced, +2 Str, +2 Dex, +2 Con, +4 Charisma, -2 Intelligence (4 RP)
  • Language: Standard, Mandate and Xarnin. Bonus Languages: Common, Tselam, Ludzie, German, Latin, Dwarvish, Catfolk (0 RP)
  • Traits
    • Advanced Dexterity, +2 Dexterity, 4 RP
    • Defensive Training, Greater, +2 dodge to AC, 4 RP
    • Pack Tactics, 4 RP
    • Weapon Familiarity, Firearms, 1 RP

Racial Traits

  • Ability Score Modifiers: +2 Str, +4 Dex, +2 Con, +4 Charisma, -2 Intelligence
  • Type: Monstrous Humanoid
  • Size: Medium
  • Base Speed: 30 feet
  • Language: Mandate and Xarnin. Bonus Languages: Common, Tselam, Ludzie, German, Latin, Dwarvish, Catfolk
  • Traits
    • Defensive Training, Greater: Hellhounds are difficult to pin down, and gain a +2 dodge bonus to AC
    • Pack Tactics: At the start of their turn, a Hellhound can designate a single enemy as their target. All allies with Pack Tactics, including the Hellhound herself, gains a +1 moral bonus to attack and damage rolls against the target, up to +5 to attack and damage.
    • Weapon Familiarity (firearms): Hellhounds are trained in the use of firearms before they are given the Hellhound gene mods, and are considered proficient with firearms regardless of class levels.
