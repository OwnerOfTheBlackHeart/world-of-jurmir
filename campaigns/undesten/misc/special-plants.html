Special Plants

Some plants offer effects beyond those of normal, mundane medicines or poisons. These valuable plants maybe magical or just alchemically potent, but the effect is the same: They do what other plants cannot.

Once a special plant is found, it can be grown in a special garden with the same cost, time and size as the Farmland room from the downtime rules, except that instead of earnings it produces the special plant as per the special plant's Grow Rate.

Plants

Bladeleaf

Cost: 10gp per dose

Grow Rate: 1 dose per day

Bladeleaf is a small, simple bush with leaves that have razor sharp edges. While unable to cause more harm than a paper cut, the bladeleaf plant is still prized by soldiers and warriors. When the leaves are ground into a powder and allowed to fully dissolve into boiling water, it creates a tea-like drink that helps a warrior stay focused.

Consuming a dose of bladeleaf grants a +1 moral bonus to attack rolls for 12 hours.

Catbite

Cost: 10gp per dose

Grow Rate: 1 dose per day

Catbite is a small, thorny bush with flowers that look like a cat's eye and white thorns that look like their teeth. Stripping the thorns from the bush, they can be powdered and mixed with water and flower, they can produce a foul tasting paste the consistency of yogurt. Those warriors capable of handling the flavor of catbite paste keep it in leather "vials" that they can quickly decork and slurp down like a potion and unleash that little bit more of their strength upon their enemies.

Consuming a dose of catbite grants a +2 moral bonus to damage rolls with melee weapons for 2 hours.

Keert

Cost: 15gp per dose

Grow Rate: 5 days per dose

Keert is a fast growing, if picky, flower whose leaves can be pickled, then mixed into a paste, to create a simple salve that helps one heal and draws away toxins on the skin. Not well known among healers, those with access to keert like to keep small stockpiles for dealing with poison ivy and lacerations.

Using a dose of keert paste as part of a Heal skill check grants a +2 circumstance bonus to Provide First aid, Provide Long-term care, Treat wounds, Treat deadly wounds and Treat poison if the poison is a contact or injury poison.

Rock Apple

Cost: 20gp per dose

Grow Rate: 7 days per dose

Rock apples are a strange breed of tree that has adapted to grow in caves and mines. Rare in nature, dwarves frequently plant these trees in their mines to keep as snacks while working. Few realize that the hard, stone-flavored fruit aids in fortifying their bodies against the dust and disease of the mines. While dwarves are willing to fight over what mines produce the best tasting rock apples, copper mines have the greatest number of supporters.

Eating a full rock apple provides a +2 circumstance bonus on Fortitude saves against disease and airborne poisons.

Revenant Grass

Cost: 150gp per dose

Grow Rate: 28 days per dose

A dark, short grass that smells like death and grows best around undead, revenant grass is considered to be a rare treat by orcs, who dry the grass before mixing it with tobacco and smoking it from a pipe. Those who know about its use to stave off death instead mix fresh picked revenant grass with various fruits and vegetables to take the edge of the grass's flavor, then lard and wax the concoction to preserve it and make a single dose last longer.

Chewing on a dose of revenant grass allows a creature to remain Disabled, instead of dying, until -10 hit points and grants a +2 alchemy bonus to Constitution checks to stabilize.
