Pack Tactics (Ex)

At the start of their turn, a Hellhound can designate a single enemy as their target. All allies with Pack Tactics, including the Hellhound herself, gains a +1 moral bonus to attack and damage rolls against the target, up to +5 to attack and damage.

Weapon Familiarity (Firearms) (Ex)

Hellhounds are trained in the use of firearms before they are given the Hellhound gene mods, and are considered proficient with firearms regardless of class levels.

Defensive Training, Greater (Ex)

Hellhounds are difficult to pin down, and gain a +2 dodge bonus to AC.

Description

Yanaba is a 4' 9", 8 out of 10 female hellhound with thick, back length brown hair, golden eyes, lightly tanned skin and D cup titties. She normally wears a sleeveless red crop-top with a long, brown parted dress bottom and sturdy boots, along with several belts and pouches for collecting items that interest her.

Yanaba is a friendly, if occasionally cold, woman fully devoted to the cause of the Heck's Hounds and her mistress Zoe. She is motivated by two things: Her orders and her own comfort, though she will sacrifice for, and look out for, other members of the Heck's Hounds. Seki Kayo is serving as her current handler.

History and Motivation

Yanaba hails from the planet Xarnin, specifically from among the Tribes of Memdi. Originally a slave traded among tribes, Yanaba was bought by a representative of the Heck's Hounds and allowed to go free. The Heck's Hounds made her an offer upon her freedom of joining up and serving with the Heck's Hounds among the stars. Yanaba didn't hesitate to join, and breezed through the indoctrination program with flying colors, though she struggled with the combat training due to her magical background.

Yanaba came to Undesten as a scout for the Heck's Hounds, to see if the world had work and the effective means to make such work worthwhile.

Yanaba was deployed under the direction of Kayo to aid the silverwolf with identifying and interacting with magic.
