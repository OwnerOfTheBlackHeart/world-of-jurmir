Ikaya typically wears a loose, white shirt with a black vest that barely covers her cleavage, a pair of thin, tight shorts, black knee-high boots, and a green sash that she wears like a belt. History and Motivation

Ikaya is from a village hidden deep in the jungles of Undesten, one whose location she will not speak of, which she left as a means to better serve and understand her god, Perform. Along the way, she has fallen in love with the arts of the various cultures, especially those of Iirie, whose melting pot of cultures lets her study several at once.
