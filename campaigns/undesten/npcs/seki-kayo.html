History and Motivation

Kayo hails from the planet Xarnin, specifically from Yamasato in the Togawa Shogunate, Fujinai. Originally working as a servant of the local daimyo, Kayo was recruited as part of Zoe Von Nalbalgrab's program to collect all the wolf people of Xarnin and "free" them from the local dragons. After a short training and indoctrination program, Kayo began loyally serving Zoe and Heck's Hounds as an infiltrator and spy.

Kayo came to Undesten as a scout for the Heck's Hounds, to see if the world had work and the effective means to make such work worthwhile.

Kayo is actually investigating the general usefulness of Undesten for conquest. She views the world as mostly useless, though the powerful magic and seemingly different rules that the creatures in Undesten follow interest her, especially magic items and the gods. A member of Zoe's Cult of the Red King, the existence of a second Red King intrigues her and she seeks to find a way to allow Zoe to consume him and his power.
