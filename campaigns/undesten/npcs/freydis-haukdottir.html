History and Motivation

Freydis hails from the mountains to the west of Arx, growing up in one of the many nomadic tribes that lived in that region. When Svala was 15, her tribe was attacked by goliaths which caused her to make for Cuul, where Svala would discover the slayer and begin testing her to she if she was worthy of joining the Fallenfeather Sisterhood.

Freydis passed the tests with flying colors, finding herself brought before Akkus and being inducted into the Fallenfeather Sisterhood.

Over her time in serving as a Fury of the Fallenfeather Sisterhood, Freydis has become particularly close to Svala. Close enough that when Svala told her that she was going to be joining an expedition to Iirie, Freydis didn't hesitate to leave everything behind to go with Svala.

Freydis is driven by the hunt and her service to Akkus, but her strongest motivator is her love for Svala.
