Description

Svala is a 5' 6", 8 out of 10 human woman with short, dark red hair, pale skin, dark blue eyes and D cup titties. She normally wears her enchanted, black spider-silk bodysuit with a dark red, hooded, crop-top poncho with loose sleeves and white floral designs, and a matching skirt over the bodysuit while a pair of sturdy boots guard her feet. Svala doesn't wear consistent jewelry, but it is normally humble compared to her attire.

Svala's outfit comes from a variety of sources, with the bodyglove having been made for her by her mentor, a drow woman named Vanos Aerqon who served as the chief Kata for Cuul, when Svala told her she would be leaving for Iirie, while the poncho and skirt were stolen from a passing Ming trader.

Svala is a curious, confident and focused woman who is always looking for an edge and a chance to build her network. She is cautious with strangers and friends alike, always ready for betrayal, but quick to put on a convincing smile if the situation calls for it.

Svala is a feminine hermaphrodite with an 8" penis.

History and Motivation

Svala hails from Ashborne, where she was born and raised by parents that only had children so they could give them chores to perform. During her time living with her parents, Svala learned both how to manipulate and how to spot when she was being manipulated, but also how to act without drawing the attention of her parents, so as to avoid doing their chores.

When Svala was old enough, she began traveling around Lyrna, eventually meeting Vanos Aerqon. Vanos helped Svala refine the talents living with her parents had taught her, as well as a few other tricks of spycraft, before subtly testing Svala, which Svala quickly noticed. Knowing that it was best to perform averagely, Svala did exactly that, passing the tests well enough to be brought before Akkus and inducted into the Fallenfeather Sisterhood.

After 3 years of operating with the Sisterhood, Svala was chosen to join the expedition to Iirie to aid in forming a cell within Undesten. A year in, and the Undesten Fallenfeather Sisterhood cell has settled comfortably in the area and Svala intends to get it acting and expanding.
