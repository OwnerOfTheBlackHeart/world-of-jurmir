GM Notes

Skyscraper Origins

The ancient ruins are from the time of Steele and were great works of the dwarves trying to build mountains artificially. The ancient arcane magics and clockwork machines that weren't outright destroyed during the war with the Red King have rotted away. The arcane magics have corrupted the lands around them while what clockwork still functions poses a danger to anyone fool enough to place themselves willingly around it.
