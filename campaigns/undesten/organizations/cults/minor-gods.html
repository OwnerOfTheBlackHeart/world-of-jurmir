Lolth

Picture of Lolth

Alignment: NE

Domains: Darkness, Death (Murder)

Portfolio: Assassins, Darkness, Drow, Spiders, Poisons, Matriarchs, Dark Magic, Demons

Symbol: A web with a headless spider on it

Favored Weapon: Dagger or ray

Colors: Black and Blood Red

Tenants

  • The spider is the greatest of all beasts
  • An ounce of venom well used is more powerful than any blade
  • Seek to be the advantaged party in all dealings
  • Weak exist to serve the strong

Description

Lolth appears as a massive, 15 foot tall drider with a black widow's body and a beautiful, purple female drow's torso dressed in finery of gold, spider silk and leather. She is normally depicted wielding powerful magics. Despite her attractive appearance, Lolth is resoundingly cruel and malicious, and no true worshiper of Lolth expects to maintain her gifts if they are cease to be useful to her or otherwise earn her vindictive wrath.

The most well known story of Lolth is when she revealed herself to the drow. Lolth arrived in the drow city of Julithron'ikan (commonly called Jikan by non-elves), proclaiming herself to be the first sane drider, then demanding that the drow of Julithron'ikan submit and accept her as their queen or she would turn them into driders like her. When the drow inevitably denied her and called upon Sithrak, the god told the god to ignore her and focus on keeping the Red King contained. Infuriated by the lack of response from the drow, Lolth extracted her own venom and dumped it into each of their wells. With the drows' god ignoring them, the drow of Julithron'ikan rapidly ran out of clean water and resorted to drinking the poisoned water. As the drow began to whither and suffer from her poison, Lolth climbed over the city wall and attacked, capturing those who put up a good fight against her into driders to serve as her army while gorging on those she viewed as only having value as food, slowly growing in size as she devoured her way through Julithron'ikan until she reached her current size.

Now 15 feet tall and having an army of driders at her back, Lolth arrived next at the city of Yuvnur'vrenti. This time, instead of demanding to be queen, she demanded they worship her as a god.

Motivation

Lolth seeks to take control of the drow and overthrow Sithrak, becoming the ruler of Hell and the Underdark.
