Iron Serpents

Description

The Iron Serpents is not a specific single cult, but rather a collection of coordinated cults of closely related deities with the same goals, namely the replacement of the current pantheon with gods of their own. The gods of these cults are reptilian or serpentine, mysterious, manipulative and power hungry, with more than one cult among the Iron Serpents collecting a number of serpent gods into their own unique pantheons.

The Iron Serpents, being as decentralized as they are, very from cult to cult. They can be organized, rigid and martial, chaotic with influence based off magical power, friendly with social status determining influence, or anything inbetween. What is shared is the veneration of snakes, lizards and drakes, especial those that live in the desert, and the objective of replacing the New Gods with serpent gods. The vast majority of Iron Serpent cults are led by a Scaled Chosen, a servant of their god(s) who has been converted into a serpentfolk by deific intervention, with the largest cults having multiple Scaled Chosen, who coordinate as often as they compete. Serving directly under the Scaled Chosen are the priests of the cults, who are charged with carrying out the will of the gods and the Scaled Chosen, as well as teaching the common cultist the faith. Everything below the priests depends on the cult itself, following whatever structure their god(s) and Scaled Chosen deem best.

Tenants

  • Scales hold mastery over flesh.
  • We are one and one are we.
  • The Age of the Corpse Gods is ending. The Age of the Serpent Gods comes next.

Relations

The Iron Serpents do not make regular contact with other cults and generally avoid them if possible... with the singular except if the Iron Serpents are working to actively manipulate another cult.

Minor Gods

Ohtluh (ot-lu)

Alignment: N

Domains: Earth, Trickery

Portfolio: Inphidians, Venomous Snakes, Gold, Greed

Symbol: A horned viper's head with two smaller heads growing from it

Favored Weapon: Razor whip

Colors: Light brown and yellow

Tenants

  • Gold given is safety gotten
  • A fang left dry is a fang unworthy of the bite

Description

Ohtluh appears as a large sand viper large enough to eat a giant with two smaller vipers growing out from its head. The golden eyes of Ohtluh seem to ever hunt for creatures to bite and inject venom into.

Ohtluh is a minor god of inphidians (snake handed humanoids) and venomous snakes that claims the transitory region between the plains and the deserts. Despite being a venomous predator, Ohtluh is also a greedy one and demands tolls of those who pass through his territory if one wishes to avoid getting eaten.

Naeem

Alignment: N

Domains: Magic, Void

Portfolio: Stars, Foresight, Prophecy, Hallucination, Magic, Serpentfolk

Symbol: A collection of 17 stars shaped like a snake's head

Favored Weapon: Sword cane

Colors: Midnight and white

Tenants

  • A starry night is worth a thousand sunny days
  • There is no greater weapon than knowing your enemy's next strike
  • The past, the present, and the future are all one, and must be taken as one

Description

Naeem appears as a massive constrictor snake with eyes that show the stars behind them, even through solid objects such as the world. Naeem's gaze only ever meets mortals that serve as his champions and is said to grant insights both glorious and horrible.

Naeem is a minor god of serpentfolk and constrictor snakes that claims the oasis around the Northern Deadlands as his own. Naeem is ever hungry on his quest to total enlightenment and knowledge, seeking to consume other gods not for strength, but to gain greater insight into all things.

Naeem serves as the chief god of the serpentfolk and is believed by them to have once been an old god who shed his power like scales to escape the notice of the Red King.

Kneph

Alignment: LE

Domains: Water, Weather

Portfolio: Beaches, Saltwater, Swimming, Sea Snakes, Coral Reefs, Storms, Serpentfolk

Symbol: A serpent swimming through coral or rocks

Favored Weapon: Tail and Tail Weapons

Colors: Blue and Orange

Tenants

  • A stormy sea is the time to strike at prey
  • The sea is ever hungry and shall be fed before it feeds on you
  • Tolerate not the false fish (merfolk, and similar aquatic humanoids)

Description

Kneph appears as a whale size blue and orange sea snake that moves through the sea with such grace that his great mass does not disturb the coral reefs, sand, or stones below him. Kneph is a calm deity, willing to make deals that are enforced with extreme prejudice, though he rarely cares about the goings on of the land dwellers.

Kneph is a minor god of ocean-travel, storms, sea snakes, and serpentfolk that claims the beaches and ocean of Undesten, locked in an eternal war with the merfolk and gillmen. Kneph desires nothing more than to claim his territory without question and without competition.

Kneph is considered by most serpentfolk to be a god worth only occasional prayers and consideration due to their desert homes, but those serpentfolk that take to boats and seek the waves worship Kneph with far greater gusto.

Kakra

Alignment: CE

Domains: War, Rune

Portfolio: Runes, War, Battle, Deserts, Sand, History, Serpentfolk

Symbol: A snake's trail through sand

Favored Weapon: Planson

Colors: Yellow and Brown

Tenants

  • It is the place of the serpentfolk to rule
  • The sands reveal the weakness of the lesser races
  • Even the oldest of ruins remains for a reason

Description

Kakra appears as a sand-brown, spiny-scaled horned viper the size of a giant with six large eyes that track all that move around her. Kakra is an aggressive god that hates most mortal races, only caring for serpentfolk.

Kakra is a minor god of war and serpentfolk that claims the sandy wastes of Undesten as her territory. Kakra hates all that isn't serpentfolk, but has a special hatred for mammals, for having stolen the world that rightfully belongs to the serpentfolk, and the lizardfolk, for being pale imitations of her beloved race.

Kakra is a beloved deity among the serpentfolk and one that serves to drive their hatred towards humanity and their wars for the deserts and the ruins within them.

Shahida

Alignment: CN

Domains: Trickery, Scalykind

Portfolio: Weresnakes, Illusion, Transmutation, Alchemy, Lizards

Symbol: A winking "snake"

Favored Weapon: Lucene

Colors: Brown and Green

Tenants

  • Let not but the dead see your truth
  • Knowledge without use is a worthless pursuit
  • Knowledge that can be wielded is a weapon most divine

Description

Shahida appears as a green backed, brown stomached scaly-foot the length of a man and as thick as an arm with an unnatural presence. Shahida is a playful god that enjoys a good trick or prank, though her own are as likely to be life threatening as they are harmless.

Shahida is a minor god of deceptive magics, alchemy, and weresnakes that has managed to fake her way into the Iron Serpents for reasons all her own. She claims the Great Cloud Forest as her own.

Shahida is worshiped by many a weresnake for her blessings making it easier to control their affliction and her love of watching their schemes play out while those seeking to turn themselves into snake or serpent people also commonly worship Shahida for her influence of transmutation and alchemy.
