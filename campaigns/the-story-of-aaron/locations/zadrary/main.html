The Country of Zandry

The leadership of Zandry currently wish to take over Regra as a staging ground for an invasion of {kingdom that Lyrna is a part of}. Zandry currently has the largest number of layline intersections within Jurmir and, as such, has many schools of wizardy.

The military of Zandry heavily incorporates magic into its troops and battle tactics. Warmages are a part of most battle groups.
