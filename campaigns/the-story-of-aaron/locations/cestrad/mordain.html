The Village of Mordain

The village of Mordain sits on the banks of Sapphire Lake with the most common path connecting Cestrad and Lyrna. While the town has a lord, the Traveller's Guild actually runs the city.

Organizations and Monsters

The Mordain Traveller's Guild

The Mordain Traveller's Guild is a group of merchants, craftsmen, and other locals within Mordain that support travellers passing through the city. Whether it is keeping up to date news on laws and regulations, directing travellers to stores and inns, or offering local jobs for the passing adventurer, the Guild provides its services at the expence of Lord Dalton Cockse.

Notable Peoples

Lord Dalton Cockse

Male human, 43 years old. 5' 3" and 130lbs. Dark tan skin and black hair with brown eyes. Generally cheerful and wears colorful and nice furs. Likes to profit by making everyone profit. (Slim, little light black man)

Milburga Blackwoode

Female half-elf, owns Blackwoode Ironworks. Dating Aston Goodwine. 30 years old. 5' 2" and 107lbs. Caramel skin, bark brown hair and deep green eyes. Quiet and slow to trust.

Rylee Blackwoode

Female half-elf, twin of Milburga. Runs the shop for Blackwoode Ironworks. 30 years old. 5' and 115lbs. Caramel skin, golden hair and light green eyes. Friendly, curious, and observant.

Aston Goodwine

Male human, owns the Reflecting Lake Bar. Dating Milburga Blackwoode. 22 years old. 5' 7" and 165lbs. Lightly tanned skin, brown hair with green highlights, and blue eyes with brown specks. Cordial, but reserved.

Lili Miltone

Female elf, owns Saddles n' More. 161 years old. 4' 11" and 110lbs. Pale skin, light brown hair, and green eyes. Warm and aloof.

Notable Locations

The Reflecting Lake Bar

Located on a small hill overlooking Sapphire Lake, the Reflecting Lake Bar has outdoor dining area with a beautiful view of the lake, especially during sunrise and sunset. The bar is three stories tall, with the first floor being the kitchen and indoor dining area/bar, the second floor being primarily meeting rooms (used by both locals and adventurers) that are catered upon request, and the third floor is 6 single person rooms, 4 2 person rooms, and a single 10 person room.

The Reflecting Lake Bar is owned by Aston Goodwine, who serves during the day.

Blackwoode Ironworks

A relatively basic smithy with a large selection of nails, tools, and horse shoes, Blackwoode Ironworks is the only smithy located on the main road through Mordain. It consists of a basic wooden shop front and a covered smithy in the back.

Blackwoode Ironworks is owned by Milburga Blackwoode, who also operates the smithy. Her twin, Rylee, runs the shopfront.

Saddles n' More

Saddles n' More is a 12 animal stables with a decently sized wooden storefront attached. Most of the stock in Saddles n' More is oriented to animals and their handling, like saddles and feed. However, the storefront also serves as a general store and keeps small stocks of food and tools.

Saddles n' More is run by Lili Miltone, who keeps the storefront open during the day.

The Lord's Manner

The lord's manner is a 5 story tall wooden building with the first 2 floors being made of stone. The first floor, which is cut into a hill, has Dalton's private stables and storage for his card and wagon. The second floor, which is generally treated as the ground floor, has the kitchen, living room, and trophy room. The third floor is setup for handling buisness dealings as well as book keeping and a small library. The fourth and fifth floors are dedicated to living spaces from guest rooms to servants quarters to the lord's suite.

Everything within Lord Dalton's estate is clearly expensive and of excellent quallity.
