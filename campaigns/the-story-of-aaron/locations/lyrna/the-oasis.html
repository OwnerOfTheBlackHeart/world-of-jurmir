The Oasis Guild Fortress

Sitting in the wilds of the Kingdom of Lyrna sits a (slightly) ruined fortress that was at one point a school of wizardry. A mix of durable stone construction and a touch of magic have preserved the buildings within for 200 years. Now the aasamar Aaron has taken control of the complex, intending to use it as a guild hall.

The complex is made up of an outer wall (repaired in 1488) with 3 sections containing a total of 8 buildings. The two most prominent being the large tower and the main building.

Building 1

This is a large stone building of 3 floors. The outside of the building is beginning to crumble with vines growing up to and from the windows of the building. A large door, roughly 4 persons wide, is the only entrance to the building.

The first floor is primarily a large hallway with empty stone pillars that stand around 4 feet tall. Along the walls are entrances to many rooms (offices). Off to the west is a set of double doors that lead into a decaying library which has been mostly scavenged. A staircase is at the east wall, going both up and down.

The second floor is made up of a thin hallway with several large rooms (classrooms) on either side. There is a strong, but old, aura of magic here. Most of the walls don't match, and many have burn or acid marks on them. The stairs on the eastern wall continue up.

The third floor is more large rooms.

In the basement is an untouched office, with a spellbook, and a metal door with a powerful magical aura. The presence of the door makes non-evil creatures feel uncomfortable. When the door is touched, whispers can be heard. Beyond the door is various forms of magical containment holding creatures of all types.

Within the basement, only five of the 20+ containment methods still work/contain their prisoners.

  • A cage with an obsidian black imp with 3 tails, 4 arms, and 6 horns in a line. Offers magic (wizard). Taken by Aaron.
  • The skeleton of a satyr frozen in glass. Offers sight (access to certain div spells per day). Taken by Erik.
  • A dog covered in quills with the head of a vulture chained to the floor. Offers might (fighter or barbarian). Taken by Chione.
  • A well dressed dwarf sitting on a couch within a magic circle. Offers wealth (will direct towards wealth)
  • A black fox with a glowing rune on its forehead. Offers stealth (rogue, Razz)

  • Rooms:
    • 10 offices and 1 library on the first floor
    • 6 2nd floor classrooms (large, unfurnished)
    • 3 3nd floor classrooms (large, unfurnished, intact), 3 3nd floor classrooms (large, unfurnished, broken roof)
    • 1 basement office (large, furnished) and 1 basement vault (magically protected, room for 20 cages beyond the 1 used cage)
  • Upgrades:
    • Clean up library: 10,000 gold
    • Convert offices to stalls: 2,000 gold
    • Repair offices: 3,500 gold
    • Turn 2nd/3rd floor rooms to furnished classrooms: 2,000 gold per 3 rooms
    • Turn 2nd/3rd floor rooms to labs: 5,000 gold per 3 rooms
    • Turn 2nd/3rd floor rooms to mass living areas: 3,500 gold per 3 rooms
    • Repair roof: 15,000 gold
    • Repair vault cage: 5,000 gold each, must be made for a particular creature

Building 2

This is a large ampitheater. Going down from the upper edge are rows of weathered and broken stone benches. At the far end is a raised section of stone covered in moss with a large stone overhang that appears to have not taken the weather well. Sections of the overhang have crumbled to the ground.

The ampitheater has a weak magical aura. No single type can be discerned.

  • Rooms:
    • Open space
  • Upgrades:
    • Repair ampitheater: 10,000 gold

Building 3

This building is half stone and half wood. The stone and wood do not appear to be rotted and no door blocks the entrance. Those with arcane magic pass through the portal with ease, but those without are stopped by the sudden appearance of a stone wall. If their hand is held by an arcane caster, the wall will allow them through.

Inside is an almost untouched laboratory, with moss and dust covering everything. The tools here are almost unusable, but the stone tables are still in relatively good condition. There are several ovens and piles of rotting wood. This building is flooded with powerful magical auras.

  • Rooms:
    • Main lab room with 2 storage closets
  • Upgrades:
    • Repair lab: 5,000 gold
    • Convert to bar: 6,000 gold

Building 8

This is a four story tall stone tower. It has a central spiral staircase with rooms on each floor. The rooms come in sets with a kitchen, living area, and bedroom. Many of the rooms' furniture does not appear to have aged. Magic still permeates this building. On the roof is a small lookout area.

  • Rooms:
    • 15 furnished livings spaces of a living room, kitchen and bedroom each with a lookout area on the roof.
  • Upgrades:
    • Dig basement: 2,000 gold or 500 man hours
    • Install vault: 50,000 gold
