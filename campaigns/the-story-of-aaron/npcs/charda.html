Charda Diliriy, Chione's Drow Slave

Charda is a 4' 8", 95 lbs female drow with white hair, red eyes, and youthful features. She has a thin body with an hourglass figure and a well defined bust.

At first, Charda was resistant and aggressive towards Chione, however Chione was able to tame the drowess (with the help of Aaron). While she is still at risk of attaining mental freedom and more than able to resist commands, Chione may break her still.
