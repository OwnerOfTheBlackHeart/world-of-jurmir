Charda Diliriy, Chione's Drow Slave

Charda is a 4' 8", 95 lbs female drow with white hair, red eyes, and youthful features. She has a thin body with an hourglass figure and a well defined bust.

At first, Charda was resistant and aggressive towards Chione, however Chione was able to tame the drowess (with the help of Aaron). During her pregnancy, Chione was able to fully tame Charda.
