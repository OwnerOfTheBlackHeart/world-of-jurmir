This is the Story of Aaron

Aaron the aasimar, bard in search of powerful magics, is the focus of the first campaign within the world of Jurmir. The sole player of the game, he follows the destiny that he makes for himself with the help of whatever mongrels he decides to pick up along the way.

Aaron is played by Cody and this campaign is run in Dungeons and Dragons 3.5e.
