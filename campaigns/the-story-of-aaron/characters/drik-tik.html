Description

Drik-Tik stands at 5' 1" with a thick, sandy yellow exoskeleton, granting a weight of 156 lbs. One of her antenna is limp half-way up it and her exoskeleton has many marks where it has clearly healed over.

Combat

Drik-Tik prefers to make sure that she has at least her pounce mutation prepared. If she does, she will usually leap into battle and try to get as many strikes in as possible. If she has her fast healing mutation active, she will try and draw attention to her and absorb the damage while her allies flank and destroy her enemies. If she knows that she will be expected to stand and fight, she will ready her class with oversized offense while ensuring that she has at least fast healing 1.

History and Motivation

Drik-Tik had a clan once. Then they died and she survived, only serving to prove that she was a better hunter than her clan. She then suffered through life on her own for a while before coming upon civilization. Civilization that she quickly grew to view as weak, but whose numbers prevented her from being able to do anything about.

Now Drik-Tik seeks to grow in power and to acquire a new clan. A strong clan. A clan that can exert influence over the weaker creatures that surround her.
