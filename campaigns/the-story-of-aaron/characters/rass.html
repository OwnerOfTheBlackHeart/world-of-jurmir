Description

Rass is a 4' 5" tall, 67 pound lizardfolk covered in fur which changes color to match the environment. She has large, yellow eyes and a tail that is 1' 4". Her muzzle is shorter than would be expected for her size and her teeth point in different directions.

Unlike her people, Rass prefers to wear armor when she can. When not in armor, Rass doesn't even bother to wear a loin cloth, except in cold weather.

Combat

Rass prefers to not attack an enemy unless she has the advantage. When she can she will use her ability to hide and move quietly to catch lone enemies and eleminate them before they can be a problem. When fighting with allies, she will engage enemies from a distance, only entering into the melee when she can get around an enemy and strike at their weak points.

History and Motivation

Rass was the unfortunate creation of a gnoll raid that left survivors. She lived among poison dusk lizardfolk until the gnolls came back and killed the rest of her clan. Since then she has been walking among the mammal races.

As a rather monsterious half-mammal, Rass has had trouble finding a place in the society of mammals and seeks to find a tribe that will let her in, whatever they may be.

During one of her first meetings with Aaron, Aaron began sexually training her to be his pet. So far, she has been receptive of the training.
