Description

Bhintel is 5' tall and 122 lbs with long pink hair, fiery red eyes, skin as black as night, and soft features. Bhintel wears a pale brown robes with several pockets and a backpack. When travelling about, he wears a chain shirt over top of the robes.

Combat

In combat, Bhintel does his best to maintain range, using his eldritch blast when he can and his crossbow when he can't. When forced to engage in melee, he prefers to focus his eldritch blast through his spear.

Bhintel came to the surface with the scouting party of House Duskryn in Arx with the intent of scouting for good locations to gather slaves. He will serve whoever is his acting mistress/master, but will continue to feed information to House Duskryn and try to advance drow operations on the surface.
