Description

Chione is a 5' 1" female Tiefling with tanned skin, big grey and brown eyes, and curly, black hair. She is 125 lbs with a mildly muscular build. She prefers darker colors, especially reds. She has a large scar just in front of her left ear. She has two 3 inch, black horns that protrude from just under her hairline and a whip-like tail.

Chione is pregnent with Aaron's child.

Combat

In combat Chione prefers to engage in melees, using spells to enhance her hammer to turn her foes into paste. If she is caught with need of ranged combat, she will do so.
When she has a round or two to prep, she will use bloody blade.

History and Motivation

Chione was born in the town of Ashborne, left her parents at the age of 10, and learned how to be a duskblade from experience and a dusty old tome she found in the local library.

Chione is motivated primarily by acquiring the power to get out of the life of petty jobs that has been sustaining her so far. She is interested in expanding her knowledge of magic, skill in combat, and political power.
