Durn's Inquisition

Summary

The Cult of Durn sees the number of events going on as a sign of heresy. In response to the mass of heresy they begin an inquisition, but this inquisition will be seen by many as an attack on business and power.

[ ["Location", "Lyrna"], ["Persons of Note", "Zita, Speaker of Durn, Yom Ki-Nam"], ["Enemies", "Primarily paladins and inquisitors"] ]

The Harpy sees this as a threat to the flow of money and the Speaker of Durn sees this as an attack on her power.
