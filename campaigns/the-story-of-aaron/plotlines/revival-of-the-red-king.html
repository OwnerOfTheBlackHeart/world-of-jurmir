Revival of the Red King

Summary

The Cult of the Red King has hidden beneath the surface of society for hundreds of years, but now they are rising. They have been kidnapping, indoctronating, and ritualistically killing, doing their best to hide from the eyes of human society. It seems those that serve the Red King are looking to build up an army.
