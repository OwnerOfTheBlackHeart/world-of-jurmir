Huntsing Down the Cult 1

Intro

Hunts on Stone found a mysterious book when she was fighting the Cult of the Red King in the sunken castle while Chione was waiting to bear her first children. The book is bound in relatively normal black leather, but is written in a red ink and is written in voidspeak.

The Notable Sections of the Book

Brothers and sisters, I have to tell you that we have been great. Our numbers have swelled from 4 to 12 in but a few weeks and we even have some that look like they might be ready soon to visit the King. We have yet to find how to visit him from here, but we will keep looking.

Sister Phylo

Sister, we are doing well here too. Seablood is large enough for us to easily hide and has enough dark alleys that we have been able to find good targets for a glorious meeting with the King. Our prior lady has decided that we will do well without her presence and has gone off to show others of their duty to the world to come, so we are seeking to find someone we can present to the King.

Brother Theopompus

Brother, that is great to hear. I do fear, however, that someone has noticed our presence. I have been forming a basic defensive force, but I do not know how long we will be able to hold out before we are forced to move. I have several potential inducties that I would like to bring with us to the new world, but their chance for such a glorious mission is growing slimmer by the day.

Sister Phylo

Sister, we weep for your plight. We have found a target. She is no human, but she should easily fall to knee before the King. I see potential in her ability to guide us to a greater fate. We will have her soon.

Brother Theopompus

Brother, we have made it to our new home. We had to leave one of the recruits behind, but he was not ready by the time we needed to move. The other three have been brought into the King's vision and are ready to serve his will. We did find a castle just north of Ashborne, so we will camp out there.

Sister Phylo

Sister, she submitted before the King. Now we await to see what glorious servant she will bring us. We are also planning for something even more glorious. She has already taken to hearing the King and guides us down a path that he will surely smile upon. She will take us closer to the new world, step by step.

Brother Theopompus

General Outline

  1. Hunts brings the book found in the abandoned castle to Aaron. Erik translates the letters and hands the information off to Aaron.
  2. Aaron and party set off to head to Mordain.
  3. Party arrives in Mordain and restocks. They then head out to go to Seablood.
  4. Party arrives in Seablood and begins tracking down the Cult of the Red King.
  5. Party is approached by the father of one of the kidnapped daughters. He offers a reward for the return of his daughter.
    • Aaron will be approached by an Abductor and, if he is willing, brought before the Lady of the Cult.
    • The Lady will offer Aaron a human warrior in exchange for just leaving (Luta, not pregnant).
  6. Party finds clues leading to the location of the Cult.
  7. Party enters where the Cult is hiding out. They also find Luta Dagdottir in a cage.
    • Another cage sits nearby that has another young woman within it (the person that the party has been hired to save).
    • Luta is found without her equipment, but mentions that she is capable of fighting. Her equipment is within an armory/storage area.
    • Both Luta and the target are pregnant (1 month). Luta is showing no signs, but the target has small horns growing out of her skull. Both are monstrosities of the Red King.
  8. The party fights through the dungeon and kills off the Cult. Gain loot.
