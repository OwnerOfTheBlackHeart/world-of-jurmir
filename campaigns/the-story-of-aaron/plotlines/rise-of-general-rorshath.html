Rise of General Rorshath

Summary

Hobgoblins have begun increasing in size, capturing slaves and forcing them to build great fortifications and farm great swaths of land. They have even begun to call their territory a nation: The Empire of 5 Generals. Leading it is Grand General Rorshath, with his council of Generals Pevron, Gardrak, Zodak, and Fulem.
