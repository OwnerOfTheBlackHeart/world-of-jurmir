Description

This humanoid is slender and shorter than a human. Its has dark brown skin, pointed ears, and black hair. It is covered in thick hides and carries a large, heavy looking stick and a small bow.

Combat

Wild elves of the steppes are used to not generally having stealth as an option and are familar with fighting on the defensive. If they can get an ambush off, they will, but they are also just as likely to attempt to barrage the enemy with their shortbows and charge in with their greatclubs.

When not fighting for the lives of themselves and their tribes, most steppes elves will retreat when a battle is going poorly. If they feel it is important, they may return when their opponents are distracted and attack from the rear. Stepes elves are not affraid to drawing in wild animals or gnolls against enemies they are having trouble with.
