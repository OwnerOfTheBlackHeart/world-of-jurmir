Description

Every red page is different, but the most common are 5' 5" tall men clad in a gambeson with a metal helmet. Red pages are the body guards of red ladies and have very little will of their own, obeying commands without any resistance or comment. Without a red lady to command them, red pages travel about and attack anything in their way.

Red pages are created by red ladies from their flock, and red pages are loyal only to the red lady that created them. A red lady can only have one page at a time and a red page who has lost their red lady wonders with only vengeful blood lust.

Combat

Red pages always put themselves in front of their red lady, doing everything in their power to prevent harm to her. If fighting on their own, they will charge at any creature they see, raging before striking wildly with their bastard sword.
