Description

Kept out of public sight, this armor wall of human and steel serves to protect the halls of the cult. However, those who get trapped in the hidden arenas or who try to attack the cult often find these protectors standing against them.

Combat

The cult guard will try to get into melee range, if it can, and manually smash whatever enemy is closest. If that isn't an option, guards can swap to using their bows and barrage enemies from affar as other members of the cult deal with the problems up close.
