Constrict (Ex): Dhako deals automatic tail slap damage (including fire damage) with a successful grapple check.

Description

Dhako Smogfang is the monstrosity given birth to by Jennlinn Miirdhek when she became a red lady. His upper body appears as a strong human covered in crimson scales from which fire erupts with a handsome face and a mane of fire running down his back. Below the waste, Dhako sits upon a long, serpentine tail that is covered in the same crimson scales with vicious spines from which fire erupts on the top of the tail.

Combat

While Dhako is intelligent and charismatic, he prefers to charge into combat and force his enemies into melee so that he can pull them into his tail and crush the life out of them. Until he has his enemies held in place, he will aggressively attack with his greataxe, or his claws if he is disarmed. Should a battle appear to go the wrong way, Dhako will use dimension door to escape and possibly polymorph to hide in plain sight, stalking his opponents until he is ready to finish them off.
