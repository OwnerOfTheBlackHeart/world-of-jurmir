Description

A human dressed in splint armor adorned with images of eyes recites a dark prayer and looks forward with cruelty and hate in his eyes. He draws a morningstar as his prayer ends.

Combat

The cult priest serves to lead his fellow cultists into combat, using his spells to turn simple cultists into serious threats. He isn't affraid of joining the melee himself, using the same spells to turn him into a monster of the armies of the Red King.
