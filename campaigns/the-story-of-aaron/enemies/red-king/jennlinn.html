Description

Jennlinn was once an ugly dwarfess who was captured by the Cult of the Red King in Seablood. After several days of "conditioning," she was taken down to the Void to be presented to the Red King. She was, at first, resistant to the demands of the Red King until he made her an offer. He would make her beautiful, so beautiful that if she didn't like the way she looked, she could change she. In exchange, she would bear him a child and serve him for the rest of her life. Jennlinn accepted almost without hesitation.

Jennlinn would give birth to Dhako Smogfand, a draconic, alu-demon salamander. She bares the same warmth (at will) as her child as well as a similarly scaly hide.

Combat

Jennlinn is not a warrior. She flees combat whenever she can. If forced to fight, she will first try to turn her enemies against each other. However, if that doesn't appear to be an option, she will try to use her magic to defend herself from a distance while sending minions out to fight the enemy and possibly calling on Dhako if the situation seems dire enough. Jennlinn is not above changing her appearance to confuse her enemies or disguise herself as a damsel in distress.
