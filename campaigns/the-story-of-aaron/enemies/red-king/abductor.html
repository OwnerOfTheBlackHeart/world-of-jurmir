Description

Hiding in plain sight as beautiful men and women, these agents of the cult serve a dual purpose: To recruit the vulnerable and to abduct those needed for the rituals of the Cult of the Red King. They are usually disguised when "on the job" within a settlement or out gathering information on possible recruits or targets.

Combat

Abductors generally enter combat with the intent to disable their opponents or to protect their recruits or cultmates. If they can they will setup ambushes or flanking attacks to allow them to utalize their sneak attack ability, but unless a wall of meat and steal is distracting their opponent, they will try to keep a distance and use their hand crossbows to as great an effect as they can.
