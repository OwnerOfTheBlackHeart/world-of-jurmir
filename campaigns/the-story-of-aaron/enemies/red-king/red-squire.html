Description

Every red squire is different, but the most common are 5' 5" tall men clad in a gambeson with a metal helmet. Red squires are the body guards of red princesses and have very little will of their own, obeying commands without any resistance or comment. Without a red princess to command them, red squires travel about and attack anything in their way.

Red squires are created by red princesses from their flock, and red squires are loyal only to the red princess that created them. A red princess can only have one squire at a time and a red squire who has lost their red princess wonders with only vengeful blood lust.

Combat

Red squires always put themselves in front of their red princess, doing everything in their power to prevent harm to her. If fighting on their own, they will charge at any creature they see, raging before striking wildly with their bastard sword. If fighting with other creatures, a red squire will utilize their guisarme if it is tactically more useful than their bastard sword.
