3.5/Pathfinder Homebrew Feats

Living in Armor [Combat]

Prerequisites

Bab +3, Proficient in medium armor

Benefit

The character reduces the armor check penalty, and increases the maximum dex, of the armor they are wearing by 1. This value increases by 1 for every extra attack the character gains from their base attack bonus, to a max of 3.

Special

A fighter may select Living in Armor as one of his fighter bonus feats.

Adjusted to Armor [Combat]

Prerequisites

Fighter level 3, Proficient in medium armor

Benefit

The character no longer suffers speed penalties for using medium armor.

Special

A fighter may select Adjusted to Armor as one of his fighter bonus feats.

Armor Mountain [Combat]

Prerequisites

Fighter level 7, Proficient in heavy armor, Adjusted to Armor

Benefit

The character no longer suffers speed penalties for using heavy armor. The character still can only run at 3 times their speed.

Special

A fighter may select Armor Mountain as one of his fighter bonus feats.

Weapon Mastery [Combat]

Prerequisites

Fighter level 1, Weapon focus

Benefit

Choose a weapon that the character is proficient with. Any feats that require a single type of weapon be chosen (weapon focus, weapon specialization, etc.) apply to the weapon chosen with this feat as well.

Special

This feat may be taken multiple times. Each time this feat is taken, choose a different type of weapon. A fighter may select Weapon Mastery as one of his fighter bonus feats.

Expanded Spell List [Magic]

Prerequisites

Ability to cast 1st level spells

Benefit

Select a spell casting class you have access to 1st level spell slots in (base class), then choose a different spell casting class to add spells from (target class). You may add 1 spell from the target class's spell list to the base class's spell list of each spell level you have access to. When you gain access to a new spell level, add 1 spell from the target class's spell list to the base class's spell list.

This feat does not add spells learned or give free spells to classes with a spellbook. The spells added to the base class's spell list must be learned as if spells normally available to that class.

Special

This feat may be taken multiple times. If so, a different target class must be chosen.

This feat may be taken as a wizard bonus feat.
