Class Features

Control (Su):

A bright one can establish complete control over a creature, given enough time. Before doing so, a bright one must purchase 100gp of ground gemstones per hit die of the target creature(s). The ritual takes 1 hour to complete, after which all creatures affected by it are treated as if they are under a constant effect equivalent to the dominate class feature, except that if they succeed on their save versus a command they are immune to being commanded for 6 hours.

A bright one can only control creatures they can use the dominate class feature on. Creatures that aren't friendly (see the diplomacy skill) with the bright one will rarely submit to a control ritual. The control ritual does not work on unwilling creatures.

Creatures that have undergone a control ritual typically lose their coloring, appearing as pale with blonde to white hair and sun colored or white eyes.

Dominate (Su):

A bright one can attempt to dominate a creature, taking control of it for a short time. In order to dominate a creature a bright one must first make a touch attack. If the attack hits, the victim makes a will save. If the victim passes, they are stunned for one turn and immune to domination for 24 hours. If they fail, they are under the control of the bright one for 1 minute. During this time they consider the bright one to be their ally and the bright one can use a move action to command the target, as per the suggestion spell with a DC equal to the bright one's dominate DC. Dominate can only affect humanoid creatures.

The save DC for dominate is 10 + 1/2 spellcasting class levels + charisma modifier. Dominate can be used a number of times per day equal to the bright one's class level plus their charisma modifier.

When the control effects of dominate on a creature end, the bright one can attempt a diplomacy check to change the attitude of the target as if it were unfriendly.

As a bright one grows in power, so does the range of creatures they can dominate and how they can dominate them. At level 2 they gain the ability to use dominate on monstrous humanoids. At level 4 they gain the ability to use dominate on animals. At level 5 they can affect humanoids with the effects of dominate person. At level 6 they can affect monstrous humanoids with the effects of dominate person and gain the ability to dominate magical beasts. At level 7 they can inflict the target with quest. At level 8 they gain the ability to use the dominate class feature on any creature. At level 9 they can affect all targets with the effects of dominate person.
