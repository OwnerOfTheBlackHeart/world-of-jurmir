Class Features

Control (Su):

A bright one can establish complete control over a creature, given enough time. Before doing so, a bright one must purchase 100gp of ground gemstones per hit die of the target creature(s). The ritual takes 1 hour to complete, after which all creatures affected by it are treated as if they are under a constant effect equivalent to the Glowing Palm class feature, except that if they succeed on their save versus a command they are immune to being commanded for 6 hours.

A bright one can only control creatures they can use the Glowing Palm class feature on. Creatures that aren't friendly (see the diplomacy skill) with the bright one will rarely submit to a control ritual. The control ritual does not work on unwilling creatures.

Creatures that have undergone a control ritual typically lose their coloring, appearing as pale with blonde to white hair and sun colored or white eyes.
