Half-Races Catalog

Powerful Build (Ex)

The physical stature of the half-breed lets them function in many ways as if they were one size category larger. Whenever a half-breed is subject to a size modifier or special size modifier for an opposed check (such as during grapple checks, bull rush attempts, and trip attempts), the half-breed is treated as one size larger if doing so is advantageous to him. A half-breed is also considered to be one size larger when determining whether a creature’s special attacks based on size (such as improved grab or swallow whole) can affect him. A half-breed can use weapons designed for a creature one size larger without penalty. However, his space and reach remain those of a creature of his actual size. The benefits of this racial trait stack with the effects of powers, abilities, and spells that change the subject’s size category.

Poison Dusk Gnoll - Poison Dusk Lizardfolk and Gnoll

  • Ability Score Bonuses: Strength +2, Dexterity +1, Constitution +2, Intelligence -1, Charisma -2
  • Size/Type: Small Humanoid (Reptilian, Gnoll)
  • Speed: 30 feet (6 squares)
  • Darkvision/Low Light Vision: Darkvision 45ft
  • Skills: +2 on Balance, Jump, and Swim checks
  • Automatic Languages: Draconic, Common. Bonus Languages: Halfling, Sylvan.
  • Favored Class: Ranger
  • Natural Armor: +2
  • Natural Weapons: Bite (1d3)
  • Special Qualities: Poison use, chameleon skin
  • Level Adjustment: +1

Poison Use: Due to their long tradition of working with venoms, poison dusks are never at risk when applying poisons to weapons. Common poisons include black adder venom and blue whinnis.

Chameleon Skin (Ex): Poison dusks can adjust the colors of their scales to blend in with their surroundings. When it is not wearing armor, robes or other covering clothing, a poison dusk lizardfolk gains a +5 racial bonus on Hide checks.

Droll - Drow and Gnoll

  • Ability Score Bonuses: Strength +2, Dexterity +1
  • Size/Type: Medium Humanoid (Gnoll, Elf)
  • Speed: 30 feet (6 squares)
  • Darkvision/Low Light Vision: Darkvision 90ft
  • Skills: +1 bonus on Listen, Search, and Spot checks
  • Saves: +1 racial bonus on Will saves against spells and spell-like abilities
  • Automatic Languages: Common, elven or gnoll Bonus Languages: Elven, gnoll, draconic, goblin, orc, sylvan
  • Favored Class: Cleric (female) or Wizard (male) or Ranger
  • Natural Armor: +1*
  • Special Qualities: Light blindness
  • Level Adjustment: +1

Weapon Proficiency*: A droll is automatically proficient with the hand crossbow, the rapier, and the short sword. A droll gets either these weapon proficiencies or +1 to natural armor.

Magic in the Blood: A droll can either have spell resistance equal to 9 + class levels or two of the following the following spell-like abilities once per day: dancing lights, darkness, faerie fire. Caster level equals the drow’s class levels.
