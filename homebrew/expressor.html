Class Features

Birth Outsider (Sp):

The expressor gains the ability to perform a ritual that impregnates a victim with an outsider. The ritual requires that the victim be willing or helpless and have the ability to normally give birth. The expressor selects an outsider with no more adjusted hit dice (see last paragraph) than the number of expressor levels they have (counting base class levels), then purchases 100gp worth of materials per hit die of the target outsider, which are then used up during the ritual. The ritual takes 24 hours of work, during which the expressor and the victim commune with the appropriate planar forces. At the end of the 24 hours, the victim is impregnated.

The pregnancy is a 3 month pregnancy following the rules outlined in the pregnancy rules. If the outsider is 2, or more, size categories smaller than the mother, it is born full sized. Otherwise, the outsider is born 2 size categories smaller than the mother and grows to its normal size over a 1 week growth period.

When the outsider created by birth outsider is born, the expressor may choose, so long as they are on the same plane as the outsider, to command it via rebuke outsider. The new-born outsider gets no save against this effect. If the outsider is not commanded, it will be indifferent to its mother and friendly to its creator (as per diplomacy). The outsider normally goes off on its own after birth if not commanded. An outsider that cannot normally be commanded by rebuke/command outsider will instead be indifferent to its creator, as per the diplomacy skill.

The expressor can use birth outsider on themself. If the expressor lacks a womb and performs birth outsider on themself, they are turned into one of the following sexes (their choice): Female, masculine hermaphrodite or feminine hermaphrodite.

An outsider's adjusted hit dice is equal to its challenge rating + 2 or its hit dice, whichever is higher.

Rebuke/Command Outsider:

If an expressor has the ability to rebuke/command undead, they gain the ability to rebuke/command outsiders. An expressor can only command outsiders that are within 1 step on the alignment scale. An outsider's hit dice for the sake of rebuke/command outsider is equal to its challenge rating + 2 or its hit dice, whichever is higher.

Smite Good (Su):

Once per day the expressor can make a normal melee attack to deal extra damage equal to their total HD (maximum of +20) against a good foe.

Ability Score Boost:

At 1st level, an expressor gains +2 to their charisma score.

At 3rd level, an expressor gains +4 to their dexterity score.

At 5rd level, an expressor gains +4 to their strength score.

At 7rd level, an expressor gains +2 to their constitution score.

At 9rd level, an expressor gains +4 to their intelligence score.

Draining Kiss (Su):

Starting at 2nd level, an expressor can sap the vitality of a target simply by kissing them. If the target is not willing to be kissed, the expressor must start a grapple, which provokes an attack of opportunity (unless the expressor possesses the Improved Grapple feat). On a success, draining kiss deals 1d6 damage plus the casting stat modifier of the base class. The expressor then heals the amount of damage dealt, up to the expressor's maximum hit points.

The damage of draining kiss increases to 2d6 at 5th level, 3d6 at 8th level, and 4d6 at 10th level.

Starting at 5th level, the expressor can apply the effects of the suggestion spell with a DC of 10 + half the expressor's caster level + the expressor's casting stat. This is in addition to any other effects of draining kiss.

Starting at 10th level, the expressor can choose to bestow a negative level in place of the damage of draining kiss. The fortitude save DC for removing the negative level is equal to DC of 10 + half the expressor's caster level + the expressor's casting stat.

Natural Attack:

At 2nd level, an expressor gains 2 claw natural attacks. They deal damage equal to the damage given by the half-fiend damage table in the half-fiend template. If the expressor already possesses claw attacks, use the damage values in the table or that the expressor's existing claw attack damage, whichever is greater.

At 5th level, an expressor gains a bite natural attacks. They deal damage equal to the damage given by the half-fiend damage table in the half-fiend template. If the expressor already possesses a bite attack, use the damage values in the table or that the expressor's existing bite attack damage, whichever is greater.

Natural Armor:

At 2nd level, an expressor gains +1 natural armor. If the expressor already has natural armor, increase her natural armor by +1.

Disguise Self (Sp):

At 3rd level, an expressor gains the ability to cast Disguise Self as an at will spell-like ability. The casting level is equal to the expressor’s caster level and the casting ability score is the same as the base classes casting ability score.

Energy Resistance (Su):

At 3rd level, an expressor gains energy resistance (fire 10). At 4th, she gains energy resistance (electricity 10). At 6th, she gains energy resistance (cold 10). At 7th level, she gains immunity to poison (as per the venom immunity druid class feature).

Wings:

At 3rd level, an expressor gains a pair of bat wings. Unless the expressor already has a better fly speed, the expressor can fly at their base land speed (average maneuverability).

False Alignment (Su):

At 4th level, an expressor gains the ability to disguise their alignment on the good vs evil spectrum as being good. The DC to see their true alignment is 10 plus half their expressor caster level plus the modifier for the expressor’s casting ability score.

At 8th level, an expressor gains the ability to use False Alignment to disguise themself as neutral on the good vs evil alignment spectrum.

Spell-Like Abilities:

Starting at 5th level, an expressor gains spell-like abilities equal to that of a half-fiend with a number of hit dice equal to the expressor's total hit dice.

Damage Reduction:

At 5th level, an expressor gains DR 5/magic.

Alter Self (Sp):

At 7th level, an expressor gains the ability to cast Alter Self as an at will spell-like ability. The casting level is equal to the expressor’s caster level and the casting ability score is the same as the base classes casting ability score.

Spell Resistance:

At 9th level, an expressor gains spell resistance equal to their total hit dice + 10 (maximum 35).

Half-Fiend:

At 10th level, the expressor becomes a half-fiend. The expressor becomes an outsider (native) with the skills of the expressor as the racial class skills, in addition to existing racial class skills. Do not recalculate Hit Dice, base attack bonus, or saves. The expressor gains energy resistance acid 10 and improves their damage resistance to DR 10/magic. The expressor's natural weapons are treated as magic weapons for the purpose of overcoming damage reduction. The expressor does not take any level adjustment for this class feature.

For all effects that target half-fiends, the expressor is considered a half-fiend.
