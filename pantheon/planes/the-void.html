The Void, Prison of the Red King

The Void is an empty plane that serves as the prison of the Red King. Throughout the Void itself float the many chunks of the Red King, which are slowly pulling themselves together, aided by the souls who have given themselves to the Red King.

Due to the fact that the Void is basically a planar empty space, great magical locks within the Underdark are used to keep the void from pulling materials from other planes into it, as well as to keep its own contents from escaping.
