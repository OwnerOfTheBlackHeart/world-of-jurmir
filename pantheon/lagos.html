Lagos of Chronos, God of Time

Alignment: Lawful Neutral

Domains: Time, Transformation, Life, Planning (CWar p115)

Portfolio: Time, Growth, Precision, Knowledge, Life, Sun

Symbol: Cresent moon behind an Hour Glass

Favored Weapon: Wakizashi

Lagos was originally a Duskblade (and Eldrich Knight) Man that focused on timing and precision to an obsessive degree. He stood 6 feet tall weighed 183lbs. Had olive colored skin and long matte black dreds. He and a small party he traveled with stumbled upon the remains of the old god Chronos that had crafted time. The aberrations that formed around the god killed most of the group but Lagos had learned the movements and timing of the aberration that he killed many without incurring a single injury.

Something stirred in the remains of the dead god Chronos as if seeing it from the past. Lagos gained immense power slaying a seemingly endless horde. His habit of looking for timing had grown to seeing the time itself. Slowing down and he sped up. At some points time even moved backwards for him. By the time he finished off the enemies he was a god.

Upon realizing he was the one controlling time he took control of the calendar and sun to form a more routine and structured. Based it off the needs of mankind. At first he did it to make his life easier to plan and more convenient.
