Hipha, Goddess of Technology and Knowledge

Hipha is a relatively new god made during the revolution of digital technology. Originally intended to serve as a companion to the Clockwork God, Zigies, Hipha developed great power within her own right as access to communication networks and a forming intelligence slowly pushed her to preside over technology.

Most worshipers of Hipha work with technology themselves, wether they be shipsmiths, IT people, mechanics, and more. Those that don't are almost always educators of some form, weather they be the professor-priests of the many school temples of Hipha or software trainers teaching people how to use computers.
