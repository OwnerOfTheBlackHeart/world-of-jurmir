Zigies, the Clockwork God

Picture of Zigies

Alternate Names: The Unfinished God, The Perfect Weapon, The Finisher of Projects

Alignment: Lawful Neutral

Domains: Craft (Spell Compendium pg. 271), Earth, Artifice (Eberron Campaign Setting pg. 104), Metal (Spell Compendium pg. 276)

Portfolio: Warforged, Crafting, Technology, War

Symbol: A broken cog

Favored Weapon: Crossbow or Ray spells

Colors: Silver and copper

Zigies was made by the old god Steele as a project to create an artificial god which the Red King could not eat. However, when Steele was torn in half in his fight against the Red King, he used his final moments to finish his artificial god, a being to not only replace himself, but to also kill the Red King. Steele's dying breath carried a curse against the Red King, but not what was needed to bring Zigies to life.

1,000 years later, the dwarven wizard Tyadin used all of his might to bring Zigies to life, but being given life has left Zigies desperately underpowered. He has the ability to access everything his maker had the power of and more but not to as great an extent. As thanks for Tyadin giving his life to awaken it, Zigies constructed the first of the Warforges and shared the knowledge with Tyadins school, and while they made their own, in the end all warforges belong to Zigies.

In the current times, Zigies is one of the most common celestial gods to interact with mortals, only seen less then the likes of Feph, Hrefna and Lindo. Zigies avatar is that of a six winged, clockwork angel with its left hand replaced with a blade made from energy. The true form of Zigies is a clockwork dragon based off of the lover and rival of his creator, Steele.

Dwarven Worship

Zigies is as prevalent in dwarven worship as Durn and is a corner stone of typical dwarven society, usually being the patron deity of crafting oriented castes. All of Zigies dwarven priests, but not all of its clerics and favored souls, are male by tradition, even though the Clockwork God is sexless and still struggles with understanding the difference between male and female.

All the dwarven clergy of Zigies take the clan name of Forgeborn upon being anointed as a child of the Unfinished God.
