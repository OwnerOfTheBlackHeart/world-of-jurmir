The Red King, Eater of Gods and Maker of Man

Alignment: Chaotic Evil

Domains: Evil, Stength, Death, Destruction

Portfolio: Humans, Slaughter, Power, Hunger

Symbol: Teeth surrounding an eye

The Red King is the old god who devoured the other old gods and whose jealousy gave birth to the humans and the current gods. He waits within the Void for the seals that bind him in place to weaken enough for him to escape.

The Red King seeks the destruction of all the gods and the slaughter of everything that isn't human. He also seeks control of the human race.

The Red King looks like a disfigured, crimson red horned god with many eyes and one massive eye on his chest. Some depictions show him as a knight clad in crimson full plate with a massive, predatory maw filled with large, serrated teeth coming from underneath the helmet.

The Red King's name as an old god was Churnobog. The Red King uses crimson red.
