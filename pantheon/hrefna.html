Hrefna Aridottir, Goddess of the Hunt

Picture of Hrefna

Alignment: Neutral Good

Domains: Hunt, Animal, Life, Plant

Portfolio: Hunting, Domesticated Animals, Spring, Fertility, The Wild

Symbol: A drawn bow or a pointer (the breed of dog)

Colors: Green and Brown

Favored Weapon: Bow

Hrefna Aridottir was once a common huntswoman who lived in the isolated Orutol Forest, which would become the grasslands of Lyrna some time later. She was wise and kind and a master of domesticating wild animals and an even better hunter. Over her mortal lifespan she would keep her small village alive through many springs and winters with her ability to bring home pelts and meat. She ascended to godhood due to Lindo's affections.

Hrefna has born a single child, Feph, with Lindo before he became a lich, which Hrefna was against. She raised both Lindo and Feph.

Hrefna is commonly worshipped by hunters, breeders, druids, and farmers, but those seeking to bear child will also pray to Hrefna. Her shrines resemble hunting blinds and other forms of camouflage which are placed in natural areas where they can be hunted from. Rituals to Hrefna often involve hunting or food animal sacrifices (like cattle or chickens).

Hrefna is portrayed as an attractive Lyrnin woman with curly black hair and 3 pointers. The names of the pointers are Thord, Antheia, and Iamus.
