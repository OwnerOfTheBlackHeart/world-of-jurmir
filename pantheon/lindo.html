Lindo, Sailor of the River Cain

Picture of Lindo

Alignment: Chaotic Good

Domains: Healing, Trickery, Repose(DnD 215), Knowledge

Portfolio: Music, Poetry, Creativity, Death, Knowledge, Surfing, Sailing

Symbol: A Stag skull

Favored Weapon: Scythe

Colors: Red and black

Lindo the Cervitaur Lich god of Death and Music. The first child of a god. That god being Zoltan. Lindo was initially the music god but with the need for souls to be guided on, Lindo took on the responsibility of death as well. Lindo stands 8ft tall and has the fleshless head of a deer with a 10 point rack. His favored weapon is the accordion (or if you must make my job harder I am fond of the Scythe). Lindo is a god who loves to entertain and play games. He often dresses in red plaids and with baubles hanging from his body. Lindo has been known to create various undead for personal amusement. The Revenant is his personal favorite (mostly from the tasty drama).

Archons of Lindo are invariably bards of knowledge (and liches). Beings who record and listen to the lives of the souls that pass through. If a soul is particularly good or evil, Lindo will send them to Horgverold or Hell respectively. Most souls, however, will spend some time in Dysvegr, awaiting the gods to claim them. Lindo makes sure to catalog every soul that rides his boat to Dysvegr. Lindo has a list of the clerics and the gods they belong to of which he express ships their souls to their patron deities (warlocks get sent to whoever they belonged to too). Lindo can be appealed to directly by the later who if they can properly impress the Dead God (which will take a lot), they can appeal to serve as his Archon or even go back as an undead.

Lindo runs the boat of the river Cain that carries souls to the afterlife. Lindo’s boat is magically controlled by his accordion (And it isn’t even limited by the river Cain). The river Cain ends in a whirlpool that is as strong as the strife of the life of the person trying to enter it. This whirlpool will allow the soul to reenter the materium. In order to be Reincarnated one must surf with Lindo to the center. He only takes the sickest waves.

Lindo has many children, but the most well known is his first child Feph, who he had with the human turned god Hrefna.
