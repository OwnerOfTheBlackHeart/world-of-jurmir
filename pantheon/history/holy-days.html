The Days and Their Celebrations

Cyclefeast

The primary holy day of Lagos. A feast on the first day of the year.

Demonbind

One of the two primary holy days of Sithrak. This is sad day where the "judges" focus on self-reflection, self-judgement, and remembrance of their own sins.

Burningday

One of the two primary holy days of Sithrak. This is a happy day where the "sinners" focus on diving as deep into sin as they can get within a single day. A common tradition is to convince people to be sat upon a chair and lit aflame.

The First Hunt

The primary holy day of Hrefna. It is the first hunt of sprint, serving as a right of passage for some worshippers of Hrefna. Depending on how long the hunters take, the feast may be on the same day or the day after.

The Festival of Voices

The primary holy day of Durn. It is celebrated with a festival where the ruler(s) hold a large, open court that is easily available to their subjects. The non-ruling nobility is responsible for providing food and festivities.

Tyrantfall

Celebrates the defeat of the Red King. Celebrated with a festival and feast that ends with the burning of an effigy of the Red King.
