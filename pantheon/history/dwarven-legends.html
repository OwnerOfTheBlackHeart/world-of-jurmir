Dwarven Legends

Wolgin the Anti-Beard

Aye it be a legend from the days of Steele himself. Of a dwarf with the strength of the mountains and the craftsmanship that could make adamantine coppers in comparison Legend states that he will guide the dwarves under one banner But Wolgin's face doesn't grow a beard. Instead his hair grows up as if to consume the mountain above or even the skies.
