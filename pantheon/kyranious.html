Kyranious, First of the Dragons

Picture of kyranious

Alignment: Neutral

Domains: Strength, Dragon (SC 273), Greed (SC 275), Magic

Portfolio: Dragons, Kobolds, Moral Extremes, Sorcerers

Symbol: The skull of a dragon

Colors: Red and Gold

Kyranious, First of the Dragons and Mother of the Mortal Dragons, is the last remaining old god (though some scholars debate that Zoltan counts as an old god). She is worshipped by both all types dragons, who she gave birth to the first of, and the kobolds, which she created from the scales of dragons. She is normally portrayed as a dragon large as a mountain with scales that appear white and ghostly, but in the right light show the colors of all dragons.

Kyranious created the gods Durn, Sithrak, and Kriotz after she survived an attack by the Red King.

Mortal Relations

Kyranious, and dragonkind in general, have a love/hate relationship with the dwarves. As master craftsmen and expert miners, the dwarves produce some of the most coveted items a dragon could want, but they also hunt dragons and kobolds and fight against Kyranious's creations for control over valuable ore veins. This relationship is further complicated by the fact that Kyranious was both a lover and a rival to Steele, the old god who crafted the dwarves in his image.

Starfinder

For the followers of Kyranious, not much has changed. However, the Mother of Dragons has had increasingly worse relations with Kriotz and Hipha over their insistence of magic and technology remaining mostly separate. Kyranious's stance on the matter is that magic and technology are both sources of power and wealth and thus both are worthy of dragonkin's hording and utilization.
